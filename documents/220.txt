                     Â  Message from the Director
                     
                     Welcome to the University of Memphis School of Accountancy where our goal is to provide
                        educational opportunities at both the graduate and undergraduate levels to prepare
                        our students for successful careers in business and education. Our curriculum is designed
                        so our graduates will be:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        knowledgeable in accounting and its functional support of organizations,
                        
                        technologically competent,
                        
                        critical thinkers and problem solvers,
                        
                        effective communicators and team members, and
                        
                        aware of ethical ramifications and global business implications of decisions.
