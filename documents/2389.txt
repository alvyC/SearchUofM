                     Undergraduates
                     
                     Thank you for your interest in the undergraduate programs in the Department of English.
                        We are one of the most popular major choices for University of Memphis students, so
                        you know you are in good company when you study English with us. Our program is diverse
                        and versatile, with six different concentrations and a variety of classes available.
                     
                     
                     For more information about the English undergraduate programs, contact the Undergraduate
                        Advisor, Dale Williams, Patterson 471, 901.678.4500, dmwllams@memphis.edu
                     
                     Concentrations
                     
                     English majors chose from one of the six following concentrations: African American Literature Creative Writing ESL (English as a Second Language) Language and Linguistics Literature Professional Writing
                     
                     Online Degree Programs
                     
                     Students can also earn an online BA in African American Literature and Professional
                        Writings. For more information about online degrees as well as other online course offerings,
                        visit the Online Degree Program page. NOTE: RODP classes (.R01, .R50, .R25, etc.) classes are not administered by the Department
                        of English. Speak with your advisor before signing up for these classes to make sure
                        they fit into your degree program.
                     
                     
                     Minor in English
                     Strengthen your degree with a second major or minor in English. To minor in English,
                     you must complete 12 hours of Upper Division English classes (Note: ENGL 3500 and
                     ENGL 3603 do not count toward the minor).
