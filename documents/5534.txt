                     The Blue Line & Tiger Football

                              Shuttles start 3 hours before kickoff (3:00pm)
                              
                              Load at the West side of the Central parking lot.
                              
                              Drop off at the Coca-Cola Bottling Co. on Hollywood.
                              
                              Pick up at the Coca-Cola Bottling Co. on Hollywood.
                              
                              Track the shuttles on our mobile app using the new TIGER Route at memphis.transloc.com .

                     Download the Blue Line mobile app here to track our shuttles!
                     
                     Routes
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              
                                 
                                 
                                    
                                    Blue Route (Main Campus)
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          Has 14 stops on the Main Campus
                                          
                                          From 7 am-5 pm M-F there are 2 buses on the route
                                          
                                          From 5 pm-11 pm M-Th there are 2 buses on the route
                                          
                                          Anyone can ride the bus for free, after 5 pm riders must show current UofM ID
                                          
                                          Follow the buses on mobile app memphis.transloc.com
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                    
                                    
                                    Gray Route (Park Avenue Campus)
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          Has 4 stops on the Main Campus and 5 on the Park Ave Campus
                                          
                                          From 7 am-5 pm M-F there are 2 buses on the route
                                          
                                          From 5 pm-11 pm M-Th there is 1 bus on the route
                                          
                                          Anyone can ride the bus for free, after 5 pm riders must show current UofM ID
                                          
                                          Follow the buses on mobile app memphis.transloc.com
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                    
                                    
                                    Green Express Route
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          Green Route will only makes stops north of the railroad tracks
                                          
                                          Green Route runs the opposite direction of the Blue and Gray Route
                                          
                                          Never gets caught by a train
                                          
                                          Track the Green Route on your app just like the other routes
                                          
                                          Follow the buses on mobile app memphis.transloc.com
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                    
                                    
                                    Purple Kroger Poplar Plaza Route
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          One bus dedicated to this route; runs continuously from the 4 stops located near Residence
                                             Halls.
                                          
                                          
                                          Route begins at the Central Parking Lot Stop #2 (near Carpenter Complex) at 2 pm.
                                             It will then travel the Visitor Information Center stop (near Centennial Place), and
                                             then to the Rawls Hall stop (includes Rawls, Smith, LLC), and finally to the Athletic
                                             Office Building stop on Normal Street (near South Hall) utilizing the existing Blue
                                             Line stops.
                                          
                                          
                                          LAST BUS leaves the Poplar Plaza at 4:45 pm; it is your responsibility to make this
                                             bus. If you miss this bus you must provide your own means of transportation back to
                                             campus.
                                          
                                          
                                          Follow the buses on mobile app memphis.transloc.com
