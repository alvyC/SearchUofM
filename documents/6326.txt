                     Center for Advancement of Youth Development
                     
                     We fill the gap for youth serving organizations by providing their staff with opportunities
                           to access high quality training, evaluation, and technical assistance without leaving
                           the community.
                     
                     CAYD Goals
                     
                     
                        
                        To provide professional development opportunities for youth servicing professionals.
                        
                        To disseminate best and promising practices of youth, family, and community interventions.
                        
                        To provide technical assistance for design and implementation of youth development
                           interventions.
                        
                        
                        To design empowerment evaluations and conduct rigorous intervention research for youth
                           development organizations.
