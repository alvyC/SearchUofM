                     University Holidays
                     
                     Administrative & Support Staff: The following dates will be observed as University holidays.
                     
                     
                     2016 Calendar Year
                     
                     
                        
                        January 1, 2016: New Year's Day
                        
                        January 18, 2016: Martin Luther King, Jr. Day
                        
                        May 30, 2016: Memorial Day
                        
                        July 4, 2016: Independence Day
                        
                        September 5, 2016: Labor Day
                        
                        November 24-25, 2016: Thanksgiving Day & One Administrative Closing Day
                        
                        December 23-30, 2016*: Christmas Day & Five Administrative Closing Days
                        
                     
                     
                     *New Year's Day for 2017 will be observed Monday, January 2.
                     
                     2017 Calendar Year
                     
                     
                        
                        January 2, 2017: New Year's Day (observed)
                        
                        January 16, 2017: Martin Luther King, Jr. Day
                        
                        May 29, 2017: Memorial Day
                        
                        July 3-4, 2017: Administrative Holiday & Independence Day
                        
                        September 4, 2017: Labor Day
                        
                        November 23-24, 2017: Thanksgiving Day & One Administrative Closing Day
                        
                        December 25-29, 2017*: Christmas Day & Four Administrative Closing Days
                        
                     
                     
                     *New Year's Day for 2018 will be observed Monday, January 1.
                     
                     2018 Calendar Year
                     
                     
                        
                        January 1, 2018: New Year's Day
                        
                        January 15, 2018: Martin Luther King, Jr. Day
                        
                        May 28, 2018: Memorial Day
                        
                        July 4, 2018: Independence Day
                        
                        September 3, 2018: Labor Day
                        
                        November 22-23, 2018: Thanksgiving Day & One Administrative Closing Day
                        
                        December 24-31, 2018*: Christmas Day & Five Administrative Closing Days
                        
                     
                     
                     *New Year's Day for 2019 will be observed Tuesday, January 1, 2019.
