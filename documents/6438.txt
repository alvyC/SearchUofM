                     Meet the Cadre & Staff
                     
                     LTC LAURA C. UPDEGRAFF - Professor of Military Science MS IV Instructor Military History Instructor  
                        
                        LTC Laura Updegraff is the Department Chair, Professor of Military Science and Battalion
                              Commander for the Tiger Battalion, at the University of Memphis. She is a 1998 graduate
                              of The University of South Carolina, where she received her commission as an Adjutant
                              General Corps Officer.  She has a Masters of Arts Degree in Business Administration
                              from Saint Martin's University, Olympia, Washington and a Master of Science Degree
                              in Counseling and Leader Development from Long Island University, Long Island, New
                              York. Lieutenant Colonel Updegraff has served in multiple human resource and command
                              positions, including an Officer and Enlisted Strength Manager and Human Resource Officer
                              at Theater and Corps level; a Company Commander; Company Tactical Officer and Regimental
                              Executive Officer at the United States Military Academy, West Point, New York; Brigade
                              Human Resource Officer for 4/3 ID; Deputy G1, 2nd Infantry Division, and G1 United
                              States Army Training Center and Fort Jackson.  Lieutenant Colonel Updegraff has deployed
                              to Iraq twice and twice to Korea in her career.
                         
                     
                     CPT REED ZIEGLER - Assistant Professor of Military Science MS II Instructor
                     
                      
                     
                     MSG CHRISTOPHER CAMPBELL. - Senior Military Instructor MS I Instructor MS III Instructor
                     
                      
                     
                     
                     
                       MR. BRIAN HAGOOD - Recruiting Operations Officer Mr. Hagood is currently a Major in the Tennessee Army National Guard.Mr. Brian Hagood
                           is the Recruiting Operations Officer and has been with Tiger Battalion since December
                           2007. He received his commission as an active duty Signal Officer in 1997 upon completion
                           of ROTC at the University of North Alabama. He deployed in 1999 in support of Task
                           Force Hawk in Albania as a Platoon Leader. In 2003, he deployed in support of Operation
                           Enduring Freedom (OEF) and Operation Iraqi Freedom (OIF) as a Joint Operations Center
                           Battle Captain for CJTF-4 and as a Signal Officer for the United States Army Corps
                           of Engineers. In 2004, he deployed support of OIF II and again in 2005 in support
                           of OIF 05-07 as a Company Commander. In 2011, he deployed in support of OEF as a Brigade
                           Signal Officer.
                     
                       MR. CASSIUS DAVIS - Supply Technician Mr. Cassius Davis is the Tiger Battalion's Supply Technician. He retired as a Sergeant
                           First Class, with a total of 20 years of active military service. His last assignment
                           before retiring was as the S4 for the Army Reserve Officer Training Program in the
                           northeastern part of the United States. In 2014, Mr. Davis earned an Associate of
                           Science degree in Criminal Justice Administration, and in 2015, a Bachelor of Science
                           degree in Criminal Justice Law Enforcement.  Mr. Davis has been with The University
                           of Memphis Army ROTC since August, 2005. 
                     
                     MR. PARRISH KIMBLE - Human Resources Assistant Mr. Parrish Kimble is the Tiger Battalion's Human Resources Assistant.  He retired
                           as a Petty Officer First Class with 20 years of active military service in the United
                           States Navy. His last assignment before retiring was as the Site Security Manager
                           at the Pass and ID Office on board Naval Support Activity Mid-South in Millington
                           TN. In 2013, Mr. Kimble earned an Associate of Science degree in Management from Park
                           University. Mr. Kimble has been with The University of Memphis Army ROTC since November
                           2014.
                       MS. DONNA HASKINS - Administrative Secretary Ms. Donna Haskins, a native of Memphis, joined the Tiger Battalion in 2012; however,
                        she is no stranger to the battalion as she was previously assigned to Army ROTC on
                        a temporary basis.  Ms. Haskins has been with the University of Memphis since 2006.
