                     Faculty Affiliates
                     
                     The International Studies Faculty Affiliate Program brings together faculty whose
                        research and teaching focuses on international topics and world regions. Faculty affiliates
                        enjoy:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        access to collaborative opportunities for research, through networking with other
                           faculty affiliates
                        
                        
                        invitations to International Studies sponsored events
                        
                        announcements for courses, grants, and other opportunities for your students that
                           come through the INTL listserv
                        
                        
                        ability to mentor students pursuing internationally-focused capstone experiences
                        
                        invitations to apply for funding to support international research travel
                        
                     
                     
                     If you are interested in joining this program, please contact kbrondo@memphis.edu.
