                     Give to Student Affairs
                     
                     Students who are engaged and involved on campus are much more likely to succeed in
                        college and graduate. Your gift will foster student learning through engagement and
                        involvement by underwriting leadership-training programs and scholarships, supporting
                        student life at the UofM., and enhancing the involvement of parents.
                     
                     
                     Donating to the office of Student Leadership and Involvement will help us offer a
                        variety of leadership training programs designed to compliment our students' academic
                        experiences.
                     
                     
                     Donating to the the Donald K. Carson Leadership Scholarship will help award scholaships
                        to a full-time student who has completed a minimum of 12 hours at the University of
                        Memphis while maintaining a minimum cumulative 2.8 GPA.
                     
                     
                     Donating to the Parent Fund today will help us continue to provide you with excellent
                        parent services. Each donation helps to continue the services that we provide to the
                        parents of University of Memphis students and increase our efforts to help you help
                        our students.
