                        Nominations  for Lupfer Excellence in Undergraduate Teaching Award
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     Michael B. and Shirley L. Lupfer 2016 Excellence in Undergraduate Teaching Award
                     
                     
                     Nature of the Award
                     
                     
                        
                        The award consists of an engraved plaque and a monetary award ($4,000.00 for this
                           award cycle, but subject to change in subsequent years) generously funded by an endowment
                           provided by former University of Memphis Department faculty members Dr. Michael B.
                           and Mrs. Shirley L. Lupfer.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Frequency of AwardÂ 
                     
                     
                        
                        The award is given at four-year intervals, with the first being made December 2012.
                        
                     
                     
                     Criteria
                     
                     
                        
                        The recipient must be a fulltime member of the Psychology Department faculty who:
                        
                        Teaches undergraduate courses (classroom, online, or both) every year, typically in
                           both fall and spring semesters;
                        
                        
                        Models scholarly teaching by seeking new evidence-based teaching methods, using them
                           in courses and assessing their effectiveness;
                        
                        
                        Consistently achieves outstanding student ratings;
                        
                        Demonstrates creativity in classroom activities and out-of-class assignments;
                        
                        Revises course content and procedures in order to remain current in the field;
                        
                        Provides undergraduate mentorship and service, including serving as mentor on Senior
                           Honors Theses when requested to do so; and
                        
                        
                        Maintains a supportive presence at undergraduate functions and is readily accessible
                           to undergraduate students.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Selection
                     
                     
                        
                        Nominations are solicited from Psychology faculty, staff, alumni, and current students
                           (both undergraduate and graduate).
                        
                        
                        Immediate past winners are not eligible for consideration.
                        
                        Nominees are required to submit a brief description of their career teaching-related
                           accomplishments, with special attention to achievements in the past four years that
                           are relevant to the award criteria.
                        
                        
                        Selection of the winner will be made by a committee comprised of the Chair of the
                           Department, the Director of the Undergraduate Program, and the President of the UofM
                           chapter of Psi Chi or his/her designated representative from among the Psi Chi officers.
                           The Department Chair may appoint substitutes in the event of conflicts of interest
                           or if the appointed members are unable to serve for any other reason.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Deadline for Submission of Materials (Nominations and Candidate Statement)
                     
                     
                        
                        November 23, 2016 for the present award cycle.
                        
                     
                     
                     (Drs. Helen Sable and Melloni Cook shared the award in 2012 and, consequently, are
                           not eligible for consideration.)
