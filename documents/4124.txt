                     Welcome
                     
                     Welcome to the undergraduate research, scholarship and creative activities website.
                        At the University of Memphis, motivated students have the opportunity to play a very
                        important role in the creation of new knowledge. Through student-faculty collaborations
                        and mentoring partnerships, students become engaged in their fields in ways that go
                        beyond the traditional classroom settings.
                     
                     
                     As a student researcher at the University of Memphis, you will:
                     
                     
                        
                        Meet other students who share your passion to discover and explore
                        
                        Clarify your academic, career, and personal interests
                        
                        Learn valuable skills to help you in your field of study
                        
                        Develop a mentored relationship with one or more of the University's faculty members
                        
                        Strengthen your record of achievement in support of applications to graduate and professional
                           schools
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     This website will familiarize faculty mentors and students alike with the wide variety
                        of undergraduate research and creative achievement opportunities on the University
                        of Memphis campus.
