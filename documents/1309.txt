                     ADULT EDUCATION (EDD)
                     Major: Higher and Adult Education Concentration: Adult Education Degree: EDD Contact:   Jeffery Wilson phone:  901.678.3428 Admission and Program Information: Program of Studies Worksheet Program Information
                     
                     The HIAD faculty is offering the Ed.D. (with a concentration in Adult Education) online
                        beginning Spring 2011. It will require attendance on campus for one week during the
                        summer. To apply to the program, go to HIAD home page for guidance. We welcome your
                        applications.
                     
                     
                     The Doctor of Education (Ed.D.) with a concentration in Adult Education is designed
                        for individuals seeking to enter academic professions or advance in fields such as
                        business and industry, health related occupations, community and non-profit organizations.
                        The Adult Education concentration prepares students to educate adult learners and
                        promote lifelong learning. Graduates with this degree work as health, religious, or
                        community educators; human resource developers; directors of staff development and
                        training in organizations; and as adult and family literacy specialists. By working
                        in these capacities, graduates of the Ed. D. program in Adult Education help adult
                        learners achieve their academic and professional goals.
                     
                     
                     The Program of Studies provides an overview of required coursework for completion
                        of the degree. Many students in the program have already established successful careers.
                        To meet the needs of working adults all our classes are available either online or
                        evenings/weekends. The Course Schedule shows courses available online to meet degree
                        requirements.
