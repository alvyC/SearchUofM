                     Study Abroad
                     
                     We highly encourage all students, but in particular Foreign Language majors and minors,
                        to pursue a study abroad program as part of their academic program.
                     
                     
                     The Department of Foreign Languages and Literatures offers several faculty-led study
                        abroad programs during the summer.  Information about these programs can be found
                        below.
                     
                     
                     The department also works closely with the Global Learning office on campus, which serves as an invaluable resource for all students interested
                        in an international academic experience.
