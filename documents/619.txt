                     SCHEDULE A VISIT
                     
                     Come visit us in our historic, state-of-the art facility today. During the semester,
                        we conduct admissions tours Monday - Friday at 12:00 noon. 
                     
                     
                     During this time, law student ambassadors from the Admissions Office are available
                        to give tours of our facility and to answer questions about law school. We do not
                        schedule tours on school holidays including spring break, weekends, or one week before
                        exams or during exams.
                     
                     
                     Please contact Penny Rogers in the Law Admissions Office at (901) 678-5403 or lawadmissions@memphis.edu to schedule a tour.
                     
                     
                     First-Year Class Visits
                     
                     During the academic year (August to April), prospective students may visit a first-year
                        class. Class visits are possible most days of the week, but an appointment is required.
                        There are no class visits available during the summer. To schedule a class visit,
                        please contact Penny Rogers in the Law Admissions Office at (901) 678-5403 or lawadmissions@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Meeting with an Admissions Representative
                     
                     The Dean of Law Admissions and the Director of Admissions are pleased to meet with
                        prospective students to answer questions about U of Memphis Law or to discuss your
                        law school application, but an appointment is required. To schedule an appointment,
                        please contact Penny Rogers in the Admissions Office at (901) 678-5403 or lawadmissions@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Meeting with the Tennessee Institute for Pre-Law (TIP) Director
                     
                     Director of Law School Diversity, Mrs. Jaqueline O' Bryant, is available to meet with
                        interested applicants by appointment only. To schedule an appointment, please contact
                        Brigitte Boyd in the TIP office at (901) 678-2528 or byboyd@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     General Public Tours
                     
                     If you are interested in touring the law school for reasons other than admissions,
                        you may contact the law school at (901) 678-4922 for more information. General tours
                        are given on a limited basis as time is available.
                     
                     
                     Traveling to Memphis Law
                     
                     If you are traveling from out of town to Memphis Law, we want to make your trip as
                        smooth and enjoyable as possible. The following links will help you plan your visit.
