                     Welcome
                     
                     The Online MBA Program is designed for ambitious professionals whose demanding schedule
                        and/or geographic location makes it difficult to attend classes in-person at the University
                        of Memphis.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                         Ranked in the Top 25 as Best Value , Online Business Schools for Veterans by US News & World Report and Top 25 by Princeton Review.
                        
                        AACSB Accredited Program – Standard achieved by only 5% business schools world-wide
                        
                        A 33 credit-hour program without concentration – No prerequisite courses required
                              for the core curriculum
                        
                        24/7 learning from synchronous and asynchronous instruction by globally-renowned faculty
