                     Master of Science in Reading Education
                     
                     The College of Education, Health and Human Sciences is proud to offer an online Masters
                        of Science in Reading Education degree. This program offers an in-depth theoretical
                        and pedagogical understanding of literacy practices, assessment, and policies. By
                        working closely with qualified faculty you can earn a degree that will enhance your
                        ability to influence the profession of literacy and ultimately positively affect the
                        lives of children.
                     
                     
                     The program prepares graduates for positions as:
                     
                     
                        
                        Literacy Coach
                        
                        Reading Specialist
                        
                        Curriculum Coordinators
                        
                        Literacy Administrators
                        
                     
                     
                     You may complete this degree online; no trips to campus are required. Our College
                        of Education, Health and Human Sciences is accredited by National Counsel of Accreditation
                        in Teacher Education and aligned with the International Reading Association standards. 
                        Faculty from the online Masters in Reading Program has the desire and expertise to
                        work with students with diverse experiences and specialties.
                     
                     
                     Graduates are encouraged to participate in the College of Education, Health and Human
                        Sciences Convocation ceremony which celebrates not only the success of the individual,
                        but also the rich traditions of the teaching profession.
                     
                     
                     The online Reading program is flexible and convenient for classroom teachers or other
                        full time professionals who have multiple responsibilities, yet are motivated to advance
                        their education. For more information on the Reading program, please complete and
                        submit the form below.
