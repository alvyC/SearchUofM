                       Software and Applications
                     
                     
                     University software (Microsoft, Browsers, etc.) and applications (Banner, etc.).
                     
                      
                     
                     ALEKS
                     
                     The ALEKS placement tests are available to incoming students only; they are allowed
                           to take the test once for free. If a student desires to retake the ALEKS math placement
                           tests, there is a fee associated with subsequent attempts to complete the math placement
                           tests.  
                     
                     AskTom
                     
                     AskTom is a part of the Remedyforce service desk system. AskTom is a searchable database
                           of questions about all aspects of the University of Memphis readily available to faculty,
                           staff, and students at the UofM. http://asktom.memphis.edu
                     
                     Banner ERP  
                     
                     Banner is the primary system of record for the University of Memphis. Banner can be
                           accessed via links in the myMemphis portal. Banner applications include: Student,
                           Financial Aid, Finance, Human Resources, and Advancement.
                     
                     Banner Workflow  
                     
                     Workflow is an automation of a business process, in whole or part, during which documents,
                           information, or tasks are passed from one participant to another for action, according
                           to a set of procedural rules.
                     
                     Blackboard
                     
                     Blackboard is the provider of our Campus Card system. Campus identification cards
                           are issued by the Bursar's Office to faculty/staff/students/guests. 
                     
                     BusinessHub
                     
                     BusinessHub is a resource for your copier machine. For information go to   https://bf.memphis.edu/bizhub/ .
                     
                     Campus Call
                     
                     Interface between the CampusCall server and Banner Application.
                     
                     Campus Card Online
                     
                     The University of Memphis issues each student, faculty, and staff member an identification
                           card that bears their name and image, referred to as your Campus Card.
                     
                     Collegiatelink
                     
                     Collegiate link is an event/organization management system that can be used by students,
                           faculty, and staff.  The owner of this service is the Office of Student Leadership
                           and Involvement.
                     
                     CEHHS Database
                     
                     Database used by the College of Education to get quick reports based on their students.
                     
                     DegreeCompass
                     
                     DegreeCompass is a tool allowing students the ability to utilize technology tools
                           for degree planning.
                     
                     DegreeWorks (umDegree)
                     
                     umDegree is a web-based tool to help students and advisors monitor progress toward
                              degree completion.
                     
                     Door Access Systems
                     
                     Door Access System is the tool the University of Memphis uses to control access to
                           campus facilities, buildings, and rooms.  
                     
                     Dynamic Forms
                     
                     Dynamic Forms provides a simple way for its users to take any paper-based form and
                           quickly build a sophisticated interactive electronic form that can be published to
                           the internet.
                     
                     eContracts
                     
                     Manage all of your eContracts, approvals, resources, and more at University of Memphis
                           eContracts. Please Login: Username; Password.  https://bf.memphis.edu/econtract/
                     
                     Employee Performance Appraisal
                     
                     The online staff performance appraisal system is available for supervisors and employees
                           at https://umwa.memphis.edu/performance/
                     
                     ETD (Electronic Thesis and Dissertation) Web App
                     
                     Electronic Thesis and Dissertation is a web application that allows these documents
                           to be submitted and reviewed electronically.
                     
                     Event Management/Room Scheduling (EMS)
                     
                     This is the University of Memphis Room Scheduling System available at http://vems.memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     FormFusion
                     
                     Evisions FormFusion application is responsible for formatted printing of various forms
                           (i.e. student transcripts, student enrollment verification, W2 Tax Forms, etc.).
                     
                     Graduate School Candidacy Forms
                     
                     Forms used by the Graduate School to gather data from students who are ready to graduate
                           from the school.
                     
                     Hobson
                     
                     Requests can be made by authorized Faculty and Staff members. Hobson's Connect is
                           a tool to manage applicant and prospect data and communications. Hobson's Retain is
                           a tool to manage current student (grad and undergrad) data and communications. These
                           communications are primarily focused on retention and student success.
                     
                     Identity Finder
                     
                     Identity Finder is a tool used to verify that the University of Memphis is compliant
                           with PCI regulations.
                     
                     IntelleCheck
                     
                     Evisions Intellecheck application is responsible for payroll check printing and direct
                           deposit payroll advice creation/emailing.
                     
                     Kronos Timekeeper
                     
                     Kronos Timekeeper application interfaces with time clocks (data terminals) and desktops
                           (via the Web) throughout the campus to track employee work hours.
                     
                     Law Rankings
                     
                     Interface between the CampusCall server and Banner Application
                     
                     Learning Curve
                     
                     Learning Curve provides faculty and staff training opportunities. Login at https://bf.memphis.edu/training/ .
                     
                     LogMeIn Rescue
                     
                     LogMeIn Rescue is a tool used by internal ITS and can be requested by a Faculty or
                           Staff member. There are license limitations for LogMeIn Rescue, UofM has 25 concurrent
                           licenses.
                     
                     Library
                     
                     Interface between the Library system and the Banner accounts receivables module.
                     
                     Marketplace
                     
                     Marketplace is a service that provides shopping cart functionality for fees, services,
                           and merchandise outside tuition.
                     
                     Nursing Application
                     
                     Application for students who wish to enroll in the undergraduate(BSN) or graduate
                           (MSN) nursing program.
                     
                     OnBase (Matrix) OnBase (Matrix) is a document management system in use by multiple departments within
                           the university. It allows the scanning, storage, and retrieval of paper documents
                           as well as the importing and retrieval of other types of files.
                     
                     NSO (New Student Orientation Registration app)
                     
                     The New Student Orientation Registration application is available for first time admitted
                           students to register for an orientation session.
                     
                     PFI (Physical Facilities Inventory)
                     
                     The PFI (Physical facilities inventory system) is used by Space Planning to create
                           data to be sent to TBR for the analysis of current and future space needs for the
                           University.
                     
                     Prism
                     
                     Prism is a compensation tracking application used by Human Resources.
                     
                     Remedyforce
                     
                     Remedyforce is the application for the University of Memphis Service Desk.
                     
                     
                     
                     Reporting/Business Intelligence Software
                     
                     Reporting Tools enables you to access a full range of reporting and analysis tools,
                           including interactive dashboards, ad hoc queries, financial reports, and search. 
                     
                     ResLife/ResNet
                     
                     ResNet lets you connect your computer and other electronic devices directly to UofM's
                           computer network and—through the University network—to the Internet. For more information
                           go to http://www.memphis.edu/resnet/ .
                     
                     
                     Royall (third party vendor student application interface)
                     
                     Royall (for Admissions) is the hosted solution we use for undergraduate applications.  
                     
                     
                     SETE/Teacher Evaluation
                     
                     Student Evaluation of Teaching Effectiveness (SETE) provides an opportunity for students
                           to evaluate all of their courses and instructors. http://www.memphis.edu/sete/ .
                     
                     StarRez
                     
                     StarRez is the University of Memphis housing portal. For more information go to https://srweb.memphis.edu/StarRezPortal/ .
                     
                     University mobile app (Straxis)
                     
                     The Straxis mobile app accommodates all phones. In addition to GPS, map locations,
                           and the ability to check grades, students can access information about the Blue Line,
                           read about upcoming events, and stream WUMR, the student radio station.
                     
                     Studio Abroad
                     
                     Studio Abroad is a hosted web site that allows University of Memphis students to register
                           for Study Abroad courses.
                     
                     Student Immunization Support Documents
                     
                     Tennessee requires state public institutions of higher education to verify that students
                           have received mandated immunizations and health requirements. You may upload immunization
                           documentation at https://umwa.memphis.edu/immunizations .
                     
                     T2 Parking Interface
                     
                     This is the Interface between the T2 third party Parking server and Banner Accounts
                           receivables.
                     
                     TigerBuy/SciQuest
                     
                     Tigerbuy is the University of Memphis online marketplace, a SciQuest product.
                     
                     TigerXpress/Touchnet
                     
                     TigerXpress/Touchnet is a cashiering and bill payment suite students can access and
                           pay balances.
                     
                     TracDat
                     
                     TracDat is a tool to track outcomes from initiatives on campus.
                     
                     Technology Resource Locator(TRL)
                     
                     TRL is used by LSPs as well as faculty and staff to find rooms available with particular
                           equipment.  LSPs are tasked with maintaining supplies.
                     
                     UC 4
                     
                     UC4 is a tool used to schedule SQL jobs, SHL scripts, and Banner INB jobs.  Also used
                           by some functional users to request a script written by IT or request a chain of Banner
                           INB jobs.
                     
                     Veterans Affairs Deferment Certification
                     
                     A VA Deferment is a request to defer payment of Tuition and fees until the end of
                           the term. You make a request for VA Deferment letter through the Veterans Educational
                           Benefits & Certification Office (003 Wilder Tower) after you register for classes
                           and complete a Veterans Request for Certification form.
                     
                     Windstar
                     
                     The Windstar application is used to report and track taxation of International employees
                           and international students who are employees. The data it provides is used to create
                           the IRS 1042 form.
                     
                     WorkForum
                     
                     WorkForum is the employee hiring process management tool.
