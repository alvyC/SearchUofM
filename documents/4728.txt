                       Classroom and Lab Support
                     
                     
                     Issues related to software and audio visual technology in classrooms, public areas,
                        and conference rooms.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Computer Lab - Create and deploy university standard operating system 
                     
                     ITS will create a standard operating system for lab deployment. Your Local Support
                        Provider may make modifications as needed.
                     
                     
                     Computer Lab - Design 
                     
                     An ITS employee will work with one of the Universities A/V and Computer vendors to
                        spec out equipment for the space in question.
                     
                     
                     Computer Lab - Repair Equipment
                     
                     Repair lab equipment, including computer station, monitor, keyboard, mouse, and printer.
                     
                     iPrint  
                     
                     iPrint gives students the ability to print in certain locations from their personal
                        computer (desktop or laptop). More info can be found at http://iprint.memphis.edu . A request for assistance with this service can be made at anytime.
                     
                     
                     Scheduling a Collaboration Room (UC265)
                     
                     Located in the University Center, Room 265, are 4 Collaborative spaces that students
                        can  reserve to discuss group projects and prepare for presentations. 
                     
                     
                     Smart Classroom - Design
                     
                     An ITS employee will work with one of the Universities A/V and Computer vendors to
                        spec out equipment for the space in question.
                     
                     
                     Smart Classroom - Maintenance
                     
                     Repair smart classroom equipment, including computer station, projector, projector
                        screen, document camera, and DVD player.
                     
                     
                     Smart Classroom - Provide Consulting and Training
                     
                     Training session on how to properly utilize the technology in the smart classroom
                        you will be teaching in.
                     
                     
                     Software - Evaluate
                     
                     Requests can be made to examine the feasibility of adding or updating software in
                        the current computer lab environment.
                     
                     
                     Software -  Install
                     
                     Requests can be made to have new software installed within your computer lab.
                     
                     Software -  TAF Procurement
                     
                     Requests can be made to purchase new software for use in either a computer lab or
                        Smart Classroom utilizing TAF funding.
