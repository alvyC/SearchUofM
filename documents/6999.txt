                        CCLC
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     Chemistry Community Learning Center (CCLC) J. M. Smith Chemistry - Room 112
                     
                     
                     Available at the cclc:
                     
                     
                        
                        10 PC workstations with access to UMWare software for Chemistry and STEM course work
                        
                        Several textbooks for General Chemistry (CHEM 1110 / 1120), the non-majors Chemistry
                           of Materials course (CHEM 1010) and for Organic Chemistry (CHEM 3301), all for in-room
                           use
                        
                        
                        Plenty of tables, couches, and work-space at which students may study in groups or
                           as individuals
                        
                        
                        The CCLC is staffed by chemistry faculty and lab instructors who are there to help
                           students learn how to work through study problems and to explain concepts that may
                           be difficult to grasp
