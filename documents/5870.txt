                     Art of the South 2016 Juried Exhibition presented by Number: Inc
                     
                     May 16 – June 17, 2016 Opening reception: June 20
                     
                     
                     A juried exhibition, open to all artists 18 and older working in any media residing
                        in AL, AR, FL, GA, KY, LA, MA, MO, MS, NC, OK, TN, TX, SC, VA, or WV. Accepted work
                        will be exhibited in one of two locations: The Martha and Robert Fogelman Galleries
                        of Contemporary Art at the University of Memphis and Gallery 121 at Belmont University
                        in Nashville. The two galleries will be connected through Skype the night of the opening
                        (May 20) and will be open to the public from May 16 – June 17, 2016. Juror: Chad Alligood,
                        Assistant Curator of Special Projects at Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art, Bentonville,
                        AR.
                     
                     
                     For more information, please visit  numberinc.org/art-of-the-south/
                     
                     
                     
                     UNIVERSITY OF MEMPHIS ART EDUCATION ALUMNI JURIED EXHIBITION, 2016
                     
                     SCHEDULE:
                     
                     
                        
                        February 8 Art Alumni/Student Submission Deadline and accepting work 3:30-5:30, Gallery A, Art
                           and Communication Building, 3715 Central Avenue.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        February 19 Opening Reception - 3:30-5:30 pm Awards at 4:00, The Martha and Robert Fogelman Galleries
                           of Contemporary Art.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        March 14 Pick up work - 3:30-5:30 Art and Communication Building, room 114, Art Education.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     CRITERIA:
                     
                     
                        
                        Title: University of Memphis 10th Annual Art Education Alumni Juried Exhibition
                        
                        Theme: "Finding New Words Through Vision and Creativity"
                        
                        Media Focus: Painting, Collage, Assemblage, Ceramics, Mixed Media, Print, Pastel,
                           Photography
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Each alumni must submit one recent work of art, and two students work (one male and
                        one female). The artwork must communicate visually the theme of "Finding New Words
                        Through Vision and Creativity". Student work may or may not show a relationship to
                        their Art Teacher's work. Share your visual interpretation of finding new words through
                        creativity and vision. This theme can be visually communicated through content or
                        media.
                     
                     
                     The University of Memphis Annual Art Education Alumni Juried Exhibition features area
                        K-12 art educators who are graduates of The University of Memphis Art Education Program,
                        or who have completed coursework at the University during their teaching preparation
                        or careers. This exhibition is held each year during Youth Art Month and is in accordance
                        with the mission and vision of The University of Memphis, the Department of Art, and
                        the Tennessee Art Education Association.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     33rd Annual Juried Student Exhibition at The University of Memphis
                     
                     Starts 11/23/2015 Ends on 12/18/2015
                     
                     The 33rd Annual Juried Student Exhibition is a competitive art exhibition of works of all media open to eligible University
                        of Memphis students selected by judge  Michael Aurbach , a sculptor and Professor at Vanderbilt University. Selected works will be on view
                        in The Martha and Robert Fogelman Galleries of Contemporary Art from January 22 to February 5, 2016.
                     
                     
                     DEADLINE FOR ONLINE SUBMISSIONS IS FRIDAY, DECEMBER 18, 2015, AT 11:59 PM CT.
                     
                     ELIGIBILITY
                     
                     University of Memphis undergraduate and graduate students enrolled in a U of M Department
                        of Art course in 2015 (i.e. Spring, Summer, or Fall 2015). This includes studio art,
                        graphic design, and photography. Non-majors are welcome to enter. Students who have
                        graduated and are not currently enrolled at U of M cannot submit an entry (Fall 2015
                        graduates are eligible).
                     
                     
                     AWARDS
                     
                     
                        
                        President's Purchase Award : $1,000 art purchase (sponsored by the Office of the President); open to all students
                        
                        
                        Graduate Purchase Award : $1,000 art purchase (sponsored by the Graduate School); open to graduate students
                           only
                        
                        
                        Department of Art Award (one Undergraduate, one Graduate): cash prize
                        
                        
                        Merit Awards : Two $100 Merchandise Certificates (sponsored by Art Center Supply Stores, Inc.),
                           annual memberships to local museums; open to all students
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Awards will be presented at the opening reception at The Martha and Robert Fogleman
                        Galleries of Contemporary Art on Friday, January 22, 2016, 5-8 PM.
                     
                     ENTRY GUIDELINES
                     
                     
                        
                        Limit of two (2) entries per student
                        
                        Works must have been completed in 2015
                        
                        Work cannot include live organic material, candles, or open flames
                        
                        Work cannot exceed 120 inches in height (10 feet) when installed and must be able
                           to clear the front entrance of ACB (60 X 76 inches); works exceeding these dimensions
                           may be considered for alternative spaces.
                        
                        
                        Entries selected for the exhibition must be outfitted with appropriate hanging or
                              installation hardware and must be dropped off at the Fogelman Galleries January 14-15,
                              2016
                        
                     
                     
                     HOW TO ENTER
                     
                     
                        
                        Complete all required fields of the online entry submission form
                        
                        Each online entry MUST be accompanied by 1-2 uploaded images (alternate views, for
                              example) or other media files for consideration
                        
                        Please complete a second entry form if you wish to submit a second entry
                        
                        Applicants will be notified of selection in early January
                        
                     
                     
                     DEADLINE FOR ONLINE SUBMISSIONS IS FRIDAY, DECEMBER 18, 2015, AT 11:59 PM CT.
                     
                     If you have questions or need more information please email fogelmangalleries@memphis.edu or call 901.678.3052
