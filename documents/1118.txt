                     CAST Cybersecurity Workshop
                     
                     June 9th - 10th
                     
                     
                        
                        The Cluster to Advance cyber Security & Testing (CAST) in partnership with the Center
                           for Information Assurance (CfIA), the Systems Testing Excellence Program (STEP) and
                           the FedEx Institute of Technology is pleased to announce the 1st Annual Research Workshop
                           on Advances and Innovations in Cyber Security. The event will be held June 9-10, 2016.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Register here
                     
                     STEP UP
                     
                     June 14th - 15th
                     
                     
                        
                        We are excited to announce that we will be offering the 2-Day STEP Up course on June
                           14-15 at the FedEx Institute of Technology on the University of Memphis campus. This
                           course is designed to provide an in-depth look at the latest advances in systems testing
                           and systems testing management. Keep your skills up to date with our custom created
                           research-based continuing professional development modules covering the latest advances
                           and emerging topics in systems testing.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Register here
                     
                     Tech Talks
                     
                     June 16th - 6:30 PM
                     
                     
                        
                        Join us on June 16th at 6:30 at the Institute for an evening of Lightning Talks about
                           what Memphis Tech Professionals are passionate about. This event, free and open to
                           the public, is an opportunity to meet local experts in programming languages, hardware
                           development, emerging technologies, and other technology innovations that take Memphis
                           beyond the edge of what's possible.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Register here
                     
                     
                     
                     CAST STEP Certification
                     
                     June 20th - 24th
                     
                     
                        
                        We are excited to announce that we will be offering the one-week STEP Foundational
                           Program on June 20-24 at the FedEx Institute of Technology on the University of Memphis
                           campus.
                        
                        
                        The cost of the course is $1,680. To sign up for the course, please go to the registration
                           page.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Register here
                     
                     GenCyber Workshop -High School
                     
                     June 25th-29th
                     
                     
                        
                        The University of Memphis is excited to host the 2016 GenCyber Boot Camp for high school students . The camp will cover various aspects of Cyber Hygiene and students will learn about
                           privacy, security and safe browsing on the Internet. They will also be introduced
                           to cyber ethics and participate in hands-on sessions that are educational, fun and
                           challenging. There will be opportunities to meet cyber experts and industry professionals.
