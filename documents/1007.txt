                     Marketing Toolkit
                     
                     Looking for a logo or PowerPoint template? Need a quick invitation template or guidance
                        on setting up a branded email blast? Check out our toolkit to facilitate your marketing
                        efforts. Interested in a resource that isn't included in this area? Send us a note
                        at logo@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Adobe (Acrobat Pro and Creative Cloud) A TBR/Adobe agreement provides a University site license for Acrobat Pro and Creative
                        Cloud. These are the programs you'll need to use to work with the templates found
                        in the Toolkit or your old files from our server we arrange to send you. For more
                        details on downloading the programs, visit memphis.edu/umtech/solutions/software/softwarefacultystaff.php .
                     
                     
                     NOTE: All University collateral material is required to carry a production code and
                        an affirmative action statement, and our office still needs to give final approval
                        before anything is sent to production or placed with media. For more information,
                        visit the Resources section of our Brand Standards site.
