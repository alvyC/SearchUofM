                     UofM Vocabulary
                     
                      Every institution has their own vocabulary and lingo that is unique to them. Below
                        are some of the commonly used words and acronyms that your student might be using
                        in their time on campus.
                     
                      
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              
                              
                              
                                 
                                 
                                    
                                    
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Nickname
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Description
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             ALEKS Test
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             ALEKS is a powerful artificial intelligence based mechanism to assess math proficiency
                                                and skill level. New incoming students are required to take the test to be properly
                                                placed in their mathematics course. 
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Carpenter Complex
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Apartment style residence hall north of Central Avenue
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Centennial
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Residence hall at Patterson Street and Norriswood Avenue with traditional single and
                                                double rooms along with 24 apartments.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             CRIS
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Campus Recreation and Intramural Services is located south of Southern Avenue. The
                                                office manages the Student Recreation and Fitness Center which offers workout equipment,
                                                a pool, volleyball, basketball and tennis courts for student, faculty, and staff use.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Flex Bucks
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Flex Bucks are funds that students receive with their meal plan which supplement the
                                                plan. The funds can be used at any dining facility. 
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Dining Dollar$
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Each full time UofM undergraduate student is automatically enrolled in the Dining
                                                Dollar$ plan. Each semester (fall and spring), your student's account will be automatically
                                                charged $300 for him or her to use at on campus dining facilities and vending machines.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             FedEx Forum
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Basketball stadium, located in downtown Memphis, where the Men's basketball team plays
                                                their home games. The Forum is also home to the Memphis Grizzlies, a NBA team.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Fieldhouse
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Athletic facility where the women's basketball and volleyball teams compete when at
                                                home.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Fresh Connections
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Fresh Connections are learning communities that use clusters of courses to connect
                                                   different disciplines to a common theme. The specially selected faculty incorporate
                                                   their specific theme in assignments and activities throughout the semester.
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Frosh Camp
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             A four day camp away from Memphis for incoming freshmen students. It is a supplement
                                                   to orientation, allowing students to meet campus leaders and fellow classmates.
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Go Tigers Go!
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Chant from the University fight song at the University of Memphis that can be heard
                                                at athletic functions and around campus. 
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             "I'm So Glad I Go to U of M"
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             University song that can be heard at athletic events
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Liberty Bowl
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Football stadium, located at Central Avenue and East Parkway, where the football team
                                                plays their home games.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             LLC
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Living Learning Complex.  A residence hall located on Patterson Avenue that houses
                                                themed or major based communities of students.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             MATA
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Memphis Area Transit Authority provides bus services in and around Memphis
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             myMemphis
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             System that allows students to manage their personal information. Allows students
                                                to register for classes, view their financial aid package, view student account, view
                                                transcript, and request enrollment verification.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             TBR
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Tennessee Board of Regents.  Governing board over the University of Memphis.
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Tiger Den
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Dining location in Jones Hall that offers options including an all you care to eat
                                                style buffet, Wendy's and Subway
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Tiger's Restaurant
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             A full service restaurant located in the University Center.  It includes a big screen
                                                   television and pool tables for students to enjoy.
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Tiger Funds
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Money added to a student's account. The funds can be used like a debit card at locations
                                                across campus, including dining locations, some vending machines, and the bookstore. Funds
                                                can be added to a student's account through the Bursar's office in Wilder Tower or
                                                online.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Tiger Escort
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Escort service that takes students to their car or residence hall at night. 
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             TigerText
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Alert system for students, faculty and staff in case of an emergency on campus. This
                                                optional service is used in the event of an on-campus emergency, an unscheduled University
                                                closing, or a delay or cancellation of classes due to, for instance, inclement weather.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             TOM
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             University of Memphis live mascot, TOM the Tiger. TOM (Tigers of Memphis) lives in
                                                a sanctuary in south Memphis and his care is funded by the Alumni Association. TOM
                                                is a regular visitor at home football games.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             UC
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             University Center. Building on campus that houses student services, including a 24/7
                                                computer lab, campus post office and boxes, student organization offices, dining options,
                                                and copy services. Often called the heart of campus.
                                             
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             UMdrive
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Online storage program that allows students to upload documents to their individual
                                                   account so that they can access them from any computer with an internet connection.
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             The Union
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Food court located in the University Center that offers a variety of food options,
                                                   including a Chick-fil-a, Taco Bell, pizza, and Grab-and-Go station.
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                       
                                       
                                       
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Wilder Tower
                                             
                                          
                                          
                                          
                                             
                                             Tallest building on campus that houses student services offices, including the Registrar,
                                                Bursar, Admissions, Financial Aid, Recruitment and Orientation, Career Services, Counseling
                                                Center, Student Disabilities, and Commencement.
