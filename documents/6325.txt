                     Mid-South Family & Community Empowerment Institute:
                     
                     Scope of Work The purpose of the Institute would be a multi-faceted facility focusing on service
                        to the community via the following major areas:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Immediate
                        
                        
                           
                            Training (BSW & MSW Students, LMSW, LCSW Licensing Training, Staff Development Programming,
                              Continuing Education).
                           
                           
                            Research and Data Center (gathering, analysis, and dissemination).
                           
                        
                        
                        Short Term
                        
                        
                           
                           Intake, Screening, Assessment, Evaluation and Support.
                           
                        
                        
                        Long Term
                        
                        
                           
                            Social work evidence-based interventions and wraparound services (Referral unless
                              specific gaps in service cannot be otherwise addressed).
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Benefits to Students
                     
                     
                        
                        The Institute would advance the pedagogic mission of the University of Memphis through
                           the development of student placements in a supportive environment in direct contact
                           with the Department of Social Work.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Graduation/Retention
                     
                     
                        
                        Ability to leverage community relationships to develop field internships to fit student
                           needs, particularly:
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Non-traditional students with complicated schedules
                           
                           Returning veterans
                           
                           Students with special needs
