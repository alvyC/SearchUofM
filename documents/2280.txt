                     In Memoriam
                     
                     
                     
                     2015 Outstanding Alumni Awards presented by The Journalism Alumni Club
                     
                     Wednesday, October 14, 2015 at 7 p.m. Registration: 6-7 p.m. (with cocktails) Tickets: $60 per person, $500 per table
                     
                     
                     Honoring Mike Pennington (posthumously), Charles E. Thornton Award for Outstanding
                        Alumni; James Overstreet, Charles E. Thornton Award for Outstanding Alumni; Otis Sanford,
                        Herbert Lee Williams Award; Amanda Mauck, Young Alumni Award; Aiden Porter, Emerging
                        Journalist Award
                     
                     
                     There will be a special presentation honoring Bill Wellborn, last year's recipient
                        of the Thornton Award, who passed away this year.
                     
                     
                     For more information, contact semiller@memphis.edu or 901.678.3043
