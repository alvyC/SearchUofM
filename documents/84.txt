                     Big Data and Analytics Certification
                     
                     FEBRUARY 13th-17th
                     
                     The Big Data and Analytics Research Cluster represents a major interdisciplinary collaborative
                        effort dedicated to research, education, and technology transfer at the University's
                        FedEx Institute of Technology. We are pleased to offer the week-long big data and
                        analytics certificate to provide training in a broad spectrum of topics critical to
                        the comprehension of big data implementation, utilization, analysis, and management. 
                     
                     
                     Join us for this exciting workshop and help expand your knowledge of the concept and
                        how it can affect your business, organization, and the word in which you live. 
                     
                     
                     Register Now
                     
                     Tentative Schedule: 
                     
                     
                     
                     Registration:
                     
                     Registration for the week long workshop is $1680.00 and includes continental breakfast,
                        lunch, refreshments, and parking for all five days. Group discounts are available. Please email cbehles@memphis.edu for information.
