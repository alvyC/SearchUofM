                     iTunes U
                     
                     iTunes U  enables departments, faculty, and staff the ability to provide a collection of information
                        and/or educational content. Originally designed for use with iOS devices, PC users
                        now have the ability to download the iTunes application. Consequently, enabling users
                        to access iTunes U content from various computers or devices. 
                     
                     
                     NOTE: An Apple ID is required to access apple products and services. If you currently
                        have one, there is no need to create a new one. If you do not have one, you can create
                        one for free by visiting the Apple ID webpage.  You must also have iTunes installed on your computer to utilize iTunes U.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     UofM iTunes U Affiliation
                     
                     To become affiliated with the UofM iTunes U site you must request your Apple ID to
                        be added by an iTunes U administrator. To request your Apple ID to be granted contributor
                        access, please submit a service  request with the ITS Service Desk. 
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     iTunes U Workshops
                     
                     For times and dates, or to register, visit the Center for Teaching and Learning Events Calendar on our website. In addition to the training provided, you may also visit the UofM iTunes U site for additional training documentation and links.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Additional information on iTunes U can be found at the links provided below:
                     
                     Overview -  UofM iTunes U Guidelines
                     
                     Getting Started - UofM  iTunes U User Guidelines
                     
                     Best Practices -   UofM iTunes U Best Practices
                     
                      
                     
                     Have questions or need technical assistance with iTunes U?
                     
                     Please contact the Service Desk at 901.678.8888 or submit a service request .
