                     Giving Online
                     
                     To make an online gift to the Carson Scholarship Fund, please follow the steps below:
                     
                     
                        
                        Click the Make a Gift button to the left or click here . Your browser will display the online giving form.
                        
                        
                        On the online giving form, under Please Designate Your Gift , select I would like to choose from a list of areas to support .
                        
                        
                        Next under Area of Support , select Libraries and Student Affairs and then under Designation select Student Affairs .
                        
                        
                        Finally under Designation Detail please select Donald K Carson Leadership Scholarship as your preferred gift designation.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Giving by Check
                     
                     Make your check payable to the University of Memphis Foundation. Please include the
                        words "Carson Scholarship Fund" on the check memo line. Mail your check to:
                     
                     
                     Department 238 The UofM Foundation P.O. Box 1000 Memphis, TN 38148-0001
                     
                     
                     Or personally deliver your check to the Alumni Center at 635 Normal Street. Office
                        hours are Monday - Friday, 8:00 am to 4:30 pm
                     
                     
                     Thank you for supporting the Carson Scholarship Fund!
