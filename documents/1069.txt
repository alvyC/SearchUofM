                     Past Events
                     
                     GenCyber Boot Camp for Students!
                     
                     This is a unique opportunity for students to learn about Cyber Security programs at
                        the University of Memphis, a nationally designated Center for Academic Excellence
                        in Information Assurance. The high school camp will be held June 20-24 and the middle
                        school camp will be July 25-29.
                     
                     
                     To register for the camp, visit memphis.edu/cfia/projects/gencyber.php
                     
                     Systems Testing Excellence Program Certification (Foundational Program)
                     
                     We are excited to announce that we will be offering the one-week STEP Foundational
                        Program on June 20-24 at the FedEx Institute of Technology on the University of Memphis
                        campus.
                     
                     
                     Date: June 20 & 24, 2016 Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Room 225
                     
                     
                     The cost of the course is $1,680. To sign up for the course, email Tammy Alexander
                        at: tlxander@memphis.edu and we'll secure your seat right away!
                     
                     
                     For more info, click here. 
                     
                     STEP-UP Two-Day Training Program
                     
                     Keep your skills up to date with our custom created research-based continuing professional
                        development modules covering the latest advances and emerging topics in systems testing.
                     
                     
                     Date: June 14 & 15, 2016 Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Room 225
                     
                     
                     The cost of the course is $810 and includes breakfast, lunch, refreshments and parking
                        for both days. Please see the Course Overview for more details.
                     
                     
                     To sign up for the course click here
                     
                     Ist Annual Research Workshop on Advances & Innovations in Cyber Security
                     
                     The workshop will be a mixture of presentations and open discussions. Attendees from
                        both academia and industry will benefit greatly from the open exchange of ideas and
                        methodologies on cyber security.
                     
                     
                     The Cyber Security Research Workshop features a half day of tutorials on Thursday,
                        June 9 and a full day of interdisciplinary research paper presentations and industry
                        panels on Friday, June 10, with an evening reception to follow.
                     
                     
                     Date: June 9 & 10, 2016 Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Methodist Presentation Theater
                     
                     
                     Click for more info and to register
                     
                      
                     
                     Cyber-Related Incident Management and Response
                     
                     Date: April 26, 2016, 1pm - 3pm Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Fishbowl
                     
                     
                     Event is free and open to the public, RSVP required. Click for more info and to register
                     
                     CAST Conversations
                     
                     Date: April 1, 2016 Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Video Conference Room Monthly Cybersecurity Research Fellow Meeting.
                     
                     
                     CAST Conversations
                     Date: February 5, 2016 Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Video Conference Room Monthly Cybersecurity Research Fellow Meeting.
                     
                     Cybersecurity Certificate Course
                     
                     Date: February 1-5, 2016 This foundational program in cybersecurity is designed to cover the fundamentals of

                     Click for more info and to register
                     
                     Cybersecurity Lightning Talks
                     Date: December 3rd, 2015 Time: 3pm - 6pm Place: FedEx Institute of Technology, Fishbowl Please join us for an evening of cybersecurity research project presentations with
                     special guest speaker: Special Agent Tim Marsh of the Memphis FBI Division. RSVP Required: There is no cost to attend, but please RSVP to : Tammy Alexander x1589
