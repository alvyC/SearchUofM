                     Online Courses and eRate Fee Information
                     
                     Tuition & Fees Charged to all students who take any classes at the University of Memphis. This fee
                        is assessed based on the student matriculation level (undergraduate/graduate/law)
                        and the number of hours enrolled, with no maximum for Mxx and TN eCampus (RODP) courses. 
                     
                     U of M Online Courses with completely numeric section numbers will be assessed at
                     the On-Campus rate and in addition to the Mxx and TN eCampus (RODP) courses (eRate will be assessed
                     on a per hour rate, with no maximum).
                     
                     Online Degree Programs Online Fees This fee is assessed on a per-hour basis, with no maximum and is in addition to tuition.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        TN eCampus (RODP) Online Fee – $125 per credit hour assessed to students registered for courses through the TN
                           eCampus Program (RODP) (courses designated with a section Rxx)
                        
                        
                        U of M Online Fee – $100 per credit hour assessed to students registered for U of M Online courses
                           (courses designated with a section Mxx) and classified as in-state for tuition assessment.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     eRate (Out-of-State) This tuition is assessed on a per-hour basis, with no maximum.
                     
                     
                     eRate tuition (replace standard out-of-state tuition at a reduced rate) is assessed
                        to students who are classified as non-residents of the State of Tennessee and enrolled in all online courses (instructional method of "Web"). Students taking online and ANY on-campus
                        course (NOT instructional method of "Web") will be assessed the standard out-of-state
                        tuition for all enrolled courses, the $100 per credit hour online fee and will not be eligible for the eRate .
                     
                     
                     The following courses are eligible for eRate:
                     
                     
                        
                        TN eCampus (RODP) – Sections Rxx
                        
                        U of M Online – Section Mxx
                        
                        U of M Online – Completely Numeric Sections xxx (ex. 410)
                        
                     
                     
                     eRate tuition is calculated as 50% of the Maintenance fee at the same matriculation
                        level and is assessed on a per hour basis, with no maximum.
                     
                     
                     Example : the Undergraduate Maintenance fee is $312.00 per hour; the corresponding eRate is
                        $156.00 per hour.
                     
                     
                     NOTE : Students eligible for the eRate are exempt from the U of M Online Fee. TN eCampus
                        (RODP) Online Fee will still apply for TN eCampus (RODP) Courses.
                     
                     
                     Examples: 
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              U of M Online Courses  Courses designated with a section Mxx
                              
                              
                              TN eCampus Program (RODP) Includes courses designated  with Rxx section numbers
                              
                              On-Campus hours mixed  with Online Courses (Not Eligible for eRate)
