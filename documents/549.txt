                     Cheer on our Tigers with the Blue Crew!
                     
                     
                     Blue Crew is a University of Memphis student group made up of the most passionate
                        Tiger fans on campus. If you're loud, rowdy, and crazy about your Memphis Tigers,
                        you need to GET INVOLVED WITH THE BLUE CREW! Blue Crew is open to students of all
                        ages.
                     
                     
                     GET CONNECTED!
                     
                     Like Blue Crew on Facebook! Follow us on Twitter: @MemphisBlueCrew Check out pics and videos on Instagram: @uofmbluecrew Tag your posts with #bluecrew
                     
                     
                     Interested in getting involved?
                     
                     Blue Crew is divided into two teams, the Spirit Team & the Game-Day Experience Team.
                     
                     Spirit Team
                     
                     
                        
                        Act as the Campus Street Team
                        
                        Act as the face of the Blue Crew on and off campus
                        
                        Assist Athletics with appearances by athletes/coaches on campus for promotion of games
                        
                        Place yard signs around campus and pick them up after game day
                        
                        Will have access to front row seats at Men's Basketball games  to serve as the student
                           section spirit leaders (leading chants, painted up, et cetera)
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Game-Day Experience Team
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Set-up the student tailgate for home football games
                        
                        First students in the doors to set up giveaways, other items for football and men's
                           basketball
                        
                        
                        Assist Athletics with coordination of student group themed nights at men's basketball
                        
                        Will assist Spirit Team with all on campus and game-day operations
                        
                        Will have access to front row seats at men's basketball games to assist Spirit Team
                            
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Important Fall 2015 Dates for all team members
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        All home games: 9/5, 9/24, 10/17, 10/31, 11/7, 11/28
                        
                        Wednesday night work: 9/2, 9/23, 10/14, 10/28, 11/4
                        
                        Campus street team: 9/3, 9/23, 10/15, 10/29, 11/5
                        
                     
                     
                      Contact bluecrew@memphis.edu for more information on getting involved on one of our teams!
