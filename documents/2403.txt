                           Jason Lee Executive Vice President / Chief Financial Officer Orion Federal Credit Union Executive MBA Class of 2013
                           
                           Jason Lee has over 15 years of professional experience in the financial service industry
                              and has served as the Executive Vice President / Chief Financial Officer for Orion
                              Federal Credit Union for 5 years. There he helps provides overall strategic direction
                              and strategy for the credit union, manages the investment portfolio and financial
                              planning functions, oversees the information systems and technology areas, and is
                              responsible for the strategic projects and electronic services departments. Additionally,
                              he serves on the credit union's Asset Liability Management and Credit Risk Management
                              Committees. Read More
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Juloy Raymer Chief Executive Officer Willow Enterprises Executive MBA Class of 2014
                           
                           Juloy Raymer has been in the healthcare field for over twenty years both in the hospital
                              and professional practice setting. Moving from physician practice operations, she
                              joined Methodist Le Bonheur Healthcare as finance manager moving to revenue services
                              director working to secure all reimbursement due the healthcare system from government
                              and managed care payers. After seven years in finance, Juloy made the switch to hospital
                              operations as administrator of the outpatient clinical services division of Methodist
                              Le Bonheur Healthcare running all outpatient clinics for the healthcare system. From
                              Methodist, Juloy moved into the vice president of revenue services role for UT Medical
                              Group (a four hundred provider multi-specialty practice) affiliated with the University
                              of Tennessee medical school. Juloy currently is managing partner and CEO at Willow
                              Enterprises. Willow Enterprises is a full service anesthesia provider practice servicing
                              multiple facilities in Mississippi and Arkansas. Under Juloy’s leadership, she hopes
                              to take Willow Enterprises to the premier provider of the anesthesia suite of services
                              in the Southeast region transforming anesthesia from a necessary expense at the facility
                              level to a value added service line. Read More
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Levernese Anderson Supply Planner, International Paper Executive MBA Class of 2009
                           
                           Levernese Anderson is a supply chain professional with more than ten years of experience
                              in supply chain planning and organization. Levernese currently serves as a Supply
                              Planner for International Paper where she manages the production capacity of about
                              200,000 tons of annual fluff pulp production that is exported all over the globe.
                              Levernese is also certified in Lean/Six Sigma tools and helps to move the organization
                              forward with regard to deliberate/process improvements. Read More
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Michele Buring Marketing Principal, Global Installed Solutions FedEx Services Executive MBA Class of 2014
                           
                           Michele Buring is a digital marketing professional with over 10 years of experience
                              designing and managing digital solutions for manufacturing, services and logistics
                              industries. Michele currently serves as Marketing Principal in Digital Access Marketing
                              at FedEx Services, a leading transportation and logistics provider headquartered in
                              Memphis, Tennessee, where she manages the Global Installed Solutions portfolio and
                              digital strategy. Michele joined FedEx in 2011 as a Sr. Marketing Specialist where
                              she led the successful re-design of automation solutions to reduce costs and generate
                              incremental revenue. Read More
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Roderick Head Executive MBA Class of 2012
                           
                           Roderick Head is a professional marketing strategist and adept business diagnostician
                              with over 21 years of entertainment and media experience. This experience includes
                              Def Jam Records, Elektra Records, ASCAP, BMI, NARM and Impact Magazine in various
                              roles. While a Featured Columnist at Impact, Roderick became known most for penning
                              "Ten Ways to Kill Your Brand: How to Ineffectively Run a Record Label." Read More
