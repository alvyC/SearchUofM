                     State of Tennessee Employee Fee Waiver Rules & Procedures
                     
                     Employees of the State of Tennessee may be eligible to enroll for *one (1) undergraduate
                        or graduate course per semester without payment of tuition, program service fees,
                        RODP program fees and RODP online fees. However, the student remains responsible for
                        payment of all other course fees, material fees, U of M online fees etc. and the submission
                        of a new certified fee waiver form each semester to the Bursar's Office by the appropriate fee payment deadline to avoid deletion of classes . The employment certification on the form should not be any earlier that July 1 for
                        Fall, December 1 for Spring and April 15 for Summer. For detail eligibility guidelines,
                        click on "Fee Waiver Rules for State Employees" below.
                     
                     
                     Enrollment under this program is on a space-available basis only. Participants may
                        not register before the specific deadline for each semester. See the Bursar's Office Calendar for the official first day to register under this program. Participants who register
                        during Late Registration WILL BE ASSESSED a $100 late registration fee not covered
                        by the fee waiver program.
                     
                     
                     NOTE: Enrollment in one course only for each Fall and Spring. Enrollment in two courses
                        for the Summer as long as the courses do not overlap between parts-of-term for the
                        Summer.
                     
                     
                     Summer Example:
                     
                     
                        
                        The fee waiver will cover enrollment in one course for the 1st part-of-term and one
                           course for 2nd part-of-term (parts-of-term do not overlap).
                        
                        
                        The fee waiver will only cover enrollment in one course if enrolled in full term (full
                           term overlaps all other Summer parts-of-term).
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Enrollment under this program is on a space-available basis only. Therefore participants
                        may not register before the specific deadline for each semester. [See the Bursar's Office Calendar for dates.] Participants who register during a Late Registration period WILL BE ASSESSED
                        a Late Registration Fee of $100 not covered by the fee waiver program.
                     
                     
                     After course selection, participants must submit their approved fee waiver form by
                        the fee payment deadline to The University of Memphis Bursar's Office, 115 Wilder Tower, Memphis, TN 38152-3520.
                        Forms may also be faxed to 901.678.0288.
                     
                     
                     State of Tennessee School Teachers are NOT eligible for this fee waiver.
                     
                     Forms are available from the employee's Human Resources department or from the Tennessee Higher Education Commission's Web site .
