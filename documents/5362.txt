                        Fall Freshmen 
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Fall 2016 Freshmen
                     
                     Make plans to attend one of these NSO sessions. Students who attend earlier sessions
                        generally have better options for class selections. Please note there are specific sessions for music majors (May 23-24, June 2-3, June
                           16-17, July 7-8, and July 11-12) as well as architecture and interior design majors
                           (May 23-24, June 2-3, July 11-12, July 28-29).
                     
                     Below are the New Student Orientation Dates for New Freshman Students. The deadline for the freshman orientation sessions is July 20, 2016 .
                     
                     May 23-24 *For Academic Scholarship Recipients Only*  (Session Closed)  May 26-27  (Session Closed) June 2-3  (Session Closed) June 6-7  (Session Closed) June 9-10  (Session Closed) June 16-17 (Session Closed) June 20-21  (Session Closed) July 7-8  (Session Closed) July 11-12  (Session Closed) July 14-15  (Session Closed) July 25-26  (Session Closed) July 28-29 (Session Closed)
                     
                     August 1-2 -- NEW SESSION ADDED!
                     
                     
                        
                     
                     
                     Below is an abbreviated outline of the schedule for your two-day session. You will
                        receive a more detailed schedule when you arrive at NSO. Orientation is a continuous
                        program with all activities scheduled in a necessary sequence. Students are required
                        to attend all sessions on both days and to spend the night on campus. The orientation
                        schedule requires extensive walking so please wear comfortable shoes, and bring an
                        umbrella!
                     
                     
                     DAY ONE
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              8:00-8:45
                              
                              Check-in & Information Fair
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              9:00-10:00
                              
                              Welcome and Meet Your Orientation Guides
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              10:00-12:00
                              
                              Academic Planning With Advisors
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              12:15-1:30
                              
                              Lunch and Information Fair
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              1:30-4:15
                              
                              Student Life Workshops and College Issues
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              6:00-7:00
                              
                              Dinner
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              7:00-9:30
                              
                              Pool Party at the Campus Recreation Center (Swimming, Basketball, Table Tennis, Video
                                 Games, and FUN, FUN, FUN!!!)
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     DAY TWO
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              8:00-9:00
                              
                              Breakfast
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              9:00-11:30
                              
                              Financial Aid & Scholarships, Health & Wellness, Preparing for Academic Success, Faculty
                                 Panel
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              11:30-4:00
                              
                              Academic Advising, Schedule Planning, Registration, and Light Lunch
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Students will have various times for advising and registration. Although you may finish
                        earlier, please be prepared to stay until 4:00 p.m.
                     
                     
                     All incoming freshmen will receive a brochure in their mailbox with detailed information
                        about New Student Orientation. If you did not receive a brochure in the mail, you
                        may download one here . You must have been admitted to the University of Memphis in order to attend New
                        Student Orientation.
                     
                     
                     Freshman Orientation Student Fee - $100 Includes one night in an on-campus residence hall, three meals, refreshment breaks,
                        and Orientation materials.
                     
                     
                     Registration for freshman sessions is open!
