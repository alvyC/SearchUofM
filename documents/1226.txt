                     LLC-HONORS AND SCHOLARS HOUSING
                     
                     
                     
                     The LLC has a unique floor plan that will house 496 students. The floor plan is comprised
                        of two buildings off of the main entrance to the residence hall. Each building has
                        4 floors and each floor has 3 individual neighborhoods where student rooms are located.
                        Each neighborhood offers 5 private baths along with mirror/sink areas. There is also
                        a variety of both single and double rooms in each neighborhood. In addition, each
                        neighborhood contains a community-style kitchenette with microwave, along with a sink
                        and 2 laundry closets per floor.
                     
                     This state-of-the-art facility offers other exceptional amenities such as card/key
                        access to the building, neighborhood and room, the ability to control heat/AC directly
                        from each room, wireless access and ethernet capabilities in each room; and a comfortable
                        lounge area on each floor where students can meet and mingle. Music students living
                        in the LLC will also have exclusive access to music practice rooms on the first floor
                        of the office building.
                     
                     
                     The LLC-Honors and Scholars Housing is centrally located on campus close to the new
                        University Center, dining facilities, and other buildings on campus.
