                     Your fun and effective fitness and nutrition program!
                     
                     Welcome to the Tiger Fit Web site!
                     
                     The Tiger Fit program is designed to educate and assist you toward reaching your goal
                        of achieving a healthier lifestyle.  We offer an affordable (only $20) eight-week
                        fitness and nutrition program for currently enrolled U of M students at the beginning of
                        each semester. 
                     
                     
                     This Web site consists of resources for the Tiger Fit program. Please check out our educational seminars for information
                        on exercise, nutrition, health, and motivation. You can also view a 40-second video of this program .
                     
                     
                     Please contact us to get in touch with a Tiger Fit staff member if you have any question or need additional
                        assistance in achieving your goals.
                     
                     
                     View our registration webpage for the Fall 2016 Tiger Fit program information and schedule.
                     
                     
                     Questions? Contact Katie Gerstemeier at krwells@memphis.edu or (901) 678-3274.
