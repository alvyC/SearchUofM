                     The Lambuth Library is located in Jackson, TN, on the University of Memphis Lambuth
                        Campus. The Library is a branch of the University of Memphis Libraries. All policies
                        are in accordance with University of Memphis Libraries Policies.
                     
                     
                     The collection includes books, journals, and media items. Journals, reference, and
                        reserve materials do not circulate and may be used only in the library.
                     
                     
                     The Lambuth Library's computer lab has 10 computers that are all linked to a printer.
                     
                     A copy machine is available for patron use. Library patrons must use their University
                        of Memphis ID card to pay for copies or purchase a copy card for that purpose.
                     
                     
                     There is a microform reader/printer available on the main floor of the library.
                     
                     Study rooms and the media/listening lab are located on the 2nd floor of the Lambuth
                        Library and can be reserved by students.
                     
                     
                     During regular business hours, library staff will be available to answer library-related
                        questions and to instruct patrons in using the catalog and other electronic resources.
                     
                     
                     Books in the Memphis campus library may be borrowed by Lambuth campus students through
                        Interlibrary Loan.
