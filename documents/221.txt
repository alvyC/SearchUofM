                     Welcome!
                     
                     
                     
                     Veterans Educational Benefits & Certification thanks you for your service to our country
                        and stands ready to assist you, your spouses, or your dependents in taking advantage
                        of your educational benefits while at the University of Memphis (UofM).
                     
                     
                     If you are a new student, we hope these pages provide you with the information you
                        need to choose UofM and to help you through your first term here. But whether you
                        are a new, continuing, or returning student, we hope the information on our site will
                        assist you in completing your educational program and receiving a degree.
