                     FAQs
                     
                     Applying to the University of Memphis Lambuth
                     
                     Students at UofM Lambuth are admitted to the University of Memphis at large and can
                        attend classes at any of UofM locations or online. A number of complete undergraduate
                        and graduate programs of study are offered at Lambuth and more programs are being
                        added. More information is available at these links:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        University of Memphis Admissions Office
                        
                        Academic programs offered at Lambuth
                        
                        Class offerings
                        
                     
                     
                     If you have additional questions about attending the University of Memphis Lambuth,
                        contact us at 731.427.4725 or 901.678.5087, or email us at lambuth@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Transferring to the University of Memphis Lambuth?
                     
                     To see if your credits will transfer to the University of Memphis, visit the transfer equivalency table section of the UofM Admissions website. If you have additional questions about transferring
                        to the UofM Lambuth, contact us at 731.427.4725 or 901.678.5087, or email us at lambuth@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Scheduling a visit
                     
                     Scheduling a visit to the University of Memphis Lambuth is easy. Call us at 731.427.4725
                        or sign up online . Campus tours are scheduled Monday - Friday at 1:30 pm (except holidays and University
                        closings). It is important that you sign up in advance. Groups of eight or more should
                        call 731.425.7982. Finding campus is easy—simply follow our Directions to UofM Lambuth.
                     
                     Housing and dining on campus
                     
                     Housing information for the UofM Lambuth is available at the campus housing page .  You may also call 731.425.1951 or 901.678.2295 for more information. Dining options
                        are available at the Follett Bookstore and Coffee Shop (located in the Wilder Student
                        Union building), which is open Monday – Thursday 8:00 am – 5:00 pm, and Friday 8:00
                        am - 3:30 pm. During spring and fall semesters, the Wilder Student Union BlueGrey
                        Café & Grill is open Monday - Thursday from 7:30 am – 6:00 pm and Friday from 7:30
                        am – 2:00 pm. If you have questions about dining services, please call 731.425.1976.
                     
                     
                     Next steps after being admitted
                     
                     First, you need to activate your University of Memphis email account by contacting
                        the Help Desk at 901.678.8888. Have your acceptance letter in hand when you call.
                     
                     
                     Next, first-time freshmen should register for New Student Orientation. Visit our Orientation website for details. Transfer students should contact us at 731.427.4725 to set up an appointment
                        with your advisor.
                     
                     
                     Finally, be sure that you have applied for financial aid. Visit the Financial Aid Office website for more information.
                     
                     
                     Purchasing books
                     
                     The UofM Lambuth Bookstore is located in the Wilder Student Union building. It is
                        open for regular hours Monday – Thursday 8:00 am – 5:00 pm, and Friday 8:00 am - 3:30
                        pm, CLOSED Saturday and Sunday. Their direct phone number is 731.425.1958. 
                     
                     
                     Testing Center
                     
                     Our Testing Center is located in Hyde Hall on the 3rd floor. Contact  Antoinette Smith  for details.
                     
                     
                     Getting a transcript
                     
                     To request a transcript from either the University of Memphis or Lambuth University,
                        visit http://www.memphis.edu/registrar/students/records/transcript-official.php .
                     
                     
                     Job opportunities
                     
                     Full- and part-time positions are posted at http://workforum.memphis.edu . Information regarding employee benefits is available at http://bf.memphis.edu/hr/benefits/prospective.php . Students interested in working on campus should visit the Student Employment website .
                     
                     
                     Conducting business with the University of Memphis
                     
                     Please visit the Procurement Services website and open the Vendor Information page .
                     
                     
                     Our location
                     
                     We are located on a 57-acre, tree-filled campus in Jackson, Tennessee. To learn more
                        about Jackson, visit the Chamber of Commerce website at http://jacksontn.com/ .
                     
                     
                     Campus safety
                     
                     UofM Lambuth is patrolled at all times by University of Memphis security personnel.
                        Click here for more information about Lambuth Campus Police Services .
