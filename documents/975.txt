                     Information for Prospective Employees
                     
                     The information contained here is to help you, as a prospective employee, get a brief
                        overview of what to expect as an employee of the University of Memphis.
                     
                     
                     A full-time regular work week is Monday through Friday, 7.5 hours per day for a total
                        of 37.5 hours per week. This is subject to change depending upon the job and department
                        for which you are applying. The hiring department will be able to explain any exceptions.
