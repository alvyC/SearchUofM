                     UofM Homecoming 2015 Will "Let the Good Times Roar"
                     
                     November 2, 2015 - The University of Memphis will "Let the Good Times Roar" during
                        Homecoming 2015. The schedule of events includes:
                     
                     
                     Class of 1965 Reunion & Golden Homecoming Reception - Wednesday, Nov. 4. All graduates
                        of 50 years or more are invited to Golden Homecoming Dinner honoring the Class of
                        '65. Guests may enjoy a complimentary bus tour of campus beginning at 2:30 p.m. followed
                        by a wine reception, dinner and induction ceremony at 5:30 p.m. The cost is $20 per
                        person. For more information, contact semiller@memphis.edu or 901.678.3043.
                     
                     
                     "Let the Good Times Roar" Homecoming Parade & Pep Rally, Friday, Nov. 6, 5:30 p.m.
                        on the UofM main campus. A pep rally will follow the parade at the Student Plaza.
                     
                     
                     True Blue Tailgate, Saturday, Nov. 7, 4 p.m., at Tiger Lane at Liberty Bowl Memorial
                        Stadium. The cost is $8 for alumni members and $10 for non-members and includes food
                        and drinks. For more information, contact alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.2586.
                     
                     
                     Memphis Tigers vs. Navy Midshipmen , Saturday Nov. 7, 6 p.m., Liberty Bowl Memorial
                        Stadium. Members of Pouncer's Pals are welcome to walk around the field with their
                        special guest before the game.
                     
                     
                     Contact: Gabrielle Maxey 901.678.2843 gmaxey@memphis.edu
