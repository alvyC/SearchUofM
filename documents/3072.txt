                     Memphis Healthy U
                     
                     The University of Memphis promotes healthy living through a healthy environment for
                        its students, faculty, staff, and visitors.
                     
                     
                     The Memphis Healthy U initiative is designed to encourage students, employees, and
                        visitors to eat healthier, move more, and become tobacco free.
                     
                     
                     We offer a plethora of FREE events encouraging you to move more, eat healthier, and be tobacco free through our Midday
                        Moves program, weekly nutrition seminars, Tiger Bike Share, and other initiatives.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Midday Moves occurs Monday-Thursday at 12:30p.m. on the UC Alumni Mall. The sessions last between
                           20-30 minutes and vary each day. *In case of inclement weather, the location may change
                           to the Campus Recreation Center. Check the Midday Moves website for the most up-to-date
                           information.
                        
                        
                        Nutrition seminars will be held each Friday in Mitchell Hall (MI), room 403, 11:45a.m.-12:20p.m. Visit
                           the Eat Healthier link for details on this semester's topics. You can view previous
                           semesters presentations, please visit the TigerFit website.
                        
                        
                        Tobacco cessation programs for students can be found by visiting Student Health Services , and for faculty and staff via Human Resources . Be sure you're familiar with the University's Limited-Use Tobacco policy .
