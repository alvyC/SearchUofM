                     Fall Seminar Series 2016
                     
                     The Department of Biology presents a series of lectures and seminars each fall and
                        spring semester. All seminars unless otherwise noted are held at 4:00 pm Thursdays
                        in Ellington Hall Auditorium (EH100) with a Reception at 3:30 pm in Ellington Hall
                        Foyer.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     9/1/16
                     
                     Dr. Laura Marek , Iowa State, “Genetic Resources for Agriculture: your favorite foods and why you
                        care about genetic diversity.” Host Dr. Jennifer Mandel
                     
                     9/8/16
                     
                     Dr. Ryan Garrick , Ole Miss, “Unexpected discoveries revealed by population genetics: case studies
                        from oceanic islands to montane forests.” Host Dr. Jennifer Mandel
                     
                     9/15/16
                     
                     Dr. Marie van der Merwe , “Nutritional interventions for the prevention of metabolic syndrome”. Host Dr. Judy Cole
                     
                     9/22/16
                     
                     Dr. Catherine Kaczorowski , UTHSC, “Systems Genetics of Cognitive Aging and Alzheimer’s Disease”. Host Dr. Ramin Homayouni
                     
                     9/29/16
                     
                     Dr. Sarah Liljegren , Ole Miss, “Blurred lines: Loss of organ boundaries prevents abscission in Arabidopsis flowers.” Host Dr. Jennifer Mandel
                     
                     10/6/16
                     
                     Dr. Hannah Henson , Union University, Jackson, TN, “The Zebrafish Eye: Insights into the mechanisms
                        of retinal development”. Host Dr. Chuck Lessman
                     
                     10/13/16
                     
                     Dr. Mary Ann Asson-Batres , Central Oregon Community College, “Retinoic Acid action in Postnatal Organ Systems”.
                        Host Dr. Andrew Liu
                     
                     10/20/16
                     
                     Dr. Santosh Kumar , UoM, TBA, Host Dr. Omar Skalli
                     
                     10/27/16
                     
                     Dr. Peter Belenky , Brown University, “Microbial Metabolism and Antibiotic Efficacy”. Host Dr. Tit-Yee Wong
                     
                     11/3/16
                     
                     Dr. Amber M. Smith , “Integrative Analysis of the Immune Response to Influenza-Pneumococal Coinfection”.
                        Host Dr. Bernie Daigle
                     
                     11/10/16
                     
                     Dr. Marko Radic , UTHSC, “Neutrophils: First Responders and Arsonists?”. Host Dr. Chuck Lessman
                     
                     11/17/16
                     
                     Hongbo Chi , St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, “mTOR signaling and metabolic control of
                        T cell fate decisions”. Host Dr. Andrew Liu
