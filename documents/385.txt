                     Graduating Students
                     
                     You are almost there. Won't be long now until you have that diploma in hand. Commencement
                        is an extremely exciting time. Here are some quick tips to remember to make the day
                        go smoothly:
                     
                     
                     What do I bring?
                     
                     
                        
                        Cap, Gown, Hood, Tassel
                        Pronouncer Card--
                     
                     REQUIRED MUST be with you
                     
                     
                        
                        At the event, use the number on your card to help your Faculty Marshals and Student
                           Marshals line you up.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Safety Pins (Hoods)
                        
                        Bobby Pins
                        
                     
                     
                     WHERE DO I GO? WHAT DO I DO?
                     
                     
                        
                        Arrive 1 hour prior to your Commencement.
                           
                           
                              
                              Take the elevator to the Event Level (EL).
                              
                              Locate your college/school sign.
                              
                              Your Faculty/Student Marshal will provide further instructions. 
                              
                              Use your pronouncer card to help your Marshal(s) line you up quickly. 
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        ONLY if you are an undergraduate listed as graduating with honors in the program,
                           ask your Faculty/Student Marshals for your honor cord (summa/gold; magna/crimson;
                           cum/white)
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Personal Items and Parking 
                     
                     For more information on personal items and parking: 
                     
                     
                        
                        Personal Items Leave personal belongings with family or friends. We cannot hold items and would not
                           suggest you leave them unattended. Please do not leave items in bathrooms, on the
                           ground or in your seat. We recommend wearing a dress or slacks with pockets.
                        
                        
                        If you prefer to bring small purse with you, we recommend bringing a purse with a
                           cross-strap that is flat enough to fit under your gown.
                        
                        
                        Parking You must bring cash to pay for parking. Parking is available on a first-come, first-served
                           basis in the FedExForum garage for $5.00
