			  Future Alumni of Memphis Future Alumni of Memphis Annual Membership: $20


First time FAM members receive a complimentary t-shirt*, a membership card and a Tiger Blue stole** to wear at Commencement. Don't forget to check out your other membership benefits including discounts on textbooks, Tiger apparel, and more!

Visit memphis.edu/thefam to learn more!

 

*After joining online you must stop by the Alumni Center, located at 635 Normal Street, to pick up your t-shirt. Your membership card will be mailed to you in 5-7 days, but if you need it before then, you can get one at the Alumni Center.
**FAM stoles are distributed at Cap & Gown pickup before your commencement.
