                     Take Your Seat
                     
                     If you love music get ready to sit up, sit back, sit proudly. 
                     
                     The curtain is about to rise on our beautiful new Music Center on Central Avenue. Celebrate
                        your love of music or surprise family and friends by naming a seat.
                     
                     
                     You're invited to Take Your Seat at the New Music Center.
                     
                     Name a seat today and become a permanent part of this new premier destination for
                        the cultural arts. Don't be left out.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Memorialize a loved one
                        
                        Present it as a graduation gift
                        
                        Celebrate your personal love for music
                        
                        Recognize the hard work of a current student
                        
                        Pay tribute to an inspirational faculty mentor
                        
                        Honor a family member, your entire family or a friend
                        
                     
                     
                     Select a Naming Opportunity Level
                     
                     $2,500 Level – includes a nameplate on a seat in orchestra or other premium section of the performance
                        hall. Donor's name will also be included on a special Donor Wall Recognition plaque
                        which will be prominently displayed in the new facility.
                     
                     
                     $1,000 Level – includes a nameplate on a seat in the performance hall.
                     
                     
                     All seats will be assigned based on the order in which the gifts are received. Seat
                        nameplates may be purchased using an installment plan of up to 4 years. Gifts made
                        to the New Music Center Seat Campaign are tax deductible.
                     
                     
                     Gift disclosures: Seat name recognition does not guarantee reservation of particular
                        seat for future performances. A five percent fee is assessed on gifts to support university
                        administrative costs.
                     
                     
                     Click HERE to Take Your Seat today!
                     
                     
                     If you are a UofM faculty or staff member and would like to purchase a Take Your Seat
                        nameplate using payroll deduction, please email gifts@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Note: The above Take Your Seat link will go to the seat inscription form. After hitting
                           Submit, you will immediately be redirected to the donation form to make your payment.
