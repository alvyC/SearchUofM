                     Advisory Board Members
                     
                     The International Studies Advisory Board is comprised of faculty, administrative staff,
                        and students from a range of departments central to research, instruction, and support
                        for the International Studies Program. The Board provides guidance on curriculum development
                        for the major and minor, and strategic direction for the International Studies Program.
                     
                     
                     All faculty that serve on the International Studies Advisory Board are also members
                        of the Faculty Affiliate Program .
