                     Graduate Admissions
                     The first 3 items should be sent to the following address:
                     
                     
                        
                        Office of Graduate School Admissions The University of Memphis 101 Wilder Tower Memphis, TN 38152-6627
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     University Application
                     
                     
                        
                        How To Apply - Read Admissions tips on applying
                        
                        
                        Apply Online - Select Graduate Applicants
                        
                        You will need to know your major and concentration to fill out the form (See SHS graduate
                           student application for details)
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Transcripts
                     
                     Request official transcripts from all colleges/universities attended be sent to Graduate
                        School Admissions. NOTE: University of Memphis graduates simply need to submit a copy
                        of their University of Memphis undergraduate transcript to the Academic Services Coordinator
                        (106 Fieldhouse).
                     
                     
                     Test Scores
                     
                     Request that official test scores (GRE, or GMAT; TOEFL for international students)
                        be sent to University of Memphis Graduate School Admissions.
                     
                     The next items should be sent to the following address:
                     
                     
                        
                        Graduate Coordinator School of Health Studies The University of Memphis 106 Elma Roane Fieldhouse Memphis, TN 38152-6627
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        A completed SHS Graduate Admission Form Â (which includes a Graduate Assistantship Application)
                        
                        Two letters of recommendation. At least one letter should be from a former professor
                           who can address the candidate's academic performance.
                        
                        Only University of Memphis graduates should submit a copy of undergraduate transcript
                           directly to the Graduate Coordinator.
                        
                        International Students - You must register for the international credit evaluation
                           through www.wes.org .
                        
                        
                     
                     NOTE: Once all materials are received by the Graduate School and by the SHS Graduate
                     Coordinator, SHS faculty members within the respective program of study will review
                     the completed application file and contact the candidate. Students interested in the graduate program in Clinical Nutrition should follow the
                     admission guidelines outlined here .
