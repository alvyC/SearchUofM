                     Current Students
                     
                     The Fogelman College of Business & Economics is committed to providing a stimulating
                        academic environment for its students. This includes developing opportunities that
                        will enhance all students’ college experience and learning.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Undergraduate Student Services Office Do you have questions about your academic coursework? Contact the Undergraduate Student
                           Services Office to get advised, request permits, apply for graduation, and more.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Majors and Minors Are you looking for more information about available majors and minors? Discover what
                           courses are best for you.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Course Syllabi Do you need information about your classes and assignment deadlines? Find your class
                           course syllabus.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Business Honors Program Are you a high performing student? Enhance your academic experience by participating
                           in the Business Honors Program.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Complete Professional Program   Are you looking for a way to gain an edge over the competition? Set yourself apart
                           by joining the Complete Professional Program offered through the Avron B. Fogelman
                           Professional Development Center.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Scholarships Are you looking for ways to alleviate the financial burden of college? Learn about
                           the scholarships offered by Fogelman College.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Financial Aid Do you need help paying for school? Apply for financial aid.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        FCBE Internships Are you looking to gain real world work experience? Contact the Complete Professional
                           Program office to submit your resume and learn about available internship opportunities.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Placements Do you need help planning your career? Contact Career Services for help.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Student Organizations   Do you want to get involved on campus? Join one of our many student associations.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Student Services Do you require extra assistance? Contact Student Services for help with tutoring,
                           advising, disability services, and more.
