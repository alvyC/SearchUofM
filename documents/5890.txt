                     How to Get Advised
                     
                     You should make an advising appointment as soon as possible after you receive notification
                        advising is available. Students are required to meet with an academic advisor each
                        semester before they can register. Please click here for appropriate dates.
                     
                     
                     You may schedule an advising appointment here " Make Your Appointment Online ". Please schedule your appointment early because we do not take walk-ins or make
                        same day appointments. We are open Monday - Friday: 8:00 am - 4:30 pm.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                                Make Your Appointment
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                             Make Your Appointment
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     At your advising appointment you can expect your academic advisor to answer any questions
                        you may have about your academic plan. Your advisor will suggest the best classes
                        to get you to your goal as quickly as possible. We expect you, as the student, to
                        be on time for your appointment and to be prepared with any questions that you may
                        have. Click here for an Advising Checklist you may find useful in planning for your advising appointment.
                     
                     
                     We also provide email advising. To request email advising, click here: " Request Email Advising ". Please include your Name , Major , U Number , Number of Courses you would like to take, and Names of Courses you are interested in taking. Please
                        note that email advising typically has a minimum 48 hour turnaround , which can vary depending on volume.
                     
                     
                     Join our Text Messaging service and never miss important events! Just text to: 703.596.8979,
                        message: @fcbead.
                     
                     
                     If you are an Honors student , Ms. Mary Johnson will serve as your advisor. You will schedule your appointment
                        with Mary.
                     
                     
                     If you are a Lambuth Management student, Dr. Kelly Mollica will serve as your advisor. Please contact her at 731.425.7960
                        or kmollica@memphis.edu to schedule your appointment.
                     
                     
                     We look forward to working with you as you follow your career path through our college!
