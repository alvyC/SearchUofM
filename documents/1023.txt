                     Admissions, Financial Aid & Orientation
                     
                     Admissions 
                     
                     Are you ready to apply to UofM Lambuth? Outstanding. You are about to be anything.
                        Do anything. Go anywhere. And we will take you there. It thrills us to have brilliant
                        students like you. We can't wait to get to know you better. Click here for the Office of Admissions .
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     UofM Lambuth Orientation
                     
                     Destination: UofM Lambuth! Congratulations on choosing this amazing college experience.
                        This university will shape you in ways you can't even being to imagine right now.
                        So, it's time to learn about all things UofM Lambuth. Get your Tiger pride going and
                        get ready for Orientation! Click HERE for more Orientation information .
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Lambuth Academy
                     
                     Lambuth Academy offers dual enrollment opportunities for area high school students
                        and home schooled students in the afternoons and evenings. Click here for additional information and to apply .

                     Financial Aid
                     
                     Nothing should stand between you and your academic aspirations. Nothing. That's why we offer many ways to offset the cost of college tuition. There
                        are tuition breaks through the 250-R Program, special grants and scholarships. Financial
                        aid packages are administered through the Office of Financial Aid , including federal, state, and institutional aid to qualified students.
