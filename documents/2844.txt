                     Faculty and Staff Giving
                     
                        
                     
                      
                     
                     Every member of the faculty and staff is important to the success of the University
                        of Memphis. As a campus community, we work together daily to make possible the dreams
                        of our students. There is an additional way we can contribute toward their success
                        and make a lasting impact on our University.
                     
                     
                     Our collective participation in the Faculty and Staff Giving Campaign to say "I'M
                        IN!" offers a tremendous opportunity to show our support for this great institution
                        and inspire confidence in other alumni, friends, parents, foundations, corporations
                        and donors – the lifeblood of our institution.
                     
                     
                     Say "I'M IN" Today! Visit the Employee tab on the myMemphis portal.
                     
                     
                     Already a Donor?
                     
                     Should you already carry the title of "Donor," thank you for your support! Please
                        know that your participation has made the University of Memphis what it is today and
                        will indeed make a lasting impact for the future.

                     Meet Your Department Champions
                     
                     Thank you to this year's Faculty and Staff Campaign Department Champions!
                     
                     Department Champions are chosen for the passion they have for the university. Champions
                        are responsible for sharing campaign news with colleagues, answering questions, encouraging
                        participation, creating awareness and being a voice for their department or college.
                        Champions are vital to the success of the Faculty and Staff Campaign and make an annual
                        gift personally as a True Blue Tiger.
                     
                     
                     Please consider serving as your Department's Faculty and Staff Campaign Champion to
                        help us ensure that every department on campus makes the commitment to say "I'm In"!
