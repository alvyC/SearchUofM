                     Clothing Resources
                     
                     
                        
                        Clio’s Closet
                        
                        
                           
                           This confidential resource provides non-perishable food items, toiletries, clothing
                              and even baby necessities and is open to all students in need.
                           
                           
                           Mitchell Hall 130
                           
                           Monday through Friday 9:00 am – 2:00 pm
                           
                        
                        
                        Good Will
                        
                        
                           
                           University Location: 574 S Highland St, Memphis, TN 38111
                           
                           901-458-6610
                           
                           Visit http://www.goodwill.org to find  a location nearest you
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Freecycle Memphis
                        
                        
                           
                           A place where you can post items you are giving away, respond to posts and request
                              specific items.
                           
                           
                           https://groups.freecycle.org/MemphisTNAreaFreecycle/posts/all
                           
                        
                        
                        MIFA Emergency
                        
                        
                           
                           Assistance with rent, utilities, food, clothing, and other necessities.
                           
                           910 Vance Ave, Memphis, TN 38126
                           
                           901-527-0208
                           
                           www.mifa.org/programs
                           
                        
                        
                        Riverside Missionary Baptist Church Community Services
                        
                        
                           
                           Provides a food pantry and clothes closet for persons with MIFA vouchers.
                           
                           3560 S Third St Memphis, TN 38109
                           
                           (901) 789-4570
                           
                           Hours:  Tuesday and Thursday 9:00-12:00
                           
                           Eligibility: Must be resident of 38109. Only one time per month.
                           
                           Documents to Bring: Photo ID, proof of residence, proof of income, or proof of public
                              assistance. Must have MIFA voucher.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        St. Anne’s Catholic Church
                        
                        
                           
                           Provides food pantry and clothes closet to those in need.
                           
                           706 S Highland St, Memphis, TN 38111
                           
                           (901) 323-3817
                           
                           Hours: Saturday 9:00-11:00
                           
                           
                              
                              Eligibility: Accepts walk-in residents of 38111, 38112, or 38114.
                              
                              Documents to Bring: Photo ID, proof of residence
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Serving in Christ Outreach Ministry
                        
                        
                           
                           Provides food pantry and clothes closet to those in need in Shelby County.
                           
                           2546 Deadrick St, Memphis, TN 38114
                           
                           (901) 290-2110
                           
                           Hours: Monday –Friday 9:00-4:00
                           
                           Eligibility: Must be a Shelby County resident. Accepts referrals from the American
                              Red Cross.
                           
                           
                           Documents to Bring: Photo ID, proof of public assistance, proof of income
                           
                        
                        
                        St. Jude Missionary Baptist Church
                        
                        
                           
                           Provides food pantry and clothes closet, plus monthly hot meal for anyone in need.
                              Persons can only visit pantry once per month.
                           
                           
                           835 E Trigg Ave, Memphis, TN 38106
                           
                           (901) 948-1489
                           
                           Hours: Clothes closet is the 4th Friday of the month from 3:30-4:30
                           
                           Eligibility: Must be a resident of 38106. Accepts MIFA vouchers.
                           
                           Documents to Bring: Proof of public assistance, proof of income no more than 150%
                              of federal poverty level (varies based on household size), proof of residence.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        The Generous House
                        
                        
                           
                           An food pantry and clothing closet where all items are free or sold at reduced prices
                              to those who qualify for assistance.
                           
                           
                           1790 N Germantown Pkwy, Cordova, TN 38016
                           
                           901-737-9006.
                           
                           Guests to Generous House may shop for food twice a month and clothing once a month.
                           
                           Hours: Mondays & Wednesdays 10:00-12:00
