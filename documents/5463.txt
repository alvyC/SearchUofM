                     Career Preparedness
                     
                     Career Services offers numerous resources to assist in your career search. Whether
                        you are deciding on a major, need help developing a résumé, or want to polish your
                        interview skills, they are here to help! Below you will find resources related to
                        all phases of the career development process:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Focus 2 - Utilize our online assessment tool to gauge your interests in relationship to academic
                              strengths, values, skills, personality and leisure.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Career Competencies Checklist - Make sure you are developing the competencies that employers seek, document them
                              on your resume, and provide specific examples of them during your interviews.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Finish in Four Academic and Career Plan - Take a look at an in-depth plan to finish your degree in four years and plan for
                              your future career along the way.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Resume and Cover Letter Writing - Tips and samples of how to best represent yourself to potential employers.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Interview Skills - Review our list of sources and other information to prepare you for the interview
                              process.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Professional Dress - Best practices when planning a professional wardrobe for the workplace.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           eRecruiting - Our Career Management Software allows employers to view student resumes and post
                              job or internship opportunities.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Contact Career Services  at (901) 678-2239 with questions, comments, or suggestions. If you reach them during
                        non-business hours, leave a message and they will contact you as soon as possible.
