                     Dining Dollars Program
                     
                     All full time undergraduate students participate in the mandatory Dining Dollars program.
                        If you are an undergraduate student, enrolled in 12 or more hours and at least one
                        of your classes is on campus, you will automatically have $300 in Dining Dollars charged
                        to your account each fall and spring semester. Dining Dollars is not charged in the
                        summer semester.
                     
                     
                     On the first day of classes in the fall and spring, you will be able to use your Campus
                        Card to purchase a meal or take a coffee break. Check out our dining and vending locations
                        tabs to see where you can next use your Dining Dollars. Log in to your account (click
                        Your Campus Card above) to see specifics about your account, including balance and transaction history,
                        meal plan status (if applicable), and Dining Dollars information.
                     
                     
                     Questions?
                     
                     Questions about dining dollars ? Please read the FAQs or e-mail us at campuscard@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     f you have questions about fee payment , please call the Bursar's Office at 901.678.5579.
                     
                     
                     If you have questions about financial aid , please call the Financial Aid Office at 901.678.4825.
                     
                     
                     If you have questions about room and board , please call Residence Life at 901.678.2295.
