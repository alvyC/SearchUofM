                     dr. yong yang to speak at the 27th Annual art & science of health promotion conference
                     
                     Dr. Yong Yang, Assistant Professor of Environmental Science, is an invited speaker
                        at the 27th Annual Art & Science of Health Promotion Conference on March 27-31, 2017
                        in Colorado Springs, Colorado. More information on this conference can be found by
                        clicking here .
                     
                     
                     dr. marian levy elected to american public health association executive board
                     
                     Marian Levy, DrPH, RD, FAND, Interim Associate Dean, was recently elected to the American
                        Public Health Association's executive board as a member of the Nominations Committee
                        at this year's 144th Annual Meeting in Denver, CO. She will serve a four-year term
                        of office (2016-2020).
                     
                     
                     sophas virtual fair
                     
                     The University of Memphis School of Public Health will be participating in the SOPHAS Virtual Fair on Wednesday, November 16, 2016. This unique virtual event will give prospecitve
                        students the opportunity to efficiently and conveniently meet admissions representatives
                        from around the country. There are currently 58 schools registered for the event,
                        and the University Of Memphis School Of Public Health is proud to be one of them.
                     
                     
                     Our own Dr. Marian Levy, Interim Associate Dean, and Dr. Vikki Nolan, Interim Assistant
                        Dean for Academic Affairs will be available during the virtual fair to answer any
                        questions about the School of Public Health, and the programs offered. Registration
                        for the virtual fair is free, and can be completed here .
                     
                     
                     Dr. Wilfried karmaus receives Nih r01 grant To study early prevention of lung disease
                     
                     Wilfried Karmaus, MD, Dr.med., MPH, has been awarded $3.2 million by the National
                        Institutes of Health to study the improvement of early prevention of asthma and chronic obstructive
                        pulmonary disease; please click here for details about this important research.

                     Faculty and doctoral students presented their research at the American Public Health
                        Association's 144th Annual meeting in Denver. CO. (Oct. 29-Nov. 2) Click here for a complete list of presentations. 
                     
                     
                      
                     
                      
                     
                      November calendar of events
                     
                     Wednesday, November 16th | 4:00pm-4:45pm | Robison 117 November Public Health Student Association Meeting This will be the final meeting for the Fall 2016 semester. There will be discussions
                        on past and future events, as well as the introduction of new officers. 
                     
                     
                      
                     
                      
                     
                     Thursday, November 17th | 11:00am-1:30pm | University Center Atrium The Great American Smokeout This is a nationwide event brought to campus by a collaboration between the PHSA and
                        College of Pharmacy from UTHSC. There will be an informational table about smoking
                        cessation. Please contact PHSA president Leah Reish for more information. 
                     
                      
                     
                     Every Wednesday in November | 11:00am-1:00pm | University Center Whitehouse Health Campus Challence Students will have the opportunity to get free, in-person assistance with insurance
                        enrollment at the University Center every Wednesday in November. Stop by the UC from
                        11-1, or book an appointment by visiting  here . Find a plan that fits your budget and needs, and all plans through Marketplace cover
                        routine preventive care including yearly check-ups, flu shots, and birth control.
