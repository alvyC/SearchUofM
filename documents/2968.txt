                     R
                     
                     Respondus LockDown Browser-Faculty
                     
                     A custom browser that locks down the testing environment within Desire2Learn (eCourseware).
                        When students use Respondus LockDown Browser they are unable to print, copy, go to
                        another URL, or access other applications. When an assessment is started, students
                        are locked into it until they submit it for grading. Available for both Windows and
                        Mac.
                     
                     
                     Respondus LockDown Browser-Student
                     
                     A custom browser that locks down the testing environment within Desire2Learn (eCourseware).
                        When students use Respondus LockDown Browser they are unable to print, copy, go to
                        another URL, or access other applications. When an assessment is started, students
                        are locked into it until they submit it for grading. Available for both Windows and
                        Mac.

                     U
                     
                     umApps 
                     
                     umApps provides easy, web-based access to a variety of specialized applications such
                        as SPSS, Mathematica, Microsoft Word, and many more. University students and faculty
                        can access the software from any PC or Mac, on and off campus.
                     
                     
                     umBlog
                     
                     umBlog also known as blogs.memphis.edu provides a place for users to share experiences, reflect on activities, and publish
                        student creative works through written posts, pictures, videos, links, and comments.
                        It belongs to you and you have the power to make it whatever you choose.
                     
                     
                     umDrive
                     
                     umDrive is a web-based file sharing service that gives you a personal place to store
                        papers, assignments, projects, and other files.
                     
                     
                     umMeet
                     
                     University sponsored web conferencing tool with Adobe Connect.
                     
                     umWiki
                     
                     University-supported wiki service for faculty, staff, and students.
