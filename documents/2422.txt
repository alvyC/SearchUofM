                     Foreign Language Fair
                     
                     Each year more than 1,000 high school students demonstrate their knowledge of foreign
                        languages and cultures at the Annual Foreign Language Fair at the University of Memphis.
                        The students come from the Memphis area to compete in a variety of contests, including
                        poetry recitation, writing, costumes, folk dance, drama, music, projects, crafts,
                        and a culture bowl. Competitive events will be conducted in French, German, Japanese,
                        Latin, and Spanish.The fair is sponsored by the University's Department of Foreign
                        Languages and Literatures. Read More
                     
                     Foreign Film Festivals
                     
                     The various language sections in the department regularly offer film festivals to
                        complement the academic programs we offer.  Information about the festivals will be
                        published on the events calendar and in the weekly departmental newsletter.
                     
                     
                     upcoming Lectures
                     
                     Information on lectures sponsored by the department or of interest to department members
                        will be included in the events calendar and in the weekly departmental newsletter.
