                     Management Research Colloquia
                     
                     Faculty and doctoral students in the Department of Management at the University of
                        Memphis are fortunate to have distinguished management scholars visit our department
                        as part of a research colloquium series. Examples of premier scholars who have visited
                        our department include Dr. Kim Cameron (Michigan), Dr. Jeff Edwards (UNC-Chapel Hill),
                        Dr. Rodger Griffeth (Ohio), Dr. Michael Hitt (Texas A&M), Dr. Ruth Kanfer (GA Tech),
                        Dr. Jeff Pfeffer (Stanford), Dr. Roy Suddaby (Alberta), Dr. Bob Vandenberg (Georgia),
                        and Dr. Herman Aguinis (Indiana). The schedule for our colloquium series is shown
                        below.
