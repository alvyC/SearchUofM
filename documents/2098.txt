                     The Loewenberg College of Nursing
                     
                     Welcome to the Loewenberg College of Nursing at University of Memphis! We offer learner-centered,
                        accessible, innovative, and high quality undergraduate and graduate programs that
                        are fully accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE) at both
                        Memphis and Lambuth campuses. Our distinguished faculty, motivated students, dedicated
                        staff, and community partners share a common goal of preparing healthcare leaders
                        and promoting health of the global community.
                     
                     
                     NEWS AND DEVELOPMENTS
                     
                     Welcome To Our Newest Faculty Member:  
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Welcome our new Assistant Dean for Nursing Students, Dr. Jennifer Hitt. An alumna
                        of the LCON, Dr. Hitt joined the faculty as the Assistant Dean of Nursing Students
                        on November 1st. In addition to receiving a BSN from the University of Memphis, Dr.
                        Hitt received a Bachelor's degree in Physics from the University of Mississippi, a
                        Master's of Science in Nursing from the University of Mississippi Medical Center,
                        and a PhD in Nursing from the University of Tennessee Health Science Center. Dr. Hitt
                        said, "I am excited to return to LCON. My career started here and I have always been
                        proud to call myself an alumna of this program."
                     
                     
                     Distinguished Lecture:
                     
                     
                     
                     Dr. Kenneth White, Associate Dean for Strategic partnerships and Innovation at the
                        School of Nursing, University of Virginia Delivered informative and inspirational
                        distinguished lecture, titled, " Palliative Care: Every Nurse Needs to Know. " Dr. White encourages every nurse to
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        clear space inside self to be fully present to patients and their families;
                        
                        continue learning about palliative care;
                        
                        grow comfortable with cultural humility, mindfulness, and resilience;
                        
                        explain the condition with patient with caring and compassion;
                        
                        ask about care goals;
                        
                        know what matters most; and
                        
                        be a role model.
