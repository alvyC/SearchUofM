                     Organization
                     
                     The Fogelman College of Business and Economics comprises seven academic units: School
                        of Accountancy; Department of Economics; Department of Finance, Insurance, and Real
                        Estate; Department of Management; Department of Marketing and Supply Chain Management;
                        and the Department of Management Information Systems.
                     
                     
                     These academic units offer a variety of majors and minors, the detailed requirements
                        for which are listed later in this section. The interdisciplinary International Business
                        Program centered in this college offers an undergraduate major in International Business;
                        requirements are listed in the section pertaining to academic department requirements.
                        In addition there are nine research units: Sparks Bureau of Business and Economic
                        Research, Center for Manpower Studies, Fed Ex Center for Supply Chain Management,
                        Center for Economic Education, Robert Wang Center for International Business, The
                        Methodist LeBonheur Center for Healthcare Economics, Systems Testing Excellence Program
                        (STEP), and the Center for Managing Emerging Technologies.
