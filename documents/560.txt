                     A hundred years down the road, you'll still remember Frosh Camp.
                     
                     Frosh Camp is a four-day, extended orientation program for incoming freshmen at the
                        University of Memphis.  Frosh Camp is a great way to meet new friends, learn about
                        getting involved at UofM, and get connected to campus before classes even begin!  Start
                        your Memphis experience on the right foot with Frosh Camp!
                     
                     
                     Let us tell you about this unforgettable experience
                     
                     After this Frosh Camp is over, you will have an upper hand on your first year at college!
                         You will learn what to expect both inside and outside the classroom, how to get connected
                        to a wide variety of activities and resources, and gain a whole bunch of Tiger spirit!
                         You will also be able to meet nearly 300 other incoming freshman as well as upperclassmen
                        mentors who will help you prepare for your first year!
                     
                     
                     At Frosh Camp you will meet people who will radically influence your college years
                        - classmates, friends, and supporters who will make and shape your experience at the
                        University of Memphis.  You will be as prepared as possible to tackle your first year
                        at Memphis, whether it's managing your academic schedule or knowing the Tiger Fight
                        Song for your first football game!
                     
                     
                     When is Frosh Camp?
                     
                     We offer three camp sessions:   Session I: June 26 - June 29, 2017 Session II: July 17 - July 20, 2016  Session III: August 7 - August 10, 2016
                     
                     
                     Remember spaces are limited and fill up quickly, so don't wait!
                     
                     Where is frosh camp?
                     
                     Frosh Camp takes place off campus at Camp NaCoMe. The NaCoMe Conference Center is
                        located between Pleasantville and Centerville in Tennessee. Please look at our logistics
                        page for more information.
                     
                     
                     What does Frosh Camp Cost?
                     
                     The total expense to attend camp is $85.00.  That's only $21.25 per day for something you will never forget! Campers receive transportation
                        to and from camp, lodging, delicious meals, all programming materials, social activities,
                        and a t-shirt.
                     
                     
                     For more information, please contact us !
