                     JOB FAIRS
                     
                     Students at the University of Memphis, Cecil C. Humphreys School of Law are annually
                        invited to a number of job fairs. See below for information on the most popular job
                        fairs and when they occur.
                     
                     
                     Damali Booker 1L Minority Job Fair
                     
                     
                        
                        When: Typically the first week of March
                        
                        Where: Nashville, Tennessee
                        
                        Who: Employers located in the southeast
                        
                        Registration deadline: Middle of January
                        
                     
                     
                     The National Black Prosecutor's Association
                     
                     
                        
                        When: Typically the middle of July
                        
                        Where: Orlando, Florida
                        
                        Who: Prosecutors at the local, state, and federal levels across the nation
                        
                        Registration deadline: Beginning of July
                        
                        More Info: www.blackprosecutors.org
                        
                     
                     
                     Southeastern Minority Job Fair
                     
                     
                        
                        When: Typically the last weekend in July or first weekend of August
                        
                        Where: Atlanta, Georgia
                        
                        Who: Employers located in the southeast
                        
                        Preregistration deadline: End of April
                        
                        Bidding closes: End of May
                        
                        More Info: http://www.semjf.org/
                        
                     
                     
                     The St. Louis Diversity Job Fair
                     
                     
                        
                        When: Typically the first weekend of August
                        
                        Where: St. Louis, Missouri
                        
                        Who: Employers located in the St. Louis region
                        
                        Preregistration deadline: End of May
                        
                        More Info: www.stldiversityjobfair.com
                        
                     
                     
                     Southern Region's Black Law Student Association's Job Fair
                     
                     
                        
                        When: Typically the last weekend of August
                        
                        Where: Varies
                        
                        Who: Employers from across the nation
                        
                        Registration deadline: Beginning of July
                        
                        More Info: http://www.nblsa.org/
