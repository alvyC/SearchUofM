                     Â GETTING READY FOR FALL...
                     
                     As the summer winds down, students should be preparing for the upcoming year. If your
                        student will be a freshmen, take some time to discuss what you both learned at Orientation
                        and how you can utilize this information to have a successful freshmen year. Help
                        your student look at fall classes and discuss a weekly schedule to help manage classes,
                        studying, work and social activities. For returning students, take time to help them assess the past year and prepare for
                        the coming year. Encourage them to think about participating in an internship or studying
                        abroad.
                     
                     
                     What parents can do...
                     
                     
                        
                        Discuss realistic expectations for academic success with your student.
                        
                        Advise your student to develop an academic plan for the semester.
                        
                        Encourage your student to stay in contact with friends made at Orientation-this will
                           help ease the transition during the first week of classes.

                     ESSENTIAL SERVICES 
                     
                     Bursar's Office
                     
                     
                        
                        Registration for Fall 2016 is in full swing! Students should check their accounts
                           online on TigerXpress (through the Account$ tab on the myMemphis portal) to ensure
                           that they do not have a hold on registration or grades due to outstanding debt.
                        
                        
                        Tuition and fees for the 2016-2017 academic year are being finalized by the Tennessee
                              Board of Regents and will be available on the Bursar website in early July. Students'
                              actual fees will be available through TigerXpress and eStatements for fall registrations
                              in late July . Emails will be sent to students and authorized users, notifying them when eStatements
                           are available.
                           
                           
                              
                              The Fall 2016 Fee Payment Deadline is August 19, 2016, by 4:30 p.m. Please visit our
                                    Fall Fee Payment Deadline Calendar for additional information.
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        The deadline to enroll in eRefunds (direct deposit) on TigerXpress for earliest refund of fall excess financial aid/scholarship
                           is August 15, 2016 . Excess financial aid/scholarships for fall will be direct deposited beginning August 19, 2016 . If your student is expecting to receive excess financial aid/scholarship, please
                           encourage them to enroll in eRefunds as soon as possible to prepare for any fall financial
                           aid/scholarship refunds/fee adjustments.
                        
                        
                        The Bookstore Advance Payment Plan (BAPP) is a program that will allow qualified students
                           to use their excess authorized financial aid at the University Bookstore to buy textbooks
                           and school supplies prior to the date that financial aid will be released to the students'
                           accounts. BAPP funds will be available for the Fall 2016 term beginning August 1,
                           2016 through August 12, 2016. Please visit the BAPP website for detail information on how students qualify for BAPP.
                        
                        
                        Your student can enroll you as an Authorized User to enable you to receive email notification
                           when invoices are available. If you are an authorized user, you can access your student's
                           account on TigerXpress to view/pay fees through the Parents'/Authorized Users' login.
                        
                        
                        For important fee payment deadlines and other important information, please visit our website.

                     Scholarship Office
                     
                     
                        
                        It's time for many students to complete the college scholarship application process
                           for the 2016 school year. With tuition rates rising and federal funding for colleges
                           and universities falling, many students find the initial costs of continuing their
                           education staggering.
                        
                        
                        If you are looking for scholarships, then our First South Financial Tiger Scholarship may be able to help!
                        
                        
                        This is a non-needs based scholarship that awards $1,000 towards tuition expense for
                           undergraduate students attending the University of Memphis in the 2016 academic year.
                        
                        
                        If you would like information about our scholarship, then please visit our website for more information. The scholarship application and guidelines are provided there.
                        
                        
                        The deadline to apply is Monday, August 1, 2016.
                        
                        If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at 901.380.7400, at
                           mail@firstsouth.com or you can call our Tiger Banking Center directly at 901.678.4040. You can also visit
                           our on-campus banking center located above the bookstore.
