                     Loewenberg School Ranked Among Best Online Graduate Nursing Programs
                     
                     The University of Memphis finished in the Top 100 for Best Online Graduate Nursing
                        Programs in the country in U.S. News & World Report's 2015 Best Online Program rankings, coming in at number 70. The rankings take into account student engagement, faculty credentials and training,
                        peer reputation, student services and technology, and admissions selectivity. "It is indeed great news that the Loewenberg School of Nursing has been nationally
                        recognized as one of the top 100 graduate nursing programs in the nation by U.S. News & World Report ," said Dean Lin Zhan. "Since 2003, our master of science in nursing program has provided
                        high quality online education to students pursuing a graduate degree in family nurse
                        practitioner, nursing education, nursing administration and executive leadership programs.
                        I commend our talented faculty, dedicated students and staff for their incredible
                        work to help us reach excellence. We are driven by excellence."
