                     Admissions
                     		
                     
                     Admission to the program is competitive, and students are encouraged to apply early
                        for full consideration.
                        To apply for graduate school, please view the University's graduate admissions regulations in addition to the prerequisites below. International students will need to view
                        additional information on 
                        international student admissions regulations and requirements .
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     Prerequisites for the MS Programs
                     
                     
                        	
                        GRE scores - an important factor in admission
                        	
                        Two letters of recommendation
                        
                        	
                        A TOEFL score meeting the requirements
                           of the graduate school (for students whose native language is not English)
                        
                        	
                        Undergraduate degree with minimum GPA of 2.5 on 4.0 scale
                        
                     
                     		
                     
                     Prerequisites for the PhD Programs
                     
                     
                        	
                        GRE scores - an important factor in admission
                        	
                        Three letters of recommendation
                        
                        	
                        A TOEFL score meeting the requirements
                           of the graduate school (for students whose native language is not English)
                        
                        	
                        Undergraduate degree with minimum GPA of 2.5 on 4.0 scale
                        
                     
                     		
                     		
                     
                     Letters of recommendation can be mailed to:
                        Ashley Holloway
                        	Department of Mathematical Sciences
                        	364 Dunn Hall
                        	The University of Memphis
                        	Memphis, TN 38152
                        	Email: anhollwy@memphis.edu
                        	 
                        For more general information about graduate admissions, please see the University's
                        page on  graduate admissions .
