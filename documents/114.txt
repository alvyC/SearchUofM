	   Congratulations on your recent graduation from the University of Memphis! As a new graduate, the Alumni Association is pleased to present you with a complimentary one year Gray membership. Your connection to the University does not end upon graduation. Participation in Alumni Association activities not only helps your  alma mater  grow stronger but makes your degree more valuable.

We hope you stay connected to your University by getting involved with some of the alumni clubs, chapters and programs based on academics, geography or a specific interest. Moving after graduation? Join a club in your area! Joining the  Young Alumni  is a perfect way to network with fellow alumni!  Click here  for a complete list of Alumni Clubs and Chapters. We hope to see you at an  event  soon!
Gray members receive limited benefits. Upgrade your membership by joining Young Alumni for only $25 to take advantage of the complete list ! Some that are particularly great for new grads include:

    Email365*
    Rec Center membership discount
    Discounts on textbooks (if you're returning to the UofM for grad school)
    Insurance Discounts
    UMAA Network : Take advantage of the member directory, view job postings, submit class notes and more

*The Email365 program will be changing in Fall 2016. Please stay tuned for additional details!

Connect with your Alumni Association and fellow Tigers on our  Facebook ,  Twitter  and  Instagram  pages.
And don't forget to  update your information  so you can stay up-to-date on news and upcoming events!
Questions about your membership? Contact the Alumni Association at  alumniassociation@memphis.edu  or 901.678.ALUM (2586).
