                     In the Now: November FAQs
                     
                     As the school year rolls on different situations and questions arise.  As a result,
                        our more frequently asked questions for each month will be posted below.  If you do
                        not see a topic covered, please review our  Frequently Asked Questions  page or feel free to e-mail us at  parents@memphis.edu  or at 901.678.2115.
                     
                     
                     Important Dates and Deadlines
                     
                     To stay up to date on all upcoming dates and deadlines, please check out our UMparent calendar . Follow us on Facebook and Twitter to receive daily updates.
                     
                     
                     November 1: 2nd Installment Fee Payment Deadline
                     
                     November 1: Daylight Savings Ends
                     
                     November 9-16: Spring 2014 Registration Begins
                     
                     November 11: Veteran's Day (Classes held)
                     
                     November 11: Undergraduate Intent to Graduate in May 2015 Form Deadline 
                     
                     November 25-29: Thanksgiving Break (No Classes; University Closed 27th-30th)
                     
                     December 2: Last Day of Classes
                     
                     
                     
                     Student Health Center
                     
                     During this time of year, students may experience allergies or illness. With their
                        student id, students will be seen free of charge by trained staff.  Encourage them
                        to take advantage of the services at Student Health Center .  For more information, click here .
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Career Services
                     
                     It is never too early for students to start thinking about employment after graduation. 
                        The Career Services office is available to help students gain an internship, review
                        resumes, and conduct mock interviews.  For more information, click here
                     
                     
                     
                     Enrollment Verification
                     
                     Do you need verification that you student is enrolled for medical insurance, car insurance,
                        or anything else? Students can request enrollment verification from the National Student Clearinghouse   or from the  Registrar’s office .  For complete details on how to request enrollment verification, please  click here .
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Student Newspaper: The Daily Helmsman
                     
                     Do you want to know what is going on on-campus from a student point of view?  Read
                        the student newspaper, The Daily Helmsman, by  clicking here . It is published Tuesday through Friday during the Fall and Spring semesters.
