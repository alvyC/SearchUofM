                     Tigers Vs. Ole Miss Football Game - Saturday, October 1, 2016
                     
                     Below is information and a link to the registration form for the road trip for University
                        of Memphis students to watch our Tigers play Ole Miss on October 1, 2016.
                     
                     
                     How much is going to cost me? Only $74.00
                     
                     
                     What is included in the fee? Transportation from the University of Memphis campus to Oxford, Mississippi and a
                        game ticket
                     
                     
                     How do I pay? Pay online at  https://secure.touchnet.com/C20227_ustores/web/store_main.jsp?STOREID=74&SINGLESTORE=true
                     
                     What about my ticket? Game ticket will be provided.
                     
                     
                     Can I take a guest? No, there are no guest tickets or guest transportation.
                     
                     
                     Registration/payment deadline: Monday, September 26, 2016, 12:00 pm (noon)
                     
                     
                     Refund policy: No refunds unless trip does not meet number of attendees required
                     
                     
                     Questions: Call 678-2188
                     
                     
                     Details about the road trip will be provided to students who have paid for the trip
                           via email no later than Thursday, September 29, 2016.
