                     Monthly Calendar
                     
                     September | October | November | December |
                     
                     
                     Every great city deserves a great University, and as the front door to research at
                        the University the FedEx Institute of Technology is committed to providing opportunities
                        to engage with technology innovation.  Through the Innovation in Action Series, Workshops,
                        Trainings, and Bootcamps offered at the FedEx Institute of Technology, we are committed
                        to serving as the hub of innovation in Memphis.
                     
                     
                     EMERGING INNOVATION SERIES
                     
                     September:  Memphis is a city on the cutting edge. Join us as City of Memphis CIO
                        speaks about smart cities and the future of technology in the city and what this means
                        to everything from infrastructure to tourism. Sign up here.  
                     
                     
                     Critical Skills Workshops
                     
                     Every month the FedEx Institute offers a free skills development workshop in a technology
                        that helps our community better engage with emerging technologies. These workshops
                        are designed to be short 2-4 hour introductions for beginners to the critical skills
                        needed to develop your technology expertise. 
                     
                     
                     October: Learn HTML/CSS, the building blocks of website design, with the Web Workers
                        User Group. Sign up here .
                     
                     
                     View upcoming workshops here.
                     
                     MemPHIS techNOLOGY FOUNDATION User Groups
                     
                     Every month we host the user group meetups for many of the Memphis Technology Foundation's
                        members. Currently meeting at the Institute every month: Gaming Development, Python,
                        R, and Machine Learning/Artifical Intelligence. Click here to learn more.
                     
                     
                     CAST CONVERSATIONS
                     
                     Thanks to the Cluster to Advance cyber Security & Testing, you can hear industry thought
                        leaders and experts discuss the constantly changing world of cyber security. Learn
                        about recent developments, and discover how important it is for large companies and
                        organizations to conduct research and adapt in order to keep their information safe.
                        CAST Conversations typically occur on the first Friday of the month.
                     
                     
                     Check out the other "Innovation in Action Series" , our events calendar for everything happening at the institute.
