                       Elizabeth McDonald, Head of Cataloging Department/Catalog Librarian Associate Professor emcdnld1@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-8237 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303J
                     
                     Responsibilities: Provides leadership in the development of cataloging and classification policies and
                        procedures; and makes available training opportunities. Creates original or adapts
                        complex cataloging records for monographic, serial, and microform formats; corrects
                        and upgrades cataloging and item records; assigns LC Classification numbers; serving
                        as an LC Rules Interpretation specialist; serves as the Library Liaison for the Civil
                        and Electrical Engineering Departments in the Libraries' Collection Development program
                        .
                     
                     
                     Research Interests: Serials cataloging issues.
                     
                     
                     John Barnett, Library Assistant III jbarnett@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-1536  Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs newly received monographic material from cataloging records created by the
                        Library of Congress; imports authority records; corrects and updates cataloging and
                        item records; transfers material between collections.
                     
                       Dr. Linde M. Brocato, Catalog Librarian Associate Professor lmbrcato@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-4288 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303J
                     
                     Responsibilities: Provides leadership in the development of cataloging and classification policies and
                        procedures; and makes available training opportunities. Creates original or adapts
                        complex cataloging records for monographic, serial, and microform formats; corrects
                        and upgrades cataloging and item records; assigns LC Classification numbers; serving
                        as an LC Rules Interpretation specialist; serves as the Library Liaison for the Civil
                        and Electrical Engineering Departments in the Libraries' Collection Development program
                        .
                     
                     
                     Research Interests: Late medieval Spanish literature and culture; comparative literature; the relationship
                        of visual and verbal texts; bibliography and codicology; book history; historical
                        and local constructions of gender and sexuality; knowledge organization and dissemination;
                        cataloging graphic novels and rare materials.
                     
                     
                     Connie Carlin, Library Clerk ccarlin@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2318 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Gloria Fitzgerald, Library Assistant II gftzgrld@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-1538 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs newly received added volumes of serial material from records created by
                        the Library of Congress and OCLC member libraries; imports authority records; corrects
                        and updates cataloging and item records; transfers material between collections; and
                        withdraws superceded material.
                     
                     
                      Lucille Freeman, Library Assistant II lfreeman@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-1569 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs newly received monographic material based on cataloging records created
                        by the Library of Congress and OCLC member libraries; imports authority records; corrects
                        and updates cataloging and item records; transfers material between collections; and
                        supervises student assistants.
                     
                        Terry D. Martin, Library Assistant II tdmartin@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-8245 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs newly received monographic material from cataloging records created by the
                        Library of Congress; imports authority records; corrects and updates cataloging and
                        item records; transfers material between collections; and serves as a Departmental
                        Service Provider for computer hardware and software issues. Serves on the WebTeam
                        useing computer language to maintain and update the webpages of the Cataloging Dept.
                        Making sure all links are appropriately connected to their designated websites.
                     
                     
                      Lee Louise Slack, Library Assistant III lslack@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2181 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs newly received music books, sheet music, compact discs, audiocassettes,
                        and phonodiscs from cataloging records created by the Library of Congress and OCLC
                        member libraries; corrects and updates cataloging and item records; imports and upgrades
                        authority records; and transfers material between collections
                     
                     
                      Cathy Swearengen, Library Assistant III cswearen@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-1567 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs monographic and serial material using complex electronic records from the
                        OCLC database; corrects and updates complex cataloging and item records; prepares
                        procedures for various cataloging tasks; plans use of cataloging software programs;
                        trains new departmental employees; and serves as a Departmental Service Provider for
                        computer hardware and software issues.
                     
                     
                      Joann Waddell, Library Assistant III jwaddell@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2106 Office: McWherter Library: RM 303
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities: Catalogs Pre-1976 government publications. Corrects and updates cataloging and item
                        records; transfers material between collections; and withdraws superceded material.
