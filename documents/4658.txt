                     ROOM DETAILS
                     
                     
                        
                        7,888 square feet
                        
                        Seats 500 in classroom style, 440 in table rounds, 820 in theater-style.
                        
                        Three sections that can be separated by airwalls.
                        
                        Lobby space for each section for registration area/vendors/catering.
                        
                        Full presentation capabilities for each section.
