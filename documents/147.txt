Get H{APP}Y! 
The new Alumni Association app brings your connection to UofM right to your smart phone! Receive updates, find an event near you and connect with our social media all in one place. Never miss out on savings at one of our benefit partners. Your membership card is now located in the app, along with easy access to discounts at hundreds of national retailers! 

features

    Exclusive content for current UMAA members including Mobile Membership Card 
    
    Tiger Benefits integrated to display discounts "near me" based on the your current location
    Pop-Up alerts to notify you of events near to you or important UofM Alumni updates
    Browse all UMAA events and add to your calendar
    Join or renew your UMAA or Guild of 1912 memberships
    
    Connect with UofM Alumni social media



Download the UofM Alumni App in the Apple AppStore or Google Play Store today! 


Install and Login
After downloading, you will be prompted to enter your First Name, Last Name and UID (ex: U00012345) to authenticate. If you do not know your UID, click here . For additional support, contact alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.2586.
If you have recently joined or renewed your membership, please allow 3-5 business days for your membership to process and your account to be updated on the app.
