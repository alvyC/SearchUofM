                     umMail - email (office 365)
                     
                     The University of Memphis uses Microsoft Office 365 (Outlook) as its standard, approved
                        email service. Outlook provides a clear, unified view of emails, calendars, and contacts
                        to keep your information and communication organized. Stay connected and go beyond
                        basic email tasks, Outlook allows you to get more done, such as, journaling and document
                        sharing.
                     
                     
                     Access umMail on a PC, Mac, or a Mobile Device.
                     
                     
                     Getting Started
                     
                     Use the Getting Started guide if you have questions on how to begin using your umMail account.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           To access your email, login using your UUID (uuid@memphis.edu) and your account password.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           If you have forgotten your account, or need additional assistance, please contact
                              the umTech Service Desk at 901.678-8888.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Forgot your password? Reset it using the self-service option at iAM .
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Once you've successfully accessed your umMail account, you can now sync your email
                        with your mobile device. Currently, you can use either an IOS, Android, or Blackberry
                        device. Please visit AskTom  for more details.
                     
                     
                     Also, now available: Office 365 for Education! It is an Office 365 plan that is available
                        for free to active students, faculty, and staff. This plan allows you to install Word,
                        Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, OneNote, Publisher, and Access on up to 5 PCs or Macs
                        and Office apps on other mobile devices including Windows tablets and iPads. Get your
                        free download at Get Office .
                     
                     
                     Additional Information
                     
                     You can find email FAQs on AskTom  ( PC , Mac , Mobile Devices , System Requirements )
                     
                     
                     Get help getting started in Outlook Web App for Office 365 at Microsoft Office Support .
