                     Emergency Housing Resources
                     
                     Students can experience situations that can foster immense hardships resulting in
                        a need for emergency housing.  By law, the University cannot provide free housing
                        on our campus. The following are community housing and shelters in the Memphis area:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Shelters
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Iris Hotel
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides a hotel room for one night; must be approved by owner or neighborhood watch
                                 liaison.
                              
                              
                              1950 Elvis Presley, Memphis, TN 38106
                              
                              901- 774-5906
                              
                              Contact: Owner or Neighborhood Watch Liaison
                              
                           
                           
                           Her Faith Ministries Housing
                           
                           
                              
                              Faith-based transitional housing that offers low-cost housing, food, clothing, transportation,
                                 and other assistance for women with children for 1-3 years and single women for 5
                                 months.
                              
                              
                              3396 Park Ave Memphis, TN 38111
                              
                              901- 324-3705
                              
                              Tuesday-Friday 10:00-6:00 (Spring/Summer); 9:00-5:00 (Fall/Winter)
                              
                              Documents to Bring: Photo ID, social security card, proof of income, birth certificates,
                                 proof of need
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           Memphis Union Mission
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides temporary lodging for single, homeless men ages 18 and older that includes
                                 meals, church services, and counseling
                              
                              
                              383 Poplar Ave, Memphis, TN 38105
                              
                              901-526-8403 (Main Office)
                              
                              901- 526-8434 (Shelter)
                              
                              Monday – Friday 8:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m.
                              
                           
                           
                           Missionaries of Charity
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides emergency shelter for single women and women with children, but clients must
                                 leave the premises during the day.
                              
                              
                              700 N Seventh St, Memphis, TN 38107
                              
                              901-526-5456
                              
                              Intake daily at 3:00 except Thursdays
                              
                           
                           
                           Alpha Omega Veterans Services
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides transitional and permanent supportive housing for homeless veterans with
                                 a dual diagnosis. 90-day program includes shelter, counseling, clothing, and meals.
                                 Participants can stay in a halfway house for 18 months.
                              
                              
                              1183 Madison Ave, Memphis, TN 38104
                              
                              901- 726-5066
                              
                               Monday – Friday 9:00-5:00
                              
                              Eligibility: Accepts referrals from the VA
                              
                           
                           
                           Homeless Services Catholic Charities of West Tennessee
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides supportive services for housing stability to veterans and their families,
                                 financial assistance and employment resources.
                              
                              
                              1325 Jefferson Ave, Memphis, TN 38104
                              
                              901-722-4748
                              
                              Monday – Thursday 8:45-3:00; Friday 8:45-12:30
                              
                           
                           
                           Calvary Rescue Mission
                           
                           
                              
                              Shelter that provides breakfast and dinner, clothing, mandatory chapel services. No
                                 time limit as long as fees are paid.
                              
                              
                              960 S Third St, Memphis, TN 38106
                              
                              901-775-2570
                              
                              Nightly check-in 4:00-7:00
                              
                              Men who are ages 21 and older. Room available for 44 men.
                              
                              Documents to Bring: Photo ID, social security number, or 3 other forms of identification
                              
                           
                           
                           Memphis / Shelby County Emergency Housing
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides 24-hour housing crisis hotline for homeless families with children and attempts
                                 to help place families in shelters.
                              
                              
                              379 Crossitt Place, Estival Place, Old MIFA Property, Memphis, TN 38126
                              
                              901-529-4545
                              
                              Documents to Bring: Photo ID, social security cards for all household members, birth
                                 certificates, school records, shot records, proof of address and pending eviction,
                                 proof of income, proof of loss or crisis
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           Living for Christ Co-Ed
                           
                           
                              
                              Provides dormitory room, three meals per day, and a locker. Clothing is also available
                              
                              3095 Thomas St, Memphis TN, 38127​​
                              
                              901- 791-4022
                              
                              http://lfcrestorationhouse.wix.com/shelter
                              
                              Check in from 2:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m.
                              
                              Rate $10/night and $3/day from 8:00 am to 2 pm. Nightly and day pass $13.00
                              
                           
                           
                           Additional Memphis Shelter Listings by zip code
                           
                           
                              
                              http://www.shelterlistings.org/city/memphis-tn.html
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Rent and Utility assistance
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        University Financial Aid One-Stop Shop
                        
                        
                           
                           Financial strain can be addressed at the University with one of our financial aid
                              employees that can complete an analysis of your financial aid package.  There may
                              be resources you haven’t yet tapped or loans that you can take out to finance your
                              education. Loans are available at any point in the semester and there is a listing
                              of alternative loans on the website.
                           
                           
                           103 Wilder Tower
                           
                           901-678-4825
                           
                           http://www.memphis.edu/financialaid/
                           
                           financialaid@memphis.edu
                           
                           Monday - Friday 8:00am - 4:30pm.
                           
                           MIFA Emergency
                           
                           
                              
                              Assistance with rent, utilities, food, clothing, and other necessities.
                              
                              910 Vance Ave, Memphis, TN 38126
                              
                              901-527-0208
                              
                              www.mifa.org/programs
                              
                           
                           
                           Neighborhood Christian Center Memphis
                           
                           
                              
                              Assistance with rent and utilities
                              
                              785 Jackson Ave Memphis, TN 38107
                              
                              901-881-6013
                              
                              www.ncclife.org
                              
                           
                           
                           Shelby County CSA
                           
                           
                              
                              Assistance with bill payment and rent for low income homes and provides up to $600
                                 until funds are exhausted.
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           3772 S Hickory Ridge Mall, #516 Memphis, TN 38115
                           
                           901-222-4315
                           
                           Monday – Friday 8:00 a.m.-4:30 p.m.
                           
                           Society of St. Vincent de Paul
                           
                           
                              
                              Work with up to a dozen local churches across the county to provide financial assistance
                                 for residents of Memphis.
                              
                              
                              3522 Carnes Memphis TN 38111
                              
                              901-722-4703 or 901-274-2137
                              
                              svdpmemphis1306@gmail.com

                              following payment and assistance programs, as well as extension policies.
