                     Collierville Center
                     
                     NEW! - Course Offerings (registration begins in November)
                     
                     
                     Click here to see -DRAFT Spring 2017 Course Schedule
                     
                     The University of Memphis is proud to announce its newest location, the Collierville
                        Center, conveniently located at the intersection of Poplar Avenue and Peterson Lake
                        in Collierville. This 27,000 square foot facility is outfitted with state of art technology.
                        All fourteen instructional rooms are smart classrooms with audio and visual equipment.
                        In addition to the two computer labs and student lounge, the Collierville Center provides
                        free tutoring, academic advising, and enrollment services!
                     
                     
                     Earn a Bachelor of Professional Studies in Organizational Leadership, Bachelor of
                        Liberal Studies, Master of Arts in Liberal Studies or Master of Professional Studies
                        in Collierville. Graduate and undergraduate classes in marketing, management, accounting,
                        education and many other disciplines are also available. The Collierville Center is
                        now the most convenient university option for Collierville and the surrounding area.
                     
                     
                     Classes are offered at times and in formats that meet the needs of all students. For
                        more details, call the Collierville Center at 901-678-5515.
