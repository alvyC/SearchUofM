                     ESPÂ Frequently Asked Questions
                     
                     Who can use the Educational Support Program?
                     
                     
                        
                        Any University of Memphis student, undergraduate or graduate level, currently enrolled
                           and taking courses at the UofM for which help is requested
                        
                        
                        Any student who wants to be better in a particular class for which he/she is enrolled
                        
                        Any student who needs help improving his/her study skills
                        
                        Any student who needs help with standardized test preparation
                        
                        Students can use any ESP Learning Center on main campus, in the residence halls, and
                           at the satellite locations
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     How do I find out the locations of the Learning Centers and what hours each center
                           is opened?
                     
                     
                        
                        Click on the Learning Centers link in the blue navigational bar on the left side of
                           this page
                        
                        
                        Call the ESP office at 901.678.2704
                        
                        Call the telephone number listed for each ESP Learning Center
                        
                     
                     
                     Is there a fee for tutoring? Tutoring is FREE! There are no tutoring fees for UofM students at our Learning Centers
                        or through our online 24/7 service.
                     
                     
                     Can I become a tutor? Yes!
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Call 901.678.2704, or come to Mitchell Hall 217, for information on the 15-hour tutor
                           training program to become an ESP-certified tutor
                        
                        
                        Watch for Training Schedule TBA
                        
                        Send an email to tutoringesp@memphis.edu to get on the email list for the next training class
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Can I use my scholarship hours as an ESP tutor? Yes, many scholarship students choose to do their service hours as tutors to satisfy
                        the requirements of their particular scholarship. For information, call 901.678.2704,
                        or come in to Mitchell Hall 217.
                     
                     
                     What if I'm taking a UofM online course? You can get help at the ESP Learning Centers. Call 901.678.2704, or come in to Mitchell
                        217.
