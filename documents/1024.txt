                           	Campus Life
                           	
                        
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     The best combination of serious and fun.
                     
                     Our students are always focused on their academics, but we have fun along the way.
                        Lots of it. Outside of the classroom, this intimate campus is a vibrant, fun place
                        to be—made up of diverse groups. You will live, play and connect in a classic college
                        environment, with Fraternity and Sorority Affairs, club sports, movies, cool concerts
                        and other special events tailored to appeal to  UofM Lambuth students. We keep campus
                        life interesting and exciting. Our students are worth it.

                     Looking to keep up with what's going at Lambuth Campus?  Stay informed by viewing
                        the Lambuth Student News !
                     
                     
                     Looking for ways to get involved on campus, meet other students, and gain leadership
                        experience? Join a student organization or attend an event on campus! Click on the
                        links below to learn more.

                        at UML_SS, and Twitter at UMLambuthSS.
                     
                     
                     Campus Recreation Intramural Services - Wellness, Health & Fitness Building hours are Monday - Thursday, 12:00 pm - 8:00
                        pm, and Friday, 12:00 pm - 6:00 pm for recreational use. Pool hours are Monday - Thursday,
                        3:00 pm - 7:00 pm, and Friday, 3:00 pm - 5:30 pm.  Fitness Center, Wilder Student
                        Union, Room 3, hours are Monday - Thursday, 12:00 pm - 8:00 pm, and Friday, 12:00
                        pm - 6:00 pm. For questions or rental information, call Alejandro Herrera, Supervisor,
                        Campus Recreation, 731.425.7328 or email Alejandro Herrera . Click HERE for more information on CRIS services at University of Memphis Lambuth.
                     
                     
                     SGA Travel Funding - Undergraduate and Graduate student travel funds are available to Lambuth campus
                        students! If you would like to attend an academic or leadership conference, you may
                        be eligible for funding here ! Applications should be submitted to Beth Ann Simpson at ecarkuff@memphis.edu or to the Office of Student Services in Wilder College Union 2 (lower level). Please
                        pay attention to the deadlines listed on the application.
