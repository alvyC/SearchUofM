GET INVOLVED!
The Lambuth Alumni Chapter is beginning a new season of projects and events to involve Lambuth alumni! If you graduated from Lambuth University and are interested in being a part of this exciting time, please contact Kristie Goldsmith at k.goldsmith@memphis.edu or 901.678.1782. A current project of the Chapter is the implementation of a Heritage Room in the library of the Lambuth campus. This room will hold various objects from the college's rich history and tradition.
