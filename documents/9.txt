All UofM graduates, former or current students, friends, and loyal Tiger fans are
welcome to join our Tiger Family! Show that you're
True Blue and JOIN TODAY !

Joining is easy!
Choose a membership option below to join online today:

    Join as an annual member
    Upgrade to Life Member
    Current Students - Join the FAM
    Kids - Join Pouncer's Pals
    Join the University of Memphis Association of Retirees (UMAR)
    Give the gift of membership - Contact us at 901.678.2586
    


Membership has its privileges!
The University of Memphis Alumni Association offers hundreds of membership perks, both in the Memphis-area and nationally. Discounts
are available at Tiger Bookstore, Campus Rec Center and at hundreds of retailers online! In addition, members will be able to take advantage of the "Email365"
program and access your @memphis.edu email account. Click here to learn more about Alumni Association membership benefits.

*Membership is tax deductible! Consult your tax advisor.
Every membership includes a contribution to the UofM Alumni
Association Scholarship Endowment. Thanks to our members, the Association awards $12,000 in scholarships each year. Click here to learn more about available scholarships.

    Blue: $50
    Young Alumni (up to 5 years after graduation): $25
    Faculty/Staff Annual Membership: $20



First time annual members receive a complimentary membership package. The box includes a car decal, a USB drive and a commemorative booklet.

Upgrade to Life Member

    Single Life Membership: $750*

*Single Life Membership Installments: Pay $100 first payment and $70 in monthly installments over 10 months (This includes
an additional premium of $50)

Join over 1,400 Tigers For Life whose life membership clearly defines them as True
Blue. 



Life members receive a complimentary membership package. The box includes
a commemorative booklet, membership card, pen, Life Member lapel pin and car decal.
Lifetime members play a critical role in shaping the future of our University. When you become a life member, your
membership dues support the UofM Alumni Scholarship Fund. Through this
fund, life members have helped ensure that promising students have the resources they need to complete their education.

Click here to learn about additional membership benefits.

Pouncer's Pals

    Single Annual Membership: $30

Pouncer's Pals is open to all children under the age of 12. Members will receive a
t-shirt, a certificate, an autographed photo of Pouncer, quarterly mailings and access
to Pouncer's Pals events.

Click here to learn more about Pouncer's Pals and upcoming events.

University of Memphis Association of Retirees

    Single Annual Membership: $25
    Joint Annual Membership: $35
    Single Life Membership: $200
    Joint Life Membership: $350

UMAR is an active organization  of retired professors, administrators and staff. The
group conducts a variety of activities including luncheons, dinners, tours and trips,
as well as helping to coordinate various volunteer activities within the University.
UMAR is a constituent organization of the Alumni Association, and its members are
entitled to the same benefits as all Alumni Association members.

Click here to learn more about UMAR and upcoming events.

JOIN NOW!
