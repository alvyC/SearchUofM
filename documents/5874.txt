smart grid Workshop on SMART GRID
Organizers:

	Dr. Thomas L. Pigg , Jackson State Community College, Tennessee
	Robert Hamilton , Information Security Specialist, Oklahoma Department of Career and Technology Education
	Dr. Hasan Ali , Department of Computer Engineering, The University of Memphis



Date: October 17, 2014 Time: 1pm - 5pm 
Location: FedEx Institute of Technology (Fishbowl) - 2nd Floor 


This Pre-Cyber Expo workshop will focus on the smart grid concept, communication delay issues, threats directed toward critical infrastructures, the vulnerabilities these infrastructures present and potential defense mechanisms that may be deployed to tackle potential attacks. The proposed workshop would meet at the FedEx Institute of Technology (FIT--Fishbowl), the University of Memphis from 1:00 PM until 5:00 PM. The workshop will include discussions, demonstrations, and poster presentations. The workshop will be conducted jointly by the Center for Information Assurance and Tennessee Cyber Security Education Consortium. 


Tentative Agenda:
1:00 - 1:10 - Welcome and Introductions 
1:10 - 1:30 - Smart Grid Overview - Dr. Hasan Ali 
1:30 - 2:00 - Infrastructure Resilience Using Cyber-Physical Game-Theoretic Approach - Dr.Nageswara S. V. Rao
2:00 - 2:30 - Wireless Sensors For Substation Monitoring - Dr. Shubhalaxmi Kher
2:30 - 3:00 - Presentation (TBA) 
3:00 - 3:15 - Break 
3:15 - 3:40 - Control Device Security Overview - Dr. Thomas L.  Pigg 
3:40 - 4:30 - Smart Grid Threats and Vulnerabilities - Robert Hamilton 
4:30 - 5:00 - Panel Discussion and Q & A - (Panelists): Dr. N. Rao, Dr. H. Ali, R. Hamilton - Dr. T. Pigg, Moderator 
5:00 - Workshop Ends
