                     Emergency Mental Health Services
                     
                     The University Counseling Center offers free counseling if you are experiencing any
                        kind of emotional, psychological, or interpersonal problems. There is a counselor
                        available to talk with you 24 hours a day at 901.678.4357 (HELP).  Alternatively,
                        you can contact the National Crisis Center 24-Hour Hotline: 1.800.273.TALK. The following
                        are additional resources available to you on campus and in the community:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Counseling Services
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        The University of Memphis Counseling Center
                        
                        
                           
                           214 Wilder Tower
                           
                           Counseling Center Office Number:901-678-2068
                           
                           Monday - Thursday: 8:00 am - 7:00 pm, Friday: 8:00 am - 4:30 pm.
                           
                           Note: Students will be seen by a counseling staff member between 10:00 am and 2:00
                              pm on weekdays.
                           
                           
                           Email: counseling@memphis.edu
                           
                        
                        
                        The Relaxation Zone
                        
                        
                           
                           Feeling stressed or need somewhere to relax? The Relaxation zone has massage chairs
                              and biofeedback equipment can help to calm and focus you
                           
                           
                           211 Wilder Tower
                           
                           Hours: Monday-Friday 9:00 a.m.-4:00 p.m.
                           
                        
                        
                        Stress Busters
                        
                        
                           
                           6-week series for students to learn to manage stress and increase calmness, concentration,
                              and productivity. 
                           
                           
                           214 Wilder Tower
                           
                           Stressbuster groups meet Tuesday (9am), Thursday (11am), or Friday (3pm).
                           
                        
                        
                        Purposeful Mindfulness
                        
                        
                           
                           The practice of cultivating awareness of one’s thoughts, feelings, and bodily sensations
                              in the present moment. People who are mindful experience a greater sense of happiness,
                              self-esteem, empathy, and acceptance.
                           
                           
                           214 Wilder Tower
                           
                           This group meets every Wednesday from 1:00pm-2:00pm
                           
                            Contact: Dr. Umieca N. Hankton (nhankton@memphis.edu). 
                           
                        
                        
                        Department of Psychology
                        
                        
                           
                           At the Psychological Services Center, general outpatient psychotherapeutic and psychological
                              assessment services are provided to the individuals and families of the greater Memphis
                              metropolitan area for a sliding scale fee.
                           
                           
                           Psychology Building Room 126,
                           
                           901-678-2147
                           
                           9 a.m. to 8 p.m. Monday through Thursday and from 9 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. on Friday.
                           
                        
                        
                        Alliance Healthcare Services
                        
                        
                           
                           Alliance Healthcare Services is available to serve anyone in need of mental health
                              care service
                           
                           
                           (901) 369-1410
                           
                           http://www.alliance-hs.org/
                           
                           Visit the website for location for services nearest you
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Domestic Violence
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Family Safety Center
                        
                        
                           
                           The Family Safety Center is one location that effectively combines civil, criminal,
                              health and social services for victims of family violence.
                           
                           
                           http://familysafetycenter.org/
                           
                           1750 Madison Ave, Suite 600, Memphis, TN 38104
                           
                           (901) 222-4400 (also a 24 hour crisis hotline)
                           
                        
                        
                        Rape Crisis Center
                        
                        
                           
                           Provides free and confidential, comprehensive forensic nursing, advocacy and counseling
                              services regardless of whether or not the victim is reporting to law enforcement.
                           
                           
                           1750 Madison Ave., Ste. 102 Memphis, TN  38104
                           
                           (901) 222-4350
                           
                           https://www.shelbycountytn.gov/index.aspx?NID=737
                           
                        
                        
                        YWCA Abused Women's Services
                        
                        
                           
                           Services: Offers shelter, hotline, and court advocacy to abused women and their children.
                              Also offers community education regarding domestic violence.
                           
                           
                           www.memphisywca.org/abused-women-services
                           
                           766 S. Highland St, Memphis, TN 38111
                           
                           (901) 725-4277 (24-Hour Hotline)
                           
                           (901) 458-2550 (Spanish Hotline)
                           
                           Eligibility: Women who are victims of abuse and their children under 12 years of age.
                           
                           Documents to Bring: Some form of ID
