	   Announcing the NEW Tiger Benefits Program

Exclusive Savings Program for Alumni Association Members only
Our new Tiger Benefits Program can save Alumni Association members up to 50 percent off of top
brands, retailers, restaurants, entertainment and recreation – in your
area and nationally. Simply search by zip code or by type of discount
online or on the mobile app and start saving immediately.

Merchants are continually being added to the list, so check back
frequently. A few of the most well-known merchants include: Papa
Murphy’s, Lenny's, Walt Disney World, Busch Gardens, Jiffy Lube and
many more. 

To begin using the Tiger Perks Program, register online following these steps:

    Visit memphisalumni.accessdevelopment.com
    Members: Learn more and redeem the promo code here (must be logged in to the UMAA Network)
    After you create your account, you can download the free mobile app by searching "My Deals" in the Apple or Google Play stores
    



Questions or problems? Contact the Alumni Association at alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.2586.
