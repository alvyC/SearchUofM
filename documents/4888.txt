                     Software discounts for students.
                     
                     University Software 
                     
                     umApps
                     
                     Use specialized software, including Mathematica, SPSS, and many more, on your own
                        computer. Software can be accessed on and off campus using any PC and Mac.  Your work
                        is saved and printed on your own desktop and printer.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Discounts for Software Purchases
                     
                     The University of Memphis has negotiated with several software vendors to provide
                        special pricing for personal purchases by students in the following programs:
                     
                     
                     General Software
                     
                     Purchase popular software packages at unbelievably low prices online. All University
                        of Memphis students are able to purchase discounted software for use on their personal
                        computer.
                     
                     
                     ENTER THE GENERAL DISCOUNT SOFTWARE STORE
                     
                     
                     
                     Adobe Products
                     
                     Adobe products are available to be purchased by students at deeply discounted prices!
                        This opportunity is for personal sales only and are to be used on personal computers.
                     
                     
                     ENTER THE ADOBE STORE
                     
                     Apple Products
                     
                     Software associated with Apple is available for purchase by students at discounted
                        prices. This is only available for personal sales and for use only on personal computers.
                     
                     
                     ENTER THE APPLE STORE FOR EDUCATION
