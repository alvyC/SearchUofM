                        Full-Time Faculty
                    
                    
                      Department
                        Computer Science
                    
                    
                      Position Summary
                        The Department of Computer Science at the University of Memphis is seeking candidates for Assistant Professor position beginning Fall 2017.  Exceptionally qualified candidates in all areas of computer science are invited while candidates with core expertise in systems, data science, security & privacy, and software engineering and an interest in emerging and interdisciplinary applications such as smart health, smart vehicles, smart transportation, smart energy, and CS education are particularly encouraged to apply.  The successful candidate is expected to develop externally sponsored research programs, teach both undergraduate and graduate courses and provide academic advising to students at all levels.
                    
                    
                      Minimum Position Qualifications
                        Applicants should hold a PhD in Computer Science, or related discipline, and be committed to excellence in both research and teaching.
                    
                    
                      Special Conditions
                        For full consideration, application material should be uploaded by December 1, 2016.  Screening of applications will begin December 1, and may continue until the position is filled.
                    
                    
                      Posting Date
                        10/25/2016
                    
                    
                      Closing Date
                        12/01/2016
                    
                    
                      Open Until Screening Begins
                        No
                    
                    
                      Hiring Range
                        Salary is highly competitive and dependent upon qualifications.
                    
                    
                      Full-Time/Part-Time
                        Full-Time: Benefits Eligible
                    
                    
                      Special Instructions to Applicants
                        To apply, please visit https://workforum.memphis.edu/.  Include a cover letter, curriculum vitae, statement of teaching philosophy, research statement, and a list of three references.  Direct all inquiries to Kendra Tillis (ktillis@memphis.edu).
                    
                    
                      Instructions to Applicant regarding references
                        Provide the name and email of three reference providers. An electronic notification will be sent to the reference provider to directly upload their reference letter to the system.
                    
                    
                      Is this posting for UofM employees only?
                        No
