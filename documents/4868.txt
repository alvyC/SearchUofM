                     Student Worker Opportunities
                     
                     The ITS Service Desk provides support to all faculty, staff, and students. It is comprised
                           of the call center, Center for Teaching and Learning, and Smart Technology Services.
                           We also manage and staff computer labs at the McWherter Library, University TechHub,
                           and Lambuth campus. If you are interested in joining our team of enthusiastic student
                           workers, please see our job postings below and fill out an application.
                     
                     IMPORTANT:  Only applications submitted by currently enrolled UofM students will be accepted.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              Position:
                              
                               
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Technical Assistant
                                 
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              Description:
                              
                               
                              
                              Technical Assistants are responsible for providing our clients with 24/7 Tier 1 technical
                                    support and customer service. A technical assistant will assist clients over the phone;
                                    document and submit support tickets; as well as monitor and maintain our computer
                                    labs.  
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                               
                              
                               
                              
                              Apply
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              Position:
                              
                               
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Smart Technology Assistant
                                 
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              Description:
                              
                               
                              
                              Smart Technology Assistants provide support to faculty and staff with hardware related
                                    issues in our Smart Classrooms, including projectors, smart carts, and other audio/visual
                                    equipment.
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                               
                              
                               
                              
                              Apply
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              Position:
                              
                               
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Shift Lead
                                 
                              
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              Description:
                              
                               
                              
                              Ensures clients are receiving the required level of customer service and support from
                                    all Technical Assistants. Additionally, responsible for assisting the Team Lead and
                                    full time staff with developing, training, rewarding, coaching, and counseling trainees.
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                               
                              
                               
                              
                              Apply
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Position :
                                 
                              
                              
                               
                              
                              Sandbox Assistant
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Description :
                                 
                              
                              
                               
                              
                              Sandbox Assistants are stationed in the Technology Sandbox in the McWherter Library.
                                    They assist users with a variety of new and emerging technologies including 3D printers,
                                    GIS software, and CAD programs.
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                               
                              
                               
                              
                              Apply
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Position :
                                 
                              
                              
                               
                              
                              Team Leader
                              
                           
                           
                           
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Description :
                                 
                              
                              
                               
                              
                              
                                 
                                 Team Leaders ensure clients are receiving the required level of customer service and
                                       support from all Technical Assistants. They are responsible for assisting the full
                                       time staff with developing and coaching Technical Assistants and Shift Leads. 
                                 
                                 To be considered for this position, you must be a current Technical Assistant.
