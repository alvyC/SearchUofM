                     The School of Urban Affairs and Public Policy (SUAPP) brings together the Departments
                        of City & Regional Planning, Criminology & Criminal Justice, Public & Nonprofit Administration,
                        and Social Work in order to maximize our efforts in research, education, and engaged
                        scholarship.
