                     Diploma Mailing Address
                     
                     Within the Third Step of the Four-Steps to Graduate process, you will be asked to provide your diploma mailing address. This address will
                        be used 2-3 months after graduation, so please contact us if you need to update the
                        form to a new address before this time. 
                     
                     
                     Diploma mailing process
                     
                     
                        
                        Diplomas are mailed 2-3 months after Commencement day.
                        
                        Doctoral diplomas will be handed to participating graduates on Commencement day. Any
                           others will be mailed following Commencement.
                        
                        
                        If you are unable to use the link, please contact Commencement with your Unumber,
                           full name, and complete address.
