	   Welcome to the FAM!
The Future Alumni of Memphis, aka the FAM , is the student-based extension of the University of Memphis Alumni Association.
The FAM provides services for current University of Memphis students that will aid in developing
stronger connections with the institution as well as with its alumni. 

All current undergraduate and graduate students can be a member of the FAM ! YOU are what makes the University of Memphis a great place and why we are proud
to be part of the Tiger family! So why shouldn’t you become part of the FAM and experience all of the great services and benefits offered to our alumni?

FAM Membership 

Cost: $20 per year

Includes:

    DISCOUNTS at great places, such as merchandise and textbook discounts at Tiger Bookstore, local restaurants, online shopping and more!
    
    Official FAM t-shirt!
    Tiger Blue Stole to wear at your Commencement

How to Join:

    Join online here
    Stop by the Alumni Center, located at 635 Normal Street

*After joining online you must stop by the Alumni Center, located at 635 Normal Street,
to pick up your t-shirt. Your membership card will be mailed to you in 5-7 days, but
if you need it before then, you can get one at the Alumni Center.
**FAM stoles are distributed at Cap & Gown pickup before your commencement.
