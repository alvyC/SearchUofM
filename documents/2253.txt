                     Departmental Seminar Schedule - Fall 2016
                     
                     Our weekly Departmental Seminar series occurs every Friday afternoon (excluding holidays,
                        cancellations, etc.) The seminar is located in Smith Chemistry Room 102. Seminar begins
                        at 1:30 PM with refreshments, coffee, and opportunities to chat with the seminar speaker
                        and fellow members of the Department. At 2:00 PM, the Seminar begins and typically
                        lasts 50 minutes + 5-10 minutes of Q&A. Please join us!
                     
                     
                     Please contact William Alexander , Seminar Coordinator, for requests for accommodations, general information, or if
                        you would like to present in our seminar series.
