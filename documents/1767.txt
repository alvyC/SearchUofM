                        Faculty Staff FREE Night at the Theatre
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     The Department of Theatre & Dance is very excited about all of the exciting things
                        happening in our building and across campus in other departments. We would like to
                        honor the hard work and dedication all U of M faculty and staff by offering a U of
                        M Faculty and Staff FREE Night at the theatre. So, take a break and join us the first
                        Friday of each Mainstage production.
                     
                     
                     Faculty and Staff FREE Nights at the Theatre for the 2016-2017 Season
                     
                     The Little Shop of Horrors : Friday, September 23 at 7:30pm Measure for Measure: Friday, November 4 at 7:30pm Anon(ymous): Friday, February 17 at 7:30pm Spring Awakening: Friday, April 14 at 7:30pm
                     
                     
                     How to get your 2 FREE tickets any Faculty and Staff FREE Night
                     
                     You may reserve your tickets by calling the box office 678-2576 or by ordering them
                        online with code sent via email the week of the performance. To pick tickets up, please
                        bring your faculty/staff id to the box office located in the Theatre Building on Central
                        Ave. All tickets are available on a first come, first served basis.
                     
                     
                     The fall 2016 box office hours are...
                     
                     Monday 9:00am  to 4:00pm Tuesday 9:00am to 10:30am and 12:00 to 4:00pm Wednesday 10:00am  to 4:00pm Thursday 9:00am to 10:30am and 12:00 to 4:00pm Friday 9:00am to 2:00pm The box office opens at 6:30 on performance nights. We ask that you pick up your tickets by 7:00pm on the night of the performance. If you have any questions, please call Alice Berry at 678-3184.
