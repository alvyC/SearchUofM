                                 Zachary Carr Leadership Programs Advisor Student Leadership and Involvement 211 University Center 901.678.8679 zcarr@memphis.edu
                                 
                                 As the Leadership Programs Advisor, Zachary works primarily with all Leadership Programs
                                    including the Tiger Leadership Institute, Professional Connections, and the Lunch
                                    with a Leader Speaker Series. Zachary is a native South Mississippian growing up just
                                    thirty miles from New Orleans Louisiana. He is a diehard New Orleans Saints fan and
                                    lover of all things New Orleans. Zachary earned his B.A. in Communications from the
                                    University of Southern Mississippi and his M.A. from LSU in Higher Education Administration.
                                    He is also a proud brother and alumni of the Thea Alpha Chapter of Pi Kappa Phi.

                                 Michael Marino Senior Coordinator Leadership Programs 211 University Center 901.678.8679 mdmarino@memphis.edu
                                 
                                 Michael is the Fraternity/Sorority Affairs Advisor; in this role he advises the individual
                                    chapters on all three of our councils and supports community-wide programming and
                                    initiatives. Michael is a proud alumnus of George Mason University where he earned
                                    a B.A. in Russian Studies and the University of Georgia where he earned a M.Ed. in
                                    College Student Affairs Administration. He is a member of Delta Chi, and has served
                                    the Fraternity as a staff member at the International Headquarters and as a volunteer.
                                    In addition to working with college students, Michael enjoys cooking and entertaining,
                                    travelling to new places, and reading mystery novels.
