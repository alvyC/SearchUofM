                     If we haven't met you yet, we can't wait to!
                     
                     The UofM is an eclectic mix of students from all kinds of backgrounds and in different
                        stages of life. While some of our students are right out of high school, many are
                        not. Some live on campus, many don't. These differences make us a better University
                        and our students better people. We have tailored services to support and assist our
                        Adult and Commuter Students . If that's you, then check out the many ways that we can help make your life as a
                        Memphis Tiger even better.
