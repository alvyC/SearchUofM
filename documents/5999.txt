                     Undergraduate Learning Center Positions Available
                     
                     Posted on 2015-07-20
                     
                     The Department is looking for several strong undergraduate students to support instruction
                        of our undergraduate courses beginning in the Fall 2015 semester.
                     
                     
                     Responsibilities may include:
                     
                     
                        
                        Holding hours in the Computer Science Learning Center to help other undergraduates with their coursework
                        
                        
                        Being available outside of class (email, text, etc.) to answer students’ questions
                        
                     
                     
                     Minimum Qualifications:
                     
                     
                        
                        Have completed COMP 1900, COMP 2150, and COMP 3160 with a B or better in all three
                           (or COMP 1900 and the new 4-hour version of COMP 2150)
                        
                        
                        Able to clearly explain basic programming and data structures in Java, both orally
                           and in writing
                        
                        
                        Detail-oriented and reliable
                        
                     
                     
                     Compensation:
                     
                     
                        
                        $10.00 per hour
                        
                        Hours per week are negotiable, usually around 8-10 hours per student
                        
                     
                     
                     To Apply:
                     
                     Please send a resume and cover letter to Top Malasri at kmalasri@memphis.edu . Applicants may be asked to participate in a technical interview and/or conduct a
                        mock tutoring session as part of the selection process. The deadline to apply is August 7, 2015 .
