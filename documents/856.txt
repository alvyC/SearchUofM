                     Swim Lessons/Classes
                     
                      
                     
                      
                     
                     american red cross swim lessons
                     
                     Our swim classes offer a supportive, nurturing, and safe environment in which participants
                        ages six months to adult can learn to swim or improve their existing skills. Group
                        swim lessons are based on availability. Please come to entry desk at Recreation Center
                        to register for group swim lessons.
                     
                     
                     Fall swim lessons begin sept. 3
                     
                     Lessons will be held on Saturdays in September and October at the following times:
                     
                     Parent/Child: 9:30-10 a.m.
                     
                     Preschool: 10:15-10:45 a.m.
                     
                     Youth Beginners I: 11-11:40 a.m.
                     
                     Youth Beginners II: 11-11:40 a.m.
                     
                     Youth Intermediate: 12-12:40 p.m.
                     
                     Adults and Teens: 12-12:40 p.m.
                     
                      
                     
                     Class Descriptions
                     
                     Parent/Child (six months to three years old):  Ages 6 months-3 years. 30-minute lessons This class teaches water safety and water adaptation, through songs and games for
                        children and their parents. Parents will become confident handling their babies in
                        the water while teaching water movement skills. 10 pairs per instructor
                     
                     
                     Preschool (four-five years old):  Ages 4-5 years. 30-minute lessons This class is divided into two levels. No more than six participants per instructor o Beginners- no previous water experience o Beginners/intermediate- comfortable in water, puts face in water and blows bubbles,
                        can swim minimum of five feet with or without belt or assistance.
                     
                     
                     Youth Beginners I (six to 12 years old): Eight lessons, 40 minutes per lesson. No previous water experience. This level teaches
                        the fundamentals of swimming.
                     
                     
                     Youth Beginners II (six to 12 years old): Eight lessons, 40 minutes per lesson. Basic water skills mastered; can float on front
                        and back, swim 15 feet without assistance, glide and kick.
                     
                     
                     Youth Intermediate (six to 12 years old):  Eight lessons, 40 minutes per lesson. Swims easily on stomach in deep water, breathes
                        side to side, and can swim front and back crawl
                     
                     
                     Adult and Teens (13 and up):   Eight lessons, 40 minutes per lesson. This class is designed to teach beginner teens/adults
                        the fundamentals of swimming, as well as work with those who want to develop their
                        skills. 
                     
                     
                     Cost: Students , $40; Members , $60; Non-Members , $80
                     
                     
                     Private Lessons
                     
                     Click here to register  for private swim lessons.
                     
                     
                     Sessions : Five lessons, 30 minutes each
                     
                     
                     Cost: Students, $80, Members, $100, Non-Members, $120
                     
                     
                     At CRIS, we are passionate about teaching kids and adults to be strong swimmers. Our
                        lessons are for swimmers from age four to adult and include a private one-on-one session
                        with your very own instructor for extreme beginners or those who want to condition
                        through swimming. 
                     
                     
                     For more information, please call 901.678.3461 or email Aquatic and Safety Coordinator Ashley Chism.
