                     WUMR Underwriting
                     
                     We would love to welcome you to the WUMR family of sponsors who makes it possible
                        for us to provide quality jazz music and top-level sports and educational programming.
                        When you become a sponsor, you help us keep classic jazz alive while bringing attention
                        to your company and boosting its image.
                     
                     
                     We invite you to browse through this Web site to learn more about WUMR and our programming,
                        as well as our many underwriting opportunities. If you have any questions or need
                        additional information, please feel free to contact us anytime.
                     
                     
                     It would be our pleasure to welcome you to WUMR and our loyal audience of Jazz Lovers,
                        but first you may want to get to know us better:
                     
                     
                     WUMR, The Jazz Lover U92 is the University of Memphis radio station, located on the
                        University's main campus. We are the only station in the Memphis area that plays a
                        "jazz only" format. A 25,000-watt powerhouse, WUMR's signal covers the entire Memphis
                        metro area and its surrounding vicinity, and listeners worldwide can tune in to The
                        Jazz Lover online. We stay true to our loyal audience by putting "U" in the music,
                        and keeping Mid-South jazz fans "hip" to what is new on the jazz scene.
                     
                     
                     WUMR has gathered a loyal, upwardly mobile, and well-educated audience with higher
                        than average disposable incomes. The station has gained a wide following in the jazz
                        community and beyond by playing the right mix of contemporary and traditional jazz.WUMR
                        is aware of the importance and diversity of art in our community. Each week, Malvin
                        Massey interviews the movers and shakers of the Memphis arts scene, highlighting upcoming
                        events and extraordinary local and national talent.
                     
                     
                     WUMR offers extensive coverage of local sports, and is the exclusive home of University
                        of Memphis women's basketball, Memphis Tiger baseball, Rhodes College football, and
                        Memphis University School football.
