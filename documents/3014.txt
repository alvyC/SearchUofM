                     New Faculty
                     
                     Welcome to the University of Memphis!  We are thrilled to have you on board!  Below
                        you will find important resources for new faculty, including information about Orientation,
                        the City of Memphis, parking, and required paperwork. The pages on the menu to the
                        left also provides important information about the CV database, tenure and promotion,
                        and other faculty resources.

                        in the FedEx Institute of Technology (FIT).   A list of speakers and events may be found here:  2016 New Faculty Academic Affairs Orientation Program
                     
                     Documents from the 2016 New Faculty Orientation and General Faculty Meeting may be
                        found here: The PowerPoint presentation from the General Faculty Meeting is available here . The PowerPoint presentations from the New Faculty Orientation are available here:
                        research , legal counsel , eCourseware , university branding , teaching online , and student affairs . Student Affairs also has information available on their website: click here .
