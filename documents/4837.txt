                     Enjoy the Benefits of Wellness!
                     
                     UofM Lambuth's Wellness, Health & Fitness Building is open Monday - Thursday, 12:00 pm
                        - 8:00 pm and Friday, 12:00pm-6:00pm and the Lap Pool is open Monday - Wednesday,
                        3:00pm - 7:00pm and Friday 3:00pm-5:30pm for recreational use. NOTE: The pool will
                        be closed until further notice on Thursdays beginning October 27. Click  HERE for our fall 2016 Group Exercise Schedule, Click HERE for our fall 2016 Intramural Schedule, and click  HERE for a copy of Fall 2016 Campus Recreation Program Guide. For questions or rental
                        information, call Alejandro Herrera, Supervisor, Campus Recreation, 731.425.7328 or
                        email aherrera@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     We are looking to hire lifeguards for the Campus recreation center at Lambuth:
                     
                     Lambuth Campus Recreation Employment Opportunity
                     
                     Now accepting applications for lifeguards Must have the following current certifications:
                        Lifeguard, CPR/AED, and First Aid.
                     
                     
                     Pay: $8.50 an hour. For more information, contact Ashley Chism at 901.678.3461 or
                        at amchism1@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     PREREQUISITES FOR EMPLOYMENT:
                     
                     1. Must hold the following CURRENT certifications: Lifeguard Training Emergency First Aid CPR for the Professional Rescuer
                     
                     
                     2. Must have a POSITIVE and HELPFUL ATTITUDE!! LIFEGUARD DUTIES:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Supervision of the pool facilities
                        
                        Clearing and securing the pool at closing
                        
                        Curtailing any misuse of the facility or inappropriate actions
                        
                        Checking for proper identification
                        
                        Check that safety equipment is in correct place, and is in good working order
                        
                        Inspection of the pool and equipment.
                        
                        Secure off any discrepancies and make a written report
                        
                        Enforce special participant rules (lap swim/recreation swim)
                        
                        Please use phone for emergencies only
                        
                        Accident care and reporting
                        
                        Covering shifts
                        
                        Checking equipment and supplies before every shift
                        
                        Clearing and securing pool at closing
                        
                        Checking for proper identification
                        
                        Maintain a helpful and pleasant relationship with regular patrons
                        
                        Keep a positive and friendly attitude when dealing with ALL patrons
                        
                     
                     
                     OTHER IMPORTANT ITEMS:
                     
                     All lifeguards MUST be on time (at his/her lifeguard station 15 minutes prior to opening
                        the pool). Proper attire must be worn at all times (lifeguard swimsuit and whistle). In the interest
                        of training -
                     
                     
                     ALL guards must swim a minimum of 500 yards per week. Applications are available here.
                        Please print, fill out and return to Student Recreation and Fitness Center or send through
                        email to amchism1@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     Contact our Aquatics Office at 901.678.3461 for more information.
                     
                     ***Upcoming Lifeguard certification courses at U of M Lambuth Campus***
                     
                     The class is a 30 hour class. It includes: 7.5 hours of online training, CPR/AED and
                        first aid training, Lifeguard classroom and pool skills.
                     
                     
                     Pre requisites for this class include: 500 yard swim, tread water for 2 minutes with
                        legs only, head first and feet first surface dives and being able to hoist yourself out of the pool
                        without using a ladder.
                     
                     
                     * PLEASE CONTACT ASHLEY CHISM, AMCHISM1@MEMPHIS.EDU FOR MORE INFORMATION ON UPCOMING
                           LIFEGUARD COURSES.
                     
                     MEMBERSHIPS:
                     
                     In order to encourage wellness among employees, students, and their household members,
                        we are offering new opportunities for memberships at our on-campus fitness facilities.
                        Although we were pleased to offer complimentary memberships to faculty and staff prior
                        to this time, in order to continue providing members of the UofM Lambuth community
                        with state-of-the art facilities and services, we are now offering memberships at
                        very affordable prices. We are also now offering new opportunities for employees'
                        and students' household members to enjoy membership privileges.
                     
                     
                     Employees
                     
                     
                        
                        For a nominal fee, employees and their household members may access all of our recreational
                           facilities. Our many amenities at UofM Lambuth include the recently renovated indoor
                           pool and basketball courts in the Wellness, Health, and Fitness Building, state-of-the-art
                           cardio and weight equipment in the Fitness Studio (located in Wilder Student Union),
                           recently renovated tennis courts, a sand volleyball court, and group fitness classes.
                           Intramural activities are also offered free-of-cost to students.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Full-Time Students
                     
                     
                        
                        Full-time UofM students (enrolled in six hours or more) will continue to have free
                           access to the Recreation Center.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Student Household Membership
                     
                     
                        
                        Full-time students may purchase a membership to sponsor one other adult who lives
                           in their same household and/or dependent children under 21 years of age.  Cost is
                           $60 per semester.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Part-Time Students
                     
                     
                        
                        If enrolled in less than six hours, part-time students may access Recreation Center
                           for $3 per use.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Employees
                     
                     
                        
                        Full-time, permanent employees may purchase memberships at the following rates:
                        
                        Individual and Household Memberships for regular employees with base salary of $27,999
                           or less may pay $10 per month for Individual Memberships or $20 per month for Household
                           Memberships (Members may sponsor one adult who lives in their household and/or dependent
                           children under 21 years of age.).
                        
                        
                        Regular employees with base salary of $28,000 or more may pay $13 per month for Individual
                           Memberships or $26 per month for Household Memberships.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Guests
                     
                     
                        
                        One guest is allowed per student or faculty/staff member for $5 per visit.  Additional
                           guest passes may be allowed at the discretion of the Recreation Supervisor.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Summer Passes
                     
                     
                        
                        Beginning Summer 2016, students who were enrolled full-time (six hours or more) in
                           the previous spring semester will be eligible to purchase summer passes.  Cost is
                           $45.
