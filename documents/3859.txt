                     Visitor Parking
                     
                     Garage Parking:  Visitors may use the Innovation Garage (Lot 40) located next to the Fogelman Executive
                        Center or the Zach Curlin Parking Garage (Lot 3) located at 120 Zach Curlin. The visitor
                        rate for both garages is $3.00 per hour with a $15.00 per day maximum.
                     
                     
                     Metered Parking: There are metered spaces and lots located all around campus for a rate of $2.00 per
                        hour. Meters accept coin and major credit cards. There is no time limit.
                     
                     
                     Metered Spaces:   Lot 44 , Lot 39 ,  Lot 32 , Lot 15 , Lot 6 , Lot 5 , and Lot 2 .
                     
                     
                     Visitor Parking Permit:  Visitors may purchase a parking permit online by visiting the TigerPark Guest Account Login .
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        PDF Tutorial for Purchasing a Visitor Permit Online
                           
                           
                              
                              One Day Visitor Permit for General Permit Parking is $3.00
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Visitor permits may also be purchased in Parking and Transportation Services located
                        at 505 Zach Curlin Parking Garage. If you have any questions, please contact PTS at
                        901.678.2212 or parking@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                       Emeriti Parking: Emeriti Parking is for retirees of the University of Memphis. The Emeriti Permit
                        (hangtag) allows the retiree to park in any General Parking lot or at any metered
                        space at no cost.
                     
                     
                     Visitor's Citation Appeal Form: Visitors may appeal parking citations online by visiting the TigerPark Guest Account Login , by e-mailing parking@memphis.edu or by submitting an appeal form to PTS located at 505 Zach Curlin Parking Garage.
