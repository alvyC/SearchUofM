University College Alumni Chapter Executive Board Applications
We want you to join the Board! The University College Alumni
Chapter is seeking to fill a few open positions on their board and are
welcoming applications. Attending quarterly meetings, mild event
planning and input into key issues are all that is expected. For more
information, contact  alumniassociation@memphis.edu  or 901.678.2586.

For more information about the University College Alumni Chapter or any upcoming events, contact  alumniassociation@memphis.edu  or 901.678.2586.
