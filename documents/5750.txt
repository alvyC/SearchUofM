                     Collaborative Piano
                     
                     Collaborative Arts Studies in Piano holds a distinguished and active place at the
                        Rudi E. Scheidt School of Music. Directed by Dr. Cathal Breslin, the program offers
                        one of the most comprehensive and challenging curriculum in the United States. Members
                        of the resident ensembles - Ceruti String Quartet, Memphis Brass Quintet and Memphis
                        Woodwind Quintet - are joined by the esteemed vocal and opera studies faculty to provide
                        coachings and a complete musical education needed by 21st century collaborative artists
                        in order to succeed.
                     
                     
                     Master and Doctorate degrees are offered in Performance with a concentration in Collaborative
                        Piano.
                     
                     
                     For more information on Collaborative Piano studies, contact Area Coordinator, Cathal
                        Breslin at cbreslin@memphis.edu .
