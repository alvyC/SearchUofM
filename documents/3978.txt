                     Common Holds
                     
                     These are the administrative holds you are most likely to encounter. Why  explains why the hold was placed and, when appropriate, gives you a link to a fuller
                        explanation. Contact tells you which office to contact about removing the hold.
                     
                     
                     Only the office that placed the hold can remove it.
                     
                     ASR Purge
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Your academic status requires that you contact the Center for Academic Retention and
                           Enrichment Services (CARES) before being allowed to register. See Academic Standing .
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Center for Academic Retention and Enrichment Services
                        
                     
                     
                     Draft Registration
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Admissions requires proof of your Selective Service registration. Males born January
                           1, 1960 or later must register with the Selective Service System .
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Admissions
                        
                     
                     
                     Final HS Transcript
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Admissions has not received your final high school transcript for evaluation. You
                           cannot be fully admitted and register until your transcript has been evaluated.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Admissions
                        
                     
                     
                     Grad Probation
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Your academic status requires that you contact the Graduate School before being allowed
                           to register.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Graduate School
                        
                     
                     
                     Hepatitis B or Meningitis
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Proof of vaccination is required. See Immunization / Hepatitis B - Meningitis .
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Student Health Center
                        
                     
                     
                     High School Deficiency
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   You did not meet all Tennessee high school requirements.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Center for Academic Retention and Enrichment Services
                        
                     
                     
                     Intl Hlth Insurance
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Proof of health insurance is required. See Health Insurance Requirements - FAQs .
                        
                        
                        Contact:   International Students Office
                        
                     
                     
                     Measles
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   You were granted a waiver for supplying documentation of two Measles immunizations.
                           However, the waiver period has expired; thus, you cannot register, add, or drop until
                           you supply the required documentation to Student Health Services. See Immunization / MMR for more information on this hold and the steps to take to remove it.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Student Health Center
                        
                     
                     
                     Non-Degreement Agreement
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   You are required to complete a Graduate Non-Degree Agreement form before enrolling in further graduate courses.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Graduate School
                        
                     
                     
                     Official College Transcript
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Admissions has not received your transcript(s) from your previous institution(s) for
                           evaluation. You cannot be fully admitted and register until your transcript(s) have
                           been evaluated.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Admissions
                        
                     
                     
                     Outstanding Accts Rec
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   You owe the University money. You have not satisfied all fees and fines. You will
                           not be allowed to register for the upcoming term, view grades, request an official
                           transcript, or perform a degree audit until your fees/fines have been satisfied.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Bursar's Office
                        
                     
                     
                     PPD or TB Screen
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Proof of tuberculosis skin testing is required. See Immunization / TB Skin Test .
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Student Health Center
                        
                     
                     
                     Returned Check - AR
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   Your wrote a check to the University that was refused for insufficient funds.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Bursar's Office
                        
                     
                     
                     Student Conduct
                     
                     
                        
                        Why?   You must be cleared by the Office of Student Conduct before you can be readmitted
                           or register.
                        
                        
                        Contact:   Student Conduct
