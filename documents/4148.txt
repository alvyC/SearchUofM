                        Early Bird Swim Program
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     Swim your way to wellness.
                     
                      If you like to start your day with a splash , we'll have the water ready! Just become a member of our Early Bird Swim Program . Early Bird Hours are Monday--Friday, 5:30 a.m.--8 a.m.
                     
                     
                     Fees: Any University of Memphis Faculty/Staff member, alumnus, or community member who
                        currently holds a membership at the Recreation Center may join the Early Bird Swim
                        Program free of charge.
                     
                     
                     Any currently enrolled full-time student may also join this program for free. All
                        other interested swimmers may pay $150 for a four-month membership to the Early Bird
                        Swim Program.
                     
                     
                     Fees are due on: January 1, May 1, and September 1 for the following periods: January-April,
                        May-August, and September-December. We will gladly pro-rate your fee after each month
                        if you decide to join in the middle of a four-month period.
                     
                     
                     To join: Fill out a  registration form  and pay all fees at the Recreation Center front desk between 5:30 - 8:00 am, Monday
                        through Friday.
                     
                     
                     Questions? For additional information, call Ashley Chism at 678.3461.
