                        education available to any undergraduate student at the University of Memphis. Â Three
                        levels of training are offered, with each level having a specific audience and purpose:

                        college success.
                     
                     
                     Level IIÂ  is open to sophomore, junior, and senior students, and focuses on effective student
                        leadership. It is also open to freshman students who have graduated from Level I.Â 
                     
                     
                     Level III is only open to Level II graduates, and it applies leadership concepts to a variety
                        of roles after college.
                     
                     
                     Level IV is only open to Level III graduates, and it focuses on career, resume, and networking
                        development through leadership concepts.
