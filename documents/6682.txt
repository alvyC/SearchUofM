                     Strategic Plan:  VISION 2020
                     
                     Mission Statement
                     
                     To develop a diverse community of students for ethical, effective, and responsible
                        business leadership; create and disseminate knowledge for new business realities in
                        the global economy; and advance the intellectual and economic vitality of the community,
                        region, and beyond.
                     
                     
                     Vision Statement
                     
                     We support economic growth by developing a diverse student body into professionally
                        prepared future business leaders and research scholars.
                     
                     
                     Values
                     
                     The Fogelman College of Business & Economics embraces the following values in teaching,
                        research and service: excellence, integrity, professionalism, innovation, respect,
                        collegiality and collaboration.
                     
                     
                     Strategic Priorities
                     
                     
                        
                        Be a national leader in ensuring student success within a diverse student body
                        
                        Significantly increase faculty's research productivity and promote research quality
                        
                        Significantly increase the support of the opportunities for greater local and regional
                           economic development
                        
                        
                        To achieve FCBE's mission and vision in an environment of declining public funding,
                           significantly increase private funding by seeking new opportunities for engagement
                           with alumni and business community stakeholders.
