                     Dining Dollars Refund
                     
                     The Dining Dollars Refund Period for Fall 2016 is Monday, November 28, 2016 through
                              Friday, December 9, 2016.
                     
                     
                        
                        Any unused Dining Dollars will remain on your card unless you request a refund during
                           this specified period.
                        
                        
                        Beginning Monday, November 28, 2016, log in to your Campus Card account (click Your Campus Card on the navigation menu above) to request your Dining Dollars refund. Select the Click Here to Refund My Dining Dollars button (made available on Monday, November 28, 2016).
                        
                        
                        Refund requests must be submitted online no later than midnight Friday, December 9,
                           2016. If you submit your refund request correctly, an e-mail confirmation will be
                           sent to your U of M email address. If you do not receive and an e-mail confirmation
                           within 30 minutes, e-mail campuscard@memphis.edu for verification.
                        
                        
                        Dining Dollars will be available for spending through Friday, December 9, 2016.
                        
                        Refunds:
                        
                        
                           
                           Refunds will be completed by Wednesday, December 21, 2016.
                           
                           eRefunds will begin to post to accounts Wednesday, December 21, 2016.
                           
                           Checks will be mailed Wednesday, December 21, 2016.
                           
                        
                        
                        Students are responsible for verifying and/or updating their eRefund account information
                           on TigerXpress as well as their mailing address on the myMemphis portal.
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Refunds will be processed to the student's eRefund (direct deposit) account through
                              TigerXpress .
                           
                           
                           If no refund payment profile exists in TigerXpress, a check will be sent to the mailing
                              address on file with the University. Sign up for eRefund on TigerXpress .
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        If you request a refund and your balance is $5.00 or less, your balance will be remain
                           on your card.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Please note that any amount owed to the University will be deducted from your available
                        balance in the account prior to the processing of a refund.
