                      Thank you for your generous support of the University of Memphis Alumni Association. Our valued members and donors play an essential role in both our future and our success.

All across campus, your contributions are making a difference. To see your impact,
simply look at our students, our faculty or our facilities. With the support of our
alumni and friends, the University of Memphis continues to enlighten, encourage and
empower the next generation of leaders and scholars.

Make A Gift
Join the Guild of 1912
The
Alumni Association has established the Guild of 1912 as a way to honor
those that have come before us and to provide programs and services to
those that are paving the way for the future. Your sustaining monthly
gift of $19.12 will directly support innovative new programming and
annual traditions for Alumni Association and Future Alumni of Memphis
members. Through the creation of career services, networking and
coaching opportunities and more, we can continue to meet the needs of
our alumni and friends. Your gift makes a lasting impact on your alma
mater.

Advocate for the UofM


Advocate for the UofM is the grassroots network of the University of Memphis to let
our elected officials know what is important to our students, faculty, staff and community.
Our advocates include thousands of alumni, employees, students and friends who believe
in the University's value to the Mid-South and across the State of Tennessee. With
your help, we will more effectively share our message with our elected officials at
the state and national level. Your voice matters, and together we can make a difference.
Become an Advocate for the UofM today by clicking here .


 
Volunteer as a Club or Chapter Leader
There are many ways alumni can give back to their
alma mater. Just as rewarding as monetary giving is giving of your time.
Working with the Alumni Association is a great opportunity to reconnect with fellow alumni while making a difference. Each Club and Chapter has a governing board that assists with planning and hosting events, encourages membership and aids in raising funds for scholarships.
Contact the staff coordinator for the Club or Chapter you are interested in to learn more.  See complete list of clubs and chapters here
