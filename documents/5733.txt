                        Enrollment Timeline 
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     2017-2018 Undergraduate Enrollment Timeline
                     
                     September 1
                     
                     School of Music registration opens. Read  APPLICATION INFORMATION .  Go to "Apply Now" to complete and submit your application.
                     
                     
                     September 1
                     
                     University of Memphis registration opens for Spring 2017 or Fall 2017 semesters. 
                        Have your high school and/or college mail official transcripts to: University of Memphis,
                        Office of Admissions, 101 Wilder Tower, Memphis, TN 38152. Have your ACT and/or SAT
                        test scores sent to the UofM Office of Admissions by the testing services. The UofM
                        accepts records of ACT and SAT exams that appear on official high school transcripts.
                        Transfer students applying for the Music Education degree program must provide high
                        school ACT or SAT scores.
                     
                     
                     October 1
                     
                     Obtain the 2017-2018 Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).  Learn more
                        about the new FAFSA changes at memphis.edu/financialaid/
                     
                     October 22
                     
                     UofM Campus Day. Contact recruitment@memphis.edu for information.
                     
                     November 5
                     
                     UofM Campus Day. Contact  recruitment@memphis.edu  for information.
                     
                     
                     December 1
                     
                     Date for those accepted to the University to receive priority consideration for academic
                        scholarship by the UofM.
                     
                     
                     December 3
                     
                     Rudi E. Scheidt School of Music (RESSOM) Audition Day/Orientation for Spring 2017
                        and Fall 2017; admissions and scholarship auditions
                     
                     
                     2017
                     
                     January 2
                     
                     The FAFSA should be mailed to the Federal Processor by this date in order to ensure
                        maximum consideration for all financial aid programs
                     
                     
                     February 4
                     
                     RESSOM Audition/Orientation Day for Fall 2017; admissions and scholarship auditions
                     
                     February 18
                     
                     RESSOM Audition/Orientation Day for Fall 2017; admissions and scholarship auditions
                     
                     February 25
                     
                     RESSOM Audition/Orientation Day for Fall 2017; admissions and scholarship auditions
                     
                     Mid-March
                     
                     Notification to scholarship recipients begins
                     
                     May 1
                     
                     Last day to accept talent-based/performance scholarship offers. Offers MUST be accepted
                        online in the MyMemphis portal.
                     
                     
                     May & June & July
                     
                     New Student Orientations. Incoming freshmen and transfer students attend university
                        orientation days. Music majors and music minors are advised by music faculty and register
                        for classes.
                     
                     
                     Late August
                     
                     Check into your dormitory.
                     
                     Auditions for placements in instrumental and vocal ensembles. See instructions on
                        our home page at http://www.memphis.edu/music/ posted in July; look for ENSEMBLE AUDITIONS.
                     
                     
                     Before August 28
                     
                     Enrollment fees must be satisfied prior to the first day of classes to avoid assessment
                        of a late payment fee.
                     
                     
                     August 28
                     
                     Start classes.
