                     Battalion Policies
                     
                     
                        
                        Female Grooming
                        
                        Male Grooming
                        
                     
                     
                     
                     
                      Female Grooming Policies
                     
                     
                     Hair
                     
                     Hair will be neatly groomed, the length and bulk may not be excessive, and may not
                        present a ragged, unkempt, or extreme appearance. Trendy styles that result in shaved
                        portions of the scalp (other than the neckline) or designs cut into the hair are not
                        authorized. Braids and cornrows are authorized as long as the braided style is conservative,
                        the braids and cornrows lie snugly on the head, and any hair holding devices are plain
                        in color and as close to the soldier's hair color as possible or clear. Dreadlocks
                        are prohibited. Hair will not fall over the eyebrows or extend below the bottom edge
                        of the collar at any time. Long hair that falls below the collar will be neatly and
                        inconspicuously fastened or pinned.
                     
                     
                     Styles that are lopsided or distinctly unbalanced are prohibited.
                     
                     Cosmetics
                     
                     May apply cosmetics as long as they are conservative and complement the uniform and
                        complexion. Designs may not be applied to nails, nor apply two-tone or multi-tone
                        colors to nails.
                     
                     
                     Nails
                     
                     Fingernails will not exceed 1/4", as measured from the tip of the finger.
                     
                     
                     
                      Male Grooming Policies
                     
                     
                     Hair
                     
                     The hair on top of the head must be neatly groomed, and the length and bulk may not
                        be excessive or present a ragged, unkempt, or extreme appearance, and may not interfere
                        with the normal wear of headgear or protective masks or equipment.
                     
                     
                     Hair must present a tapered appearance.
                     
                     When combed, the hair will not fall over the ears or eyebrows, or touch the collar
                        except for the closely cut hair at the back of the neck. Braids, Cornrows, or dreadlocks
                        are not authorized while in uniform or civilian clothes on duty.
                     
                     
                     Sideburns
                     
                     Sideburns will be kept neatly trimmed, may not be flared, and will be clean-shaven
                        and horizontal at the base. Sideburns will not extend below the lowest part of the
                        exterior ear opening.
                     
                     
                     Shaving
                     
                     Faces will be clean shaven while in uniform or in civilian clothes on duty.
                     
                     Mustaches are permitted; if worn they will be neatly trimmed, tapered, and tidy. They
                        will not present a chopped off or bushy appearance, and may not cover the upper lip
                        line or extend sideways beyond a vertical line drawn from the corners of the mouth.
                     
                     
                     Nails
                     
                     Fingernails will be kept neatly trimmed so as not to exceed beyond the fingertip.
                     
                     No piercings may be worn in uniform.
                     
                     Any civilian clothes worn will be free of offensive logos or imagery.
