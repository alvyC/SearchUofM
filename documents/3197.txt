                     Football Game-Day Information
                     
                     ARE YOU READY FOR SOME TIGER FOOTBALL?!?! It's time to get excited! The 2016 season of Memphis Tiger football kicks off this
                        Saturday, September 3rd against the Southeast Missouri State Redhawks at 6:00 p.m.
                        at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium. Please read the following details carefully as it
                        includes everything you need to know in preparation for the game:
                     
                     
                     Memphis Tigers Football Game - Saturday, September 3rd, 6:00 p.m. at Liberty Bowl
                           Memorial Stadium. Come watch the Tigers as they take on Southeast Missouri State in the season opener.
                     
                     
                     Student Shuttles
                     
                     CHASE THE TIGERS TO THE SOUTHERN MISSOURI STATE FOOTBALL GAME!
                     
                     
                        
                        FREE Blue Line shuttle bus service for all students, faculty, and staff
                        
                        Depart West end of Central Parking Lot 3 hours before 6:00 pm Kickoff and will begin
                           running continuously two hours before Kickoff until 1 hour following the game.
                        
                        
                        Drop Off at the Cocoa Cola Bottling Co on Hollywood
                        
                        Pick Up at the Cocoa Cola Bottling Co on Hollywood
                        
                        Must have valid UM ID or be accompanied by someone with a valid ID.
                        
                        Track the buses using the Tiger Route (pink) on mobile app: memphis.transloc.com
                        
                        For more information, please visit http://bf.memphis.edu/parking/blueline.php .
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Student Tailgate
                     
                     The official student tailgate, "The Den," is back . The Den will be staged in the Tan lot adjacent to the Liberty Bowl – in front of
                        the Coliseum and across from the Pipkin Building. To reserve a spot in The Den, visit
                        http://www.memphis.edu/studentinvolvement/activities/tailgate.php . Each group with a reserved tailgating spot will receive two reserved parking spots.
                        Reservations are on a first-come, first-served basis. Your group will receive a confirmation
                        email regarding your reservation. The tailgating lot is accessible by taking Southern
                        Ave from campus to the stadium and turning right on Early Maxwell at Access 11 – the
                        lot is on the right. The game starts at 6:00 p.m. so tailgating will be open to the
                        public at 2:00 p.m. for groups who have reserved spots. Have questions? Email bluecrew@memphis.edu or stop by University Center 211. You can join the Tailgate even if your group doesn't
                        have a reserved parking spot; just walk on over – Join in with your TIGER FAMILY!!
                        See map below.
                     
                     Student parking
                     
                     Students can pick up free parking passes for each home game at the University Center Theater Ticket Window the Thursday before
                        every home game, unless otherwise stated. There will be two times to pick up a parking
                        pass: 9:30 am and 4:30 pm. The student parking ticket allotment will be split evenly
                        between the two times. One parking pass will be given out with a valid student ID.
                        Student can only use Gate Access 10 off of East Parkway to enter the student parking lot.
                     
                     
                     Student Gate Procedures
                     
                     Students must access the Liberty Bowl through Gate 6.
                     
                     Students enrolled in six hours or more with a valid University of Memphis ID will be admitted free of charge to all home
                        football games. You must have your Student ID with you, or you will not be admitted.
                        Student ID barcodes will be scanned to verify active enrollment. Student seating is in Sections 123 and 124 . Again, you MUST enter through Gate 6. Gates will open 1 ½ hours before kickoff time.
                        This week the gates will open at 4:30 pm for the 6:00 pm kickoff. Liberty Bowl policies
                        prohibit bringing food or beverages into the game. Umbrellas are not allowed in the
                        stadium so please be prepared and bring a poncho or raincoat. If you leave the Liberty
                        Bowl after you enter, re-entry is NOT allowed.
                     
                     
                     Students enrolled in five hours or less may purchase a $7 ticket for themselves at the Athletic Ticket Office (south of Southern,
                        east of the Rec Center) during regular office hours, while supplies last. These tickets will also be sold at Gate 6 on game days for cash only. Credit Cards
                           at Gate 1. 
                     
                     Students may purchase two guest tickets for each home football game at the Athletic Ticket Office for $10 each while supplies
                        last. Guest tickets will be available at the Athletic Ticket Office (south of Southern
                        and east of the Rec Center) which is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 am - 4:30
                        pm. Guest tickets may also be purchased at Gate 6 on game days for cash only. Credit
                        Cards at Gate 1. Only a limited number of guest tickets will be available for each
                        game.
                     
                     
                     All students and persons holding guest tickets MUST enter through the Student Gate
                           (gate 6).   If your ID card is damaged, you may receive a replacement ID card at the Campus Card
                        Office, 115 Wilder Tower, during normal business hours upon presentation of your damaged
                        card. Please note that if your ID card is used by someone else, your ID card will
                        be confiscated and turned over to the Student Conduct office.
                     
                     
                     Students registered with Disability Resources for Students who may need assistance with special seating should contact Melissa Moore-Brigance
                        at mmoore@memphis.edu at least 24 hours prior to each game if they wish to attend.
