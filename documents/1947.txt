                     Photos
                     
                     Students and guests are encouraged to take photos or videos (without tripods) at Commencement
                        as long as it does not interrupt the event or block other students and guests. Your
                        guests are not permitted on the FedExForum event floor, so as not to interfere with
                        the ceremony. 
                     
                     
                     Graduation photos
                     
                     GradImages will be photographing all graduates at the upcoming graduation ceremony.
                           Visit their website, www.gradimages.com , and click on the Pre-Event Email Registration, to register your email address. You
                           will also receive an email link and free passport-sized digital proofs of your photos
                           within 3 to 7 days following the ceremony. After graduation, you may order online
                           at www.gradimages.com or call 800.424.3686.
                     
                     Further Details  
                     
                     
                        
                        Photos at Commencement 
                           
                           
                              
                              GradImages will take 2-3 photos throughout your graduation ceremony. These photos
                                 will be emailed to your Memphis account 3-7 days after the event. 
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Borrowing caps and gown from Commencement
                           
                           
                              
                              The Commencement Office does NOT keep student gowns in the office for borrowing. Please
                                 work with your individual photographer if you want to take photos before cap and gown
                                 distribution.
