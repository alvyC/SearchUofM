                     Aquatics at the Rec Center
                     
                     Make your next workout in the pool. Welcome to the CRIS Aquatics department, where
                        safety and security are top priorities. Water sports and recreation offer the fun
                        factor in fitness, but it is very important that all our visitors follow proper aquatics
                        safety rules for the good of everyone. If the aquatics area is open, our lifeguards
                        are on duty (at least three at all times). Progressive security measures are in place,
                        creating a comfortable and safe environment for all U of M faculty, staff, students,
                        alumni, and community members.
                     
                     
                     Come meet our friendly staff and let them tell you more about the low-impact, total
                        body, invigorating workout that swimming provides.
                     
                     
                     Pool Facilities
                     
                     One 50 Meter Heated Indoor Pool (82.5 degrees)
                     
                     One 50 Meter Outdoor Pool: 6.5' center, 4' deep on ends
                     
                     One Dive Well (17' deep)
                     
                     12 person Whirlpool, Sauna, and Steam Rooms
                     
                     One Heated Indoor Leisure Pool
                     
                     Pool hours are subject to change depending on facility hours, University holidays,
                        or inclement weather.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Water Aerobics
                     
                     Water Aerobics classes are offered throughout the year in our Leisure Pool. You do
                        not have to be a Recreation Center member to enroll in these classes.
                     
                     
                     Six-week sessions cost $69.
                     
                     You may pay for the sessions at the front desk or click here to print the class registration form. Please bring completed form and payment one
                        week before the starting date of each session.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Lifeguard Jobs
                     
                     Now accepting applications for lifeguards to work the following shifts: Mondays, 3-7
                        p.m., Tuesdays, 12:30-3 p.m., and Thursdays, 12:30-3 p.m. Must have the following
                        current certifications: Lifeguard, CPR/AED, and First Aid. Must also be able to work
                        some weekends and be able to attend Lifeguard In-service training once a month on
                        Sunday evenings from 5 to 7 p.m. Pay: $9 an hour. For more information, contact Ashley
                        Chism at 901.678.3461 or at amchism1@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     PREREQUISITES FOR EMPLOYMENT:
                     
                     1. Must hold the following CURRENT certifications:
                     
                     Lifeguard TrainingEmergency First AidCPR for the Professional Rescuer
                     
                     2. Must have a POSITIVE and HELPFUL ATTITUDE!!
                     
                     LIFEGUARD DUTIES:
                     
                     
                        
                        Supervision of the pool facilities
                        
                        Clearing and securing the pool at closing
                        
                        Curtailing any misuse of the facility or inappropriate actions
                        
                        Checking for proper identification
                        
                        Check that safety equipment is in correct place, and is in good working order
                        
                        Inspection of the pool and equipment.
                        
                        Secure off any discrepancies and make a written report
                        
                        Enforce special participant rules (lap swim/recreation swim)
                        
                        Please use phone for emergencies only
                        
                        Accident care and reporting
                        
                        Covering shifts
                        
                        Checking equipment and supplies before every shift
                        
                        Clearing and securing pool at closing
                        
                        Checking for proper identification
                        
                        Maintain a helpful and pleasant relationship with regular patrons
                        
                        Keep a positive and friendly attitude when dealing with ALL patrons
                        
                     
                     
                     OTHER IMPORTANT ITEMS:
                     
                     All lifeguards MUST be on time (at his/her lifeguard station 15 minutes prior to opening
                        the pool). Proper attire must be worn at all times (lifeguard swimsuit and whistle).
                        In the interest of training - ALL guards must swim a minimum of 500 yards per week.
                        Applications are available here . Please print, fill out and return to Student Recreation and Fitness Center.
                     
                     
                     Contact our Aquatics Office at 901.678.3461 for more information.
