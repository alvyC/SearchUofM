Tigers @ Ole Miss Watch Party
 
 
Tigers @ Ole Miss Watch Party
sponsored by the UofM Alumni Association
ON the Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium Field

Saturday, October 1
Doors Open: 5:00 pm   |   Kick-Off: 6:00 pm


*Open to: UMAA, 901 Club, Tiger Scholarship Fund & M Club Members
                  Members of Athletic Booster Clubs
                  Current Faculty, Staff & Students
*Must show membership or UofM ID to enter
Download the UofM Alumni App and show your mobile membership card to enter and get a True Blue Happy!

Not a member? Join online or at the door!
Parking/Entry:

    Enter at Access 4 off of Hollywood Street
    One person per vehicle show one form of UM ID to access lot
    Pouncer will Point your way into the Stadium next to Gate 2 (North End Zone)
    



Can't make it to the big game? Take the field like the Tigers! Bring a
chair or picnic blanket and cheer on our Tigers against Ole Miss ON the
Liberty Bowl Field! 

Please note:

    Outside food and beverages are not permitted (no coolers allowed)
    Gameday grub and refreshments will be available to purchase from Memphis food trucks*
    
    Bring a chair or picnic blanket; stadium seating is not available
    Do not bring items that are prohibited in the Stadium (pets, weapons, etc.)
    
    The Bluff City Fair will be taking place in the Tiger Lane area. Please follow the parking map above.
    

*Food Trucks Available: El Mero Taco, Sushi Jimmy, General Concession


Life Members ONLY

Tour the newly renovated Recruitment Facility at halftime! Must show Life Membership card.



For more information, contact alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.2586.

Show your support for the Tigers and the UofM Alumni Association by sponsoring the event! Contact alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.2586 for more information about partnership opportunities.
