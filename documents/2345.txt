                     COE Degrees
                     
                     Undergraduate Students
                     
                     New undergraduate students who would like to major in a College of Education program
                        should check with the Academic Counseling Center before visiting the College to make
                        sure they have all the general requirements for admission to the university. When
                        they are ready they can contact:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Undergraduate Teacher Education Advisor
                        
                     
                     
                     Graduate Students
                     
                     New graduate students must apply to the Graduate School to be admitted to our graduate
                        programs. Students not seeking a degree, must also be apply to the Graduate School
                        as graduate no-major student before enrolling in our graduate courses.
                     
                     
                     Graduate students seeking advising should contact the department of their major.
