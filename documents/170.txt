                     Leadership and Policy Studies
                     
                     Our Vision
                     
                     To be an innovative national leader in preparing effective urban, suburban and rural
                        professionals. 
                     
                     
                     Our Mission 
                     
                     To provide a series of program activities that will enable individuals to work with
                        highly professional educators in an acquired chosen area and become recognized as
                        a national educational leader in their chosen area. 
                     
                     
                     Our Purpose
                     
                     We produce highly effective educational leaders who:
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           are scholarly instructional leaders
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           transform theory onto scholarly practice 
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           support and enhance opportunity for individuals to master knowledge and acquired skills
                              and disposition to work in a social, political democracy
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           lead and or direct schools that address he needs of each student in attendance
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Our Core Values 
                     
                     
                        
                        Respect: thinking and acting in a way that shows others you care about their feelings
                           and their well-being.
                        
                        
                        Trust: believing in the honesty and reliability of others. Having confidence in a
                           person or plan.
                        
                        
                        Communication: a connection that allows access between people or places. Communication
                           is conversation or discourse, a potential for exchanging information and ideas.
