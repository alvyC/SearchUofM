                     Fulbright Program
                     
                     Campus Deadline: September 14 The campus application deadline for Fulbright is 3pm on September TBD. Applicants
                           will receive an e-mail notifying them of the date and time for their campus interviews
                           which is tentatively scheduled for the first week of October.
                     
                     Please do not submit the on-line application till the Fulbright coordinator has reviewed
                        your application with you after the interview.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     For detailed information regarding the Fulbright Program please go to the Fulbright
                        Website:  http://us.fulbrightonline.org/
                     
                      
                     
                     Application Process
                     
                      
                     
                     All candidates applying through a U.S. institution must submit the Fulbright application
                        through the Embark online application system by the campus deadline; all supporting
                        materials as outlined in the Application Components section, must also be included by the campus deadline date.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Once your application has been submitted for institutional review, the Fulbright Program
                        Adviser will schedule a campus interview for you.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Following the interview and before the final deadline in October, you will have the
                        opportunity to revise your application based on feedback provided by the Fulbright
                        Program Adviser and the campus interview committee.
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     You must work with the Fulbright Program Adviser to ensure that your application is
                        officially submitted and that all supporting documents are attached by the final deadline:
                        October 13 (5:00 p.m., Eastern Time).
