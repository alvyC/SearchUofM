                        Research Interests
                        
                        
                           
                           In vitro platelet signal transduction investigations related to hemostasis and thrombosis
                              using knock out mice as model systems
                           
                           In vivo evaluation of the physiological significance of signal transduction pathway
                              information obtained from invitro studies
                           
                           Evalution of ligand receptor and signal transduction requirements for stable thrombus
                              formation in vivo in arteries and veins
                           
                           Characterization of outside-in signaling mediated by the individual subunits of the
                              major platelet integrin alphallb/beta3
                           
                           Development of a model system for the reproducubile induction of venous thromboembolism
                              (VTE) under quasi physiological conditions for the subsequent evaluation of ligand
                              receptor and signal transduction requirements for VTE.
