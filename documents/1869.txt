                     At the School of Music in Memphis, TN, you can fill your world with music, like no

                     OCTOBER     NOVEMBER
                     
                     October 6, 7:30 pm Jazz Singers and Jazz Ensemble II Harris Concert Hall Admission is free A small singing ensemble and a big band. Both dedicated to the sounds of jazz.
                     
                     
                     October 13, 7:30 pm Concerts International presents Wu Han, piano and David Finckel, cello Harris Concert Hall For tickets, Contact Concerts International at 527-3067. Cellist David Finckel and pianist Wu Han rank among the most dynamic and influential
                        forces in classical music today. The thrilling virtuosity and insightful musicianship
                        of their performances have inspired audiences around the world and have won universal
                        critical acclaim.
                     
                     
                     October 15, 7:30 pm UofM Piano Weekend (all day) Piano Extravaganza I Harris Concert Hall Admission is free As part of the UofM Piano Weekend, this concert will feature Drs. Cathal Breslin,
                        Artina McCain, Chih-Long Hu, Kasandra Keeling, and Esther Park. Each pianist will
                        perform as soloist and in various combinations with others. Click here for entire weekend schedule.
                     
                     
                     October 16, 7:30 pm UofM Piano Weekend (all day) Piano Extravaganza II Harris Concert Hall Admission is free As part of the UofM Piano Weekend, this concert will feature Drs. Cathal Breslin,
                        Artina McCain, Chih-Long Hu, Kasandra Keeling, and Esther Park. Each pianist will
                        perform as soloist and in various combinations with others. Click here for entire weekend schedule.
                     
                     
                     October 17, 7:30 pm UofM Symphony Orchestra Harris Concert Hall $15 General Admission, $10 Seniors, free to Students, UofM Faculty and Staff with
                           ID
                     
                     October 18 and19, 5:30 pm and 7:30 pm each evening Octubafest Harris Concert Hall Admission is free 2016 series will feature an internationally renowned guest artist, University of Memphis
                        faculty, and will showcase the University of Memphis "Bottom Line Tuba Ensemble."
                        Visit tuba.memphis.edu for more information.
                     
                     
                     October 23, 4:00 pm Lights, Camera, Choir Lindenwood Christian Church Admission is free but donations ($10/adults, $5/students) are accepted at the door. This concert will feature the men & women of Memphis ChoralArts performing music from
                        the stage and the silver screen, including music from Rent, Into the Woods, Wizard
                        of Oz, Les Miserables, Amistad, and so much more. It will feature two wonderfully,
                        talented soloists Chuck Hodges and Amy Nabors singing duets, as well our collaboration
                        choral ensemble of the year, the University of Memphis Collegiate Choir.
                     
                     
                     October 25, 7:30 pm Jazz Combos Harris Concert Hall Admission is free Small student jazz groups playing the music of jazz greats and original compositions.
                     
                     
                     October 26, 6:30 PM Todd Waldecker, clarinet and Adam Clark, piano Harris Concert Hall Admission is free Recital of works for clarinet and piano. Harris Auditorium. Weds. 10/26. 7:30 p.m.
                     
                     
                     October 28, 7:30 PM CANCELLED Faculty and Friends Harris Concert Hall Admission is free
                     
                     October 31, 7:30 pm University of Memphis Wind Ensemble Rose Theater Admission is free but you must call 678-2263 for reservations. This Halloween, experience the horror story that started it all. As the classic 1931
                        film Frankenstein plays, the Wind Ensemble performs the music live. Admission is free but you must call 678-2263 for reservations.
                     
                     October 31, 7:30 pm Concerts International presents the Ceruti Quartet Harris Concert Hall Admission is free but you must call 527-3067 for reservations. Ceruti performs the first in a six part series of the complete Beethoven quartets
                        in a special concert presented by Concerts International. The ensemble welcomes two
                        new members (Marcin Arendt, violin and Kimberly Patterson, cello) in their first concert
                        of the season. The performance is sponsored by Concerts International. Admission is free but you must visit CIMemphis.org or call 527-3067 for reservations.
                     
                      November 1, 7:30 pm Faculty Jazz Group Harris Concert Hall Admission is free The jazz faculty of the University of Memphis presenting small group repertoire from
                        the jazz greats.
                     
                     
                     November 3, 7:30 pm Sound Fuzion Harris Concert Hall $15 General Admission, $10 Seniors, free to Students, UofM Faculty and Staff with
                           ID ORDER TICKETS ONLINE HERE Sound Fuzion will raise the roof of Harris Concert Hall with the best in pop, rock,
                        and country music.
                     
                     
                     November 4, 7:30 pm Southern Comfort Jazz Orchestra Harris Concert Hall $15 General Admission, $10 Seniors, free to Students, UofM Faculty and Staff with
                           ID ORDER TICKETS ONLINE HERE Music of the big band repertoire played by the top jazz ensemble at the University
                        of Memphis.
                     
                     
                     November 5, 7:30 pm New Sounds Harris Concert Hall Admission is free New music written by University of Memphis student composers.
                     
                     
                     November 6, 3:00 pm UofM Chamber Choir Harris Concert Hall Admission is free
                     
                     November 12, 7:30 pm University Singers with the Memphis Symphony Orchestra and Chorus Cannon Center for the Performing Arts $15-$85. Contact the Memphis Symphony Box Office at 537-2500. The program will include R. Vaughn Williams" Dona Nobis Pacem and Dan Forrest's In
                        Paradisum.
                     
                     November 13, 2:30 pm University Singers with the Memphis Symphony Orchestra and Chorus Germantown Performing Arts Center $15-$85. Contact the Memphis Symphony Box Office at 537-2500. The program will include R. Vaughn Williams" Dona Nobis Pacem and Dan Forrest's In
                        Paradisum.
                     
                     
                     November 13, 3:00 pm UofM Symphonic Band and Tiger Youth Wind Ensemble White Station High School Auditorium, 514 S Perkins Rd.  Admission is free
                     
                     November 18, 7:30 pm UofM Opera presents Die Fledermaus by Johann Strauss II Harris Concert Hall $20 General Admission, $15 Seniors, free to Students, UofM Faculty and Staff with
                           ID Many years ago, a practical joke made Falke the laughing stock of Vienna. Tonight,
                        through a hilarious web of intrigue and disguise he'll get his revenge. Join him and
                        the the other revelers at as they waltz their way through an unforgettable evening
                        of sparkling melodies and farcical fun. Performed in a new English translation by
                        Quade Winter. Order tickets here .

                           ID Many years ago, a practical joke made Falke the laughing stock of Vienna. Tonight,

                     November 22, 7:30 pm Contemporary Chamber Players Harris Concert Hall Admission is free The University of Memphis' resident new music ensemble. Contemporary Chamber Players
                        will premiere works written by University of Memphis student composers.
                     
                     
                     November 29, 7:30 pm UofM Wind Ensemble Harris Concert Hall ORDER TICKETS ONLINE HERE $15 General Admission, $10 Seniors, free to Students, UofM Faculty and Staff with
                           ID
                     
                     November 30, 12:35 pm School of Music Holiday Concert Harris Concert Hall Admission is free School of Music Holiday Concert The holidays will be filled with music and memories
                        as the School of Music ushers in the season. Performances by student ensembles will
                        include old favorites and some new, original works.
