                     Traffic and Parking Rules and Regulations
                     
                     Article VII - Towing/Storage
                     
                     
                        
                        Agents designated by the University shall have authority to remove to a place of storage
                           at the owner’s expense, any vehicle(s):
                           
                              
                              Blocking disabled or handicapped curb cut or ramp
                              
                              Blocking a fire lane or fire hydrant (within fifteen (15) feet) or hindering movement
                                 of emergency vehicles.
                              
                              
                              Blocking or parking in a driveway or traffic lane, or impeding traffic flow (double
                                 parked).
                              
                              
                              Abandoned.
                              
                              Without license, parking permit or Vehicle Identification Number information visible.
                              
                              Upon which an Intent to Tow notice has been placed.
                              
                              Parked in lots not designated for parking permit purchased or reserved space without
                                 authorization.
                              
                              
                              Parking in a restricted-disabled or handicapped space without authorization.
                              
                              Parked in violation of Traffic and Parking Regulations.
                              
                              Parked in no parking zones.
                              
                              Parked on sidewalks or on grounds (grass or unpaved areas).
                              
                              Parked opposing traffic on the University streets.
                              
                              Occupying more than one parking space.
                              
                              Parked in designated loading zones for longer than 15 minutes.
                              
                              Unregistered.
                              
                              Parked overtime in a metered space.
                              
                              Registered and parked in visitor spaces.
                              
                              Upon which a University Official determines it is necessary for facilitating the expeditious
                                 flow of traffic.
                              
                              
                           
                        
                        
                        In addition to any appropriate fine, the owner or operator of the offending vehicle
                           shall be liable for payment of towing and storage fees.  The University of Memphis
                           is not responsible for damage to any vehicle resulting from towing or storage.  Appeal
                           procedures for towing are the same as described at rule 0240-3 3-.08(6).
