                     Employment Opportunities
                     
                     Thank you for your interest in employment at the Recreation Center!
                     
                     Applicants for all positions may print and fill out an  application , then submit it in person to the appropriate person listed for each specific job
                        opening, including:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                        
                        Entry Worker--no openings at this time.
                        
                        Equipment Attendant--no openings at this time.
                        
                        Fitness Attendant--no openings at this time.
                        
                        Lifeguard--now accepting applications for lifeguards to work all shifts. Must have
                           the following current certifications: Lifeguard, CPR/AED, and First Aid. Must also
                           be able to work some weekends and be able to attend Lifeguard In-service training
                           once a month on Sunday evenings from 5 to 7 p.m. Pay: $9 an hour. For more information,
                           contact Ashley Chism at 901.678.3461 or at amchism1@memphis.edu .
                        
                        
                        Intramurals-no openings at this time.
                        
                     
                     
                     Students must be: a full-time U of M student in good academic standing; have a U.S.
                        Social Security number; have a bank or CU checking account; not work at another on-campus
                        job; have appropriate certifications (lifeguards, instructors); be reliable and flexible;
                        be able to articulate and able to communicate clearly; be able to deal with customer
                        conflicts; be able to work on weekends along with during the week; be able to come
                        to work 10 minutes early for your shift and punch out in a timely manner; be able
                        to make quick judgments concerning incidences and emergencies; be able to attend all
                        mandatory training and in-service training sections; and be able to understand clearly
                        all policies concerning building usage and safety.
