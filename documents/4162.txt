                     Emergency Funds Resources
                     
                     Students sometimes experience financial strains that they are unable to handle on
                        their own. Please visit these resources to assist with your financial situation:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        University Financial Aid One-Stop Shop
                        
                        
                           
                           Financial strain can be addressed at the University with one of our financial aid
                              employees that can complete an analysis of your financial aid package.  There may
                              be resources you haven’t yet tapped or loans that you can take out to finance your
                              education. Loans are available at any point in the semester and there is a listing
                              of alternative loans on the website.
                           
                           
                           103 Wilder Tower
                           
                           901-678-4825
                           
                           http://www.memphis.edu/financialaid/
                           
                           financialaid@memphis.edu
                           
                           
                              
                              Monday - Friday 8:00am - 4:30pm
                              
                              MIFA Emergency
                              
                              Assistance with rent, utilities, food, clothing, and other necessities.
                              
                              910 Vance Ave, Memphis, TN 38126
                              
                              901-527-0208
                              
                              www.mifa.org/programs
                              
                           
                           
                           Neighborhood Christian Center Memphis
                           
                           
                              
                              Assistance with rent and utilities
                              
                              785 Jackson Ave Memphis, TN 38107
                              
                              901-881-6013
                              
                              www.ncclife.org
                              
                           
                           
                           Shelby County CSA
                           
                           
                              
                              Assistance with bill payment and rent for low income homes and provides up to $600
                                 until funds are exhausted.
                              
                              
                              3772 S Hickory Ridge Mall, #516 Memphis, TN 38115
                              
                              901-222-4315
                              
                              Monday – Friday 8:00 a.m.-4:30 p.m.
                              
                           
                           
                           Society of St. Vincent de Paul
                           
                           
                              
                              Work with up to a dozen local churches across the county to provide financial assistance
                                 for residents of Memphis.
                              
                              
                              3522 Carnes Memphis TN 38111
                              
                              901-722-4703 or 901-274-2137
                              
                              svdpmemphis1306@gmail.com
                              
                           
                           
                           Utility Assistance (MLGW)
                           
                           
                              
                              We realize that our customers sometimes have extenuating circumstances that prevent
                                 the timely payment of utility bills. To help ease this burden, we've developed the
                                 following payment and assistance programs, as well as extension policies.
                              
                              
                              Customer Care Center 901-544-6549 then press 2
                              
                              http://www.mlgw.com/residential/assistanceprograms
                              
                              http://www.mlgw.com/images/content/files/pdf/SpecialAssistancePrograms.pdf
                              
                           
                           
                           SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program)
                           
                           
                              
                              The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program provides nutritional assistance benefits
                                 to children and families, the elderly, the disabled, unemployed and working families.
                              
                              
                              40 South Main Street Memphis, TN 38103
                              
                              901-543-7351
                              
                              Monday-Friday 7:00 am - 4:30 pm
                              
                              http://www.tennessee.gov/humanservices/topic/supplemental-nutrition-assistance-program-snap
                              
                           
                           
                           Modest Needs
                           
                           
                              
                              A national non-profit organization that accepts online application for modest one-time
                                 financial assistance
                              
                              
                              https://www.modestneeds.org/
