                     B&F Areas of Interest for Faculty and Staff
                     
                     B&F Departments Looking for a B&F department? All top-level departments are listed in the left menu.
                        In addition, all top-level and internal departments are listed in the department directory.
                     
                     
                     B&F Forms As part of our ongoing efforts to better serve our users, the Division of Business
                        & Finance has incorporated all of its online forms into one convenient Web site.
                     
                     
                     Your University ID - Campus Card The University of Memphis issues each student, faculty and staff member an identification
                        card that bears their name and image. This permanent identification card is used as
                        the primary campus-wide method of determining privileges and accesses.
                     
                     
                     Crisis Management The Office of Crisis Management is responsible for developing and implementing programs
                        and projects in emergency planning, training, response, and recovery.
                     
                     
                     Employee Benefits Find out more about the comprehensive benefits program offered to University employees
                        at the Employee Benefits Web site.
                     
                     
                     Employee Discounts The University of Memphis offers a number of discounts to faculty and staff.
                     
                     
                     Focus on Finance The Focus of Finance users group was created as a communication and networking resource
                        for those within the University community who provide financial services throughout
                        their respective areas. Focus on Finance provides opportunities for training, information
                        sharing, and questions.
                     
                     
                     HR Partners HR Partners works to serve as the communications liaison between the University's
                        Department of Human Resources and members of the University community.
                     
                     
                     Holiday Schedule Throughout the year numerous dates are observed as University holidays for faculty
                        and staff.
                     
                     
                     Physical Plant Work Order Request System (WORQ) Use the WORQ System to contact the Physical Plant Department for problems concerning
                        the operation and maintenance University facilities.
                     
                     
                     Shared Services Center The Shared Services Center (SSC) is an office providing a range of finance, human
                        resources, and payroll services to campus administrative divisions.
                     
                     
                     Tigerbuy Tigerbuy is the University's online bidding and eprocurement system. Learn more about
                        gaining access to Tigerbuy on the Tigerbuy Program Guide .
                     
                     
                     WorkforUM WorkforUM is the University's online position description and applicant tracking system.
                        Search for employment opportunities in WorkforUM!
