			  You've Earned Your Diploma.
Now Graduate To Our Network.
Join Young Alumni.
Congratulations! You successfully completed one of the most challenging, fun and rewarding experiences of your life. It was worth all the late nights studying in McWherter Library, the quick lunches in the Tiger Den between classes and the hours of searching for a parking spot to make it to class before you heard the train horn. You’ve graduated from the University of Memphis!
What now? First, join Young Alumni! The UofM Alumni Association introduced the Young Alumni program for recent grads trying to get their foot in the door and their career off the ground. Join fellow grads to network and make professional connections while giving back to your alma mater.

For just $25 a year, Young Alumni members can:

    Meet other recent grads at various events
    Cheer on our Tigers at Young Alumni watch parties
    Network with UofM grads who are established in their careers
    Volunteer at events and help raise money for the Young Alumni Legacy Scholarship
    Access the UMAA Network (member directory, job postings, class notes and more)
    Take advantage of career services and coaching opportunities
    

*UofM graduates who received their diploma within the past five years are eligible.
Membership has its benefits.

    Save with discounts and special offers from hundreds of local and national retailers
    Discounts on UofM Grad School textbooks and Tiger gear
    Insurance program offering premium pricing
    Email365: Memphis Alumni email account

For more information, contact alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.2586.
