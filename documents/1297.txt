                     Graduate Assistantships
                     
                     Graduate teaching and research assistantships are available in most of the academic
                        areas of The University of Memphis, requiring 3-20 hours of service per week. Most
                        assistantships are provided by the student's home academic unit. Contact the department directly for additional information . Graduate assistants who work at least 10 hours per week are classified as in-state
                        students for fee-paying purposes for the term of their appointment as graduate assistants
                        only. Graduate assistantship contracts filed by the fourth day of class are eligible
                        for a scholarship equal to the amount of tuition and fees. Be certain to review the responsibilities of a graduate assistant . To learn how to upload your resume when seeking an assistantship please click here .  A student must be admitted into a graduate academic program to be eligible for appointment
                        as a graduate assistant.
                     
                     
                     Nonresident assistants appointed for the preceding spring semester are eligible for
                        in-state fees for summer, whether or not the student holds an assistantship in that
                        summer term. 
                     
                     
                     Non-degree students may not be awarded an assistantship. Graduate assistants must
                        maintain a 3.00 GPA to retain their assistantships. TN eCampus Fees are not covered by graduate assistantship waivers.   UM Online Program Fees are not covered by graduate assistantships or any other fee
                           waivers.
                     
                     Graduate teaching assistants must have a master's in the teaching discipline or 18
                        graduate credit hours in the discipline. GAs must have direct supervision by a faculty
                        member experienced in the teaching discipline, regular in-service training, and planned
                        and periodic evaluations. Non-native English speakers must also pass the SPEAk test
                        before they can be appointed teaching assistants. Contact the Center for International
                        Programs and Services for information on when the SPEAK test is administered. Teaching
                        assistants may teach only undergraduate courses; in exceptional cases they may be
                        approved by the Dean of the Graduate School  to teach 4000/6000 courses. They may
                        not teach more than 6 hours of for-credit courses without prior approval from the
                        Dean of the Graduate School.
                     
                     
                     UofM/Church Health Center's Memphis Plan for Graduate Assistants
                     
                     The University of Memphis has partnered with Church Health Center to offer Memphis
                        Plan health coverage to all domestic graduate assistants. Please make certain to carefully read your email announcing your new eContract for
                           the coming semester for instructions as to how to apply for the UofM/Church Health
                           Center Memphis Plan for Graduate Assistants. You will be required to authorize payroll deduction for the student portion of plan
                        costs in order to participate in the plan. Access to both the plan application, and
                        a way to electronically accept payroll deduction, will be included in the aforementioned
                        email. Alternately, a link to both is provided for eligible GAs in their MyMemphis
                        portal, next to the link associated with their GA eContract.

                     All amounts are expressed as monthly, payroll deducted charges. Both the amounts a

                     To find out more information regarding the Memphis Plan, please visit the following
                        webpage: www.churchhealthcenter.org/memphisplan .
                     
                     
                     Plans
                     
                     Month to Month:  Student and University pays on a month by month basis for the semesters the student
                        selects in their application. DEADLINE: Please submit your application to CHC on the
                        23rd day of the second month prior to the desired coverage starting month. For example,
                        if you submit your application by November 23rd, your coverage (if your application
                        is accepted) will begin January 1st.  Academic Year: Coverage is for the academic year (October through May), billing will take place
                        September through April (at the end of each month). DEADLINE: Please send in your
                        application for this plan by August 23 rd to be eligible for full academic year coverage. Annual (Full Year): Coverage is for the full calendar year (beginning in October). Payroll deduction
                        will occur, in even amounts, September through April (at the end of each month). DEADLINE:
                        Please send in your application for this plan by August 23rd to be eligible for this
                        full calendar year coverage.
                     
                     
                     Ending your Memphis Plan coverage
                     
                     Plan termination requests must be submitted to the MEMPHIS Plan office using the termination
                        form supplied at the time of enrollment. Terminations will be effective the first
                        of the month after the termination form is received by the MEMPHIS Plan office. Please
                        make certain the Memphis plan office has your termination form well before you graduate
                        or your GA contract ends.
