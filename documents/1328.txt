                     Thesis/Dissertation Preparation Guide
                     
                     The Graduate School Thesis/Dissertation Preparation Guide sets forth requirements for preparing and submitting master's theses and doctoral
                        dissertations to the Electronic Thesis and Dissertation (ETD) archival system.
                     
                     
                     Students must have successfully defended their document and have it approved by their
                        committee before submitting an electronic copy for review.
                     
                     
                     Do not submit your document to the ETD system until AFTER the Graduate School review.
                        Make ALL additional corrections (if any), convert your document to PDF and then submit
                        your electronically.
                     
                     
                     REQUIRED FORMS FOR REVIEW PROCESS
                     
                     
                        
                        Final Committee Approval Form for Electronic Submission .This form must be submitted with your Review copy and NOT before.
                        
                        
                        Thesis/Dissertation Defense Announcement form  NOTE: This form must be submitted at least three weeks prior to the date of your defense.
                        
                        
                        Final Thesis/Dissertation Defense Results
                        
                        Comprehensive Exam Results Form (for Master's Students Only). The Graduate School is required to have a Comprehensive
                           Exam Results form for ALL master's degree seeking students. If you have not been administered
                           a separate oral and/or written comprehensive exam by your department, the oral defense
                           of your thesis will satisfy the comprehensive exam requirement. This form must be
                           submitted with your review copy.
