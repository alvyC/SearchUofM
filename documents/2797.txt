                     Proctor Services
                     
                     The Testing Center serves as a test center for outside schools or institutions that
                        offer online or correspondence courses.  These exams can be taken in our facility
                        as either paper & pencil or online.
                     
                     
                     **Please Note : All inquires regarding RODP testing for University of Memphis students must be directed
                        to the office of Extended Programs or Mary Crites at 901.678.5784. 
                     
                     
                     HOW TO SCHEDULE A PROCTORED EXAM
                     
                     
                        
                        To set up an exam to be proctored by the Testing Center, you must first contact your
                           school or institution to receive permission to take your test at the University of
                           Memphis.  If our centers contact information is required at that time, provide them
                           with the following:
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     University of Memphis Testing Center
                     
                     112 Brister Hall/Wilder Tower
                     
                     Attn: Kimberly Avant
                     
                     Phone: (901) 678-2428
                     
                     Fax: (901) 678-2745
                     
                     Email: kavant1@memphis.edu
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           It is the test taker's responsibility to ensure that your school or institution sends
                              the exam along with detailed proctoring and return instructions.  Test takers are
                              responsible for bringing an addressed and stamped envelope or paid courier envelope
                              and label if the test must be mailed back.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           Tests may be sent to us via mail, fax, email or courier service.  Please be advised
                              that USPS estimated times might be delayed by 1 or 2 days since the exam goes through
                              our campus mail system.  It is your responsibility to make sure the exam materials
                              are received by us in time to meet any exam deadlines put in place by your institution. 
                              Please schedule all tests at least 2 days prior to the requested testing date; all
                              tests must be complete by 3:30 pm.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           Once your are given permission to take your exam at the University, you may set up
                              an appointment during normal business hours with Kimberly Avant by contacting the office at 901.678.2428 or by email at kavant1@memphis.edu.   We
                              recommend that you contact the center to confirm the receipt of the exam prior to
                              your appointment.  Be sure to pay close attention to when your test must be received
                              by your institution and make your appointment with plenty of time to account for test
                              returned via USPS or a courier.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           The cost for proctoring services is  $35.00 per exam. Exam fees may be submitted through
                              our secure online payment system . (you must present a printed receipt in order to sit for your exam)
                        
                        
                        
                           Disability Related Accommodations :  Please note that the students home school or institution from where the exam originates
                              must directly provide the Testing Center with a request for accommodations.
