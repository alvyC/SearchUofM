Distinguished Alumni Awards
The Distinguished Alumni Awards are presented in the categories of achievement and
service. The purpose of the Distinguished Alumni Award is to publicly recognize alumni
for distinguished personal and career accomplishments and for exemplary contributions
to society that bring credit to the University of Memphis. Additionally, the Distinguished
Alumni Award publicly honors alumni who have performed distinguished service for The
University of Memphis through cumulative contributions or by an extraordinary act
or effort that advances the University.

Outstanding Young Alumni
Outstanding Young Alumni Awards publicly honor alumni who are 40 years old or younger
for their outstanding personal and career achievements and contributions to society
that are a credit to the University. These awards are given only in years when a deserving
recipient is identified.

distinguished service for the University of Memphis through cumulative contributions
or by an extraordinary act or effort that advances the University. These awards are
given only in years when deserving recipients are identified.
