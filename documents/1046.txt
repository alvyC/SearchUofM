                     Training
                     
                     OIE offers a number of online, anytime training modules. To access these trainings,
                        please visit Learning Curve .
                     
                     
                     Once there, follow these steps:
                     
                     
                        
                        Sign in to Learning Curve .
                        
                        
                        Click the "Class Schedule/Registration" menu item.
                        
                        Choose "Web-based" from the menu provided.
                        
                        You will see the trainings below listed among the choices.
                        
                        Click the Register button and a link to the training module will be e-mailed to you.
