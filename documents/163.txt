                     Apply for Undergraduate Admission
Students should choose the application that best fits their intentions. Applications are generally available approximately one year before the term the student wishes to enter.  If you applied to the University of Memphis previously, but did not enroll at that time, select the " Readmission Application " option to avoid paying the application fee a second time.  Readmission is available to those who previously enrolled at the University of Memphis and are now seeking to return.
Degree-Seeking Applications

First-Time Freshman Application

For applicants who have never attended another college or university


First-Time Transfer Application

For applicants with transfer credits from another college or university 


Readmission Application - Check the status of this application

For those who had previously been enrolled at the University of Memphis, but who have not been enrolled at the University for at least one Fall or Spring semester
Students who were previously enrolled at the University of Memphis are not required to pay an additional application fee
After logging in, select the Readmission Application (2-Readmit Undergraduate)


International Student Application - Check the status of this application

For applicants who plan to enroll at the University on a student visa.



Non-Degree-Seeking Applications

Non-Degree Application - Check the status of this application

Completing courses for personal enrichment, not to complete a degree
Select "Non-Degree" as your major on the application
No financial aid is available for Non-Degree status


Information for Transient Students

Currently enrolled at another post-secondary institution and plan to transfer your UofM course(s) back to that institution upon completion
Select "Non-Degree" as your major on the application
For more information, consult the Transient Student page.

Dual Enrollment Application

Contact your school counselor to see if your high school participates
