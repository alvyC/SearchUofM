                        IDT MS Checklist
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     Register for Classes - IDT Master's
                     
                     STEP 1: Select Classes
                     
                     Consult these resources as you prepare to register for classes each semester.
                     
                     
                        
                        M.S. Degree Planning Sheet
                        
                        M.S. Course Rotation Schedule
                        
                        Course Descriptions
                        
                     
                     
                     Questions?
                     
                     Contact your advisor or the ICL Graduate Coordinator .
                     
                     STEP 2: Enroll in Classes
                     
                     Register for classes before the  enrollment deadline .
                     
                     
                     Questions?
                     
                     Contact the Office of the Registrar at (901) 678-2810 or registrar@memphis.edu .
                     
                     STEP 3: Payment
                     
                     Pay tuition and fees before the  payment deadline .
                     
                     
                     Questions?
                     
                     Contact the Bursar's Office .
                     
                     
                     
                     Late Course Registration:  Obtain an electronic permit submitted by the department staff to add a course. Obtain
                           a Schedule Adjustment Form from the  COE Office of Graduate Programs . This form must be signed by the COE Associate Dean for Graduate Studies. Then walk
                           the documents to Records in Wilder Tower. Be prepared to pay fees at this time.
                     
                     Dropping Classes/Withdrawal Procedure:  The procedures and support forms needed for dropping classes or withdrawing from the
                              University after the last date specified on the University Calendar are available
                              on the Graduate School website . Bring the completed support form and required documentation to the COE Office of Graduate Programs , and obtain a Schedule Adjustment Form. This form must be signed by the COE Associate
                              Dean for Graduate Studies. Then walk the Schedule Adjustment Form to Records in Wilder
                              Tower.
