                     Welcome to the FedEx Institute of Technology
                     
                     
                     Success Requires Innovation
                     
                     The FedEx Institute of Technology is an advanced technology and research organization
                        that serves as the front door to the research infrastructure and innovation capabilities
                        of the University of Memphis. It functions as a catalyst for interdisciplinary research
                        and innovation in emerging technologies by supporting cross-campus research innovation
                        clusters in areas such as intelligent systems, drones, cyber security testing, biologistics,
                        autonomous vehicles, robotics, smart biomaterials, additive manufacturing and precision
                        medicine.
                     
                     
                     The Institute is also home to the University's intellectual property and patent repository,
                        which is the focal point of technology transfer and licensing operations. The Institute
                        supports regional community efforts to increase both the size and technical sophistication
                        of the regional technology workforce. Additionally, the University's Graduate School,
                        that serves appropriately 1,000 doctoral and 3,000 masters students in 118 graduate
                        and research programs across campus, is also located at the FedEx Institute of Technology.
                     
                     
                     The Institute enjoys a unique innovation partnership with FedEx, which sponsors the
                        Institute for advanced research purposes . The Institute is the central hub for efforts to maintain a cutting-edge innovative
                        environment in the Mid-South region. It also offers training and collaboration opportunities
                        in emerging technologies and related breakthrough concepts.
                     
                     
                     Technology Transfer
                     
                     The Office of Technology Transfer, housed in the FedEx Institute, is where you'll
                        hear about the next big phenomenon. We boldly invest in promising new innovations
                        that have the potential to be breakthrough commercial products. We also license valuable
                        intellectual property to local companies, clearing the way for them to exclusively
                        develop cutting-edge technologies. We are a revenue generator for the University and
                        the inventors that support and encourage development of new science and technology
                        innovations. The work we do is exciting and far reaching — impacting the region's
                        economy by influencing new business ventures, strengthening existing enterprises and
                        fueling remarkable new research.
                     
                     
                     Corporate Engagement
                     
                     Memphis is an idea city. It's an environment where people feel free to try all sorts
                        of innovations. Memphis has the foundation for innovation and our University has the
                        research capabilities. It takes both to make magic happen. The FedEx Institute is
                        proud to serve as a gateway to introduce business leaders to the University's vast
                        resources in research. Our corporate partnerships lead to groundbreaking collaborations
                        with faculty and employment opportunities for students. We work closely with a wide
                        contingent of corporate, government and non-profit leaders, decision makers and innovators
                        throughout Memphis and Shelby County.
