                     POSITION AS A CONFERENCE ASSISTANCE
                     
                     CONFERENCE ASSISTANT JOB SUMMARY:
                     
                     The Conference Assistant serves under the Assistant Director for Residence Education
                        and the Assistant Director of Conference Planning and Operations. The individual works
                        as an official representative of the Department of Residence Life & Dining Services,
                        the Department of Conference Planning and Operations, and The University of Memphis.
                     
                     
                     Click here to Apply Now
                     
                     Click here if Returning CA
                     
                     SUMMER EMPLOYMENT:
                     
                     Sunday, May 9, 2016 through Friday, August 19, 2016
                     
                     JOB DUTIES:
                     
                     
                        
                        Staff 24-hour Conference Housing desk.
                        
                        Staff check-in and check-out of Conference Housing guests including distribution and
                           collection of keys.
                        
                        
                        Inspect rooms to insure preparation before group arrival and after group departure.
                        
                        Work with Housekeeping Staff to insure satisfactory preparation of facilities.
                        
                        Assist in preparation of conference information materials and paperwork.
                        
                        Insure completion of registration paperwork by Conference Housing guests.
                        
                        Advise Asst. Director and Managers of all phases of conference daily activities.
                        
                        Evaluate conferences indicating problems and concerns as well as positive feedback.
                        
                        Work assigned desk shifts as necessary. The work schedule will be determined in advance
                           and will include evening and weekend work as determined by the conference schedule.
                        
                        
                        Be available in the residence halls for scheduled duty nights.
                        
                        Respond to emergency situations.
                        
                        Assist Asst. Director and Managers with other duties as required.
                        
                        Present a good image of The University of Memphis by means of a tidy appearance, a
                           pleasant and helpful disposition, and a positive attitude and responsive action.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     REQUIREMENTS:
                     
                     
                        
                        Must be a student enrolled at The University of Memphis.
                        
                        Must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.25
                        
                        Must live in assigned residence hall during summer months. As a live-in staff member,
                           the conference assistant will be expected to be accessible in person and/or by telephone
                           to handle questions, respond to emergency situations and perform other administrative
                           tasks as required.
                        
                        
                        Must have an answering machine or personal cell phone to be used as work contact.
                        
                        Flexible hours to coincide with conference schedule.
                        
                        Must wear appropriate conference attire.
                        
                        May only take one summer school class per session. Preference may be given to those
                           who are not enrolled in summer school.
                        
                        
                        May not be employed at another job on or off campus.
                        
                     
                     
                     COMPENSATION:
                     
                     
                        
                        Stipend of $1,160.00 plus an hourly rate of $7.25 for hours worked at the desk. Free
                           room.
                        
                        
                        If you have any further questions about the Summer Conference Assistant position or
                           about Summer Conference Housing, please call 678-1674.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     CA SELECTION TIMELINE-SUMMER 2016
