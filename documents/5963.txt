                      Position Summary
                        The instructor will teach undergraduate/graduate courses, participate in curriculum development and improvement, and advise students.  Research in CS related areas is a plus.
                    
                    
                      Minimum Position Qualifications
                        An M.S. or PhD degree in computer science or a related field and University level teaching experience is required.
                    
                    
                      Special Conditions
                        Initial appointment will be for one academic year.  Position may be extended up to three years based on performance, department needs and funding availability.  Screening will begin March 1, 2017 and may continue until the position is filled, subject to budgetary approvals.
                    
                    
                      Posting Date

                        No
                    
                    
                      Hiring Range
                        Competitive
                    
                    
                      Full-Time/Part-Time
                        Full-Time: Benefits Eligible
                    
                    
                      Special Instructions to Applicants
                        Submit applications at https://workforum.memphis.edu.  Include cover letter, vitae, a statement of teaching philosophy and a minimum of three references.  A background check will be required for employment.  Direct inquiries to Kendra Tillis (ktillis@memphis.edu).
                    
                    
                      Instructions to Applicant regarding references
                        Dear Applicant,
Please be sure to provide an e-mail address of three reference providers as they will be required to submit their reference letter electronically in the system.
