                     Disabled Graduates
                     
                     If any graduate needs special assistance including, but not limited to, mobility impairments,
                           please notify both the Commencement office (901.678.5547) and Disability Resources
                           for Students (901.678.2880) as soon as possible. Both offices will reach out to you
                           with any information, if needed. The Commencement Office will reach out to your college
                           faculty marshal and student marshal to inform them of your accommodation request. 
                     
                     Disabiilty Resources for Students
                     
                     110 Wilder Tower 901.678.2880 memphis.edu/drs
                     
                     Parking and seating information 
                     
                     
                     If you need special accommodations, please notify the Commencement hours at least
                        4 weeks prior to your graduation to ensure all of your questions have been asked and
                        any accommodations will be made in a timely fashion before your Commencement event.
                        The FedExForum stage has a ramp and stage lift for any graduates with mobility issues. 
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        A specially designated drop-off location for disabled patrons is located on the north
                           side of FedExForum, off the Beale Street alley which can be reached by turning eastbound
                           off Third Street, which runs one-way north. 
                        
                        
                        Parking for guests with disabilities is available in the FedEx Forum parking garage,
                           and the Linden Avenue marshalling area. All vehicles must have a current state-issued
                           hang tag or license plate.
                        
                        
                        Handicapped Services, including wheelchairs, can be obtained through the FedEx Forum
                           Customer Service Department or by visiting the Commencement table, both located in
                           the Grand Lobby. Elevators are available.
                        
                        
                        Seating for hearing impaired is reserved in seating section 115. An interpreter for
                           the deaf is present at all ceremonies. The first row is reserved and only one guest
                           is permitted to sit with the guest in this row.
