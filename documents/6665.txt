                     Guests, Retiree and Sponsored Accounts
                     
                     We welcome you to The Department of Biological Sciences. If you are our guest as a
                        Seminar Speaker please note the following:
                     
                     
                     You will be presenting in Ellington Hall 100 auditorium. It is outfitted with a top
                        end PC, HD ceiling mounted projector, microphone for the presenter, and DVD ability
                        via the PC. A laptop VGA cable to projector is available as is a sound cable (separate
                        and requires a sound card in the laptop not combined with the video card).
                     
                     
                     **If you are presenting with a Mac laptop please bring with you the adapter you will require to connect to a VGA video cable.
                        Your Mac must be able to connect (audio out) to a cable separate from the video card.
                     
                     
                     As with any technology, if not using the standard equipment available it is highly
                        recommended that you also bring your presentation on a USB key as a back up plan.
                     
                     
                     Tech support is available for Thursday seminar setup at 3:30 pm for 4:00 pm seminar
                        in Ellington 100 or Ellington 101 by scheduled appointment or by calling 901.678.8888.
                     
                     
                     If you would like to contact Technical Support prior to your visit please call 901-678-8888.
                     
                     Retiree accounts
                     
                     Please visit iam.memphis.edu to activate your retiree email account, All other retiree
                        benefits are managed thru Human Resources.
                     
                     
                     Sponsored accounts
                     
                     Please contact 678-8888 to renew or initiate a sponsored account.
