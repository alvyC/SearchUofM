                     Welcome
                     
                     An MBA that takes you to the next level.
                     
                     The Executive MBA program at the University of Memphis transforms already accomplished
                        business professionals into proven business leaders.  This intensive 17-month, Fall-to-Fall
                        program is exclusive to professionals with five-plus years of management experience
                        and an established record of excellence in their field.
                     
                     
                     During the executive mba program.
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Students may continue to work full-time (* Full-time employment not a requirement for admission )
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Attend class once per week with an alternating Thursday evening and Saturday schedule
                              (* Enrolled for 12 credit hours during the Fall and Spring semesters )
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Experience a high-level of service from this all-inclusive program that frees you
                              to focus on learning.  All-inclusive covers: advising; registration; tuition and fees;
                              textbooks; software; case studies/learning materials; premiere parking; room/board
                              for two domestic residencies and flight, hotels and most meals for the international
                              residential week; and, catered meals on class days
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Interact in a dynamic classroom setting that brings together faculty who have outstanding
                              academic reputations and achievements, with a highly-skilled student body who are
                              leaders in their companies
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Build your network with other business leaders from various industries through executive
                              lecture events and unique collaboration opportunities
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Beyond the program...The Return oN Investment.
                     
                     As a result, senior executives learn savvy business techniques that have real-world
                        impact and further enhance their leadership skills.  Factor in the international experience
                        and challenging curriculum taught from a strategic viewpoint, makes the University
                        of Memphis Executive MBA Program as exciting as it is comprehensive.
