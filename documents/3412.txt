                     SGA Travel Funding
                     
                     As a service to students, SGA provides travel funding for University organizations
                        and individuals.
                     
                     
                     ATTENTION :  SGA travel funds are awarded as a reimbursement after the travel is completed .  If you receive funding from SGA and any other source you must meet with Mrs. Julia Rhodes in the Dean of Students Office prior to travel.
                         Mrs. Rhodes may be reached at 901.678.2188 or via email at  jrhodes@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     To be eligible for Student Government Association travel funds you must be a student
                              during the fall or spring semester in which you travel.  If travel occurs during the
                              summer you must either be registered for summer classes or have been registered for
                              the spring semester and be registered for the following fall semester.  If you have
                              graduated you are NOT eligible for Student Government Association travel funds.
                     
                     To apply for funding:
                     
                     Undergraduate students may download application and then submit completed application to the Student Government
                        Association Office, 214 University Center, prior to the application deadline.  Applications
                        will not be accepted after the deadline.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Undergraduate Travel Application
                        
                     
                     
                     Graduate and Law students may download application and then submit completed application to the Graduate School,
                        303 FedEx Institute of Technology, prior to the application deadline.  Applications
                        will not be accepted after the deadline.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Graduate / Law School Travel Application
                        
                     
                     
                     Lambuth students may download application and then submit completed application to the Wilder Student
                        Union 2A, prior to the application deadline. Applications will not be accepted after
                        the deadline.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Lambuth Travel Application
                        
                     
                     
                     Questions: Undergraduate and Lambuth Travel: Mrs. Julia Rhodes  |  901.678.2187  |   jrhodes@memphis.edu
                     
                     Graduate and Law Travel: Yasuko Kanamori  |  901.678.1408  |   yknamori@memphis.edu
                     
                     DEADLINES FOR APPLICATION (for both Undergraduate and Graduate/Law):
                     
                     
                        
                        Domestic Travel (continental United States) fifteen (15) business days in advance of departure date
                        
                        
                        Hawaii and Alaska eight (8) weeks in advance of departure date
                        
                        
                        International Travel eight (8) weeks in advance of departure date
                        
                        
                        Canada and Mexico eight (8) weeks in advance of departure date
                        
                        
                        If travel falls during the winter holiday break or during the break between spring
                           and summer terms or immediately following the start of a semester. It is recommended
                           that the application be submitted before the fifteen (15) business day deadline. If
                           travel is to occur during or shortly after a break the application should be submitted
                           earlier.
