                     Para Pro Assessment
                     
                     The ParaPro Assessment is a computerized exam that measures the reading, writing and basic math competencies of practicing and prospective paraprofessionals.  In accordance with the No Child Left Behind Act, the ParaPro Assessment can be used to certify teacher's aides and assistants in the instruction of these subjects to school children.
                     
                     
                     Registration Information
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Cost: $35 Test Administration Fee - payable through our secure online payment system + a  $55 Exam Fee - payable on test day by Credit/Debit only- all testing fees are non-refundable &
                              non-transferable.  Exam fee must be paid and your confirmation receipt must be submitted to Kimberly
                                 Avant via email or fax ( kavant1@memphis.edu or (901)678-2745) prior to making your appointment.
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Examines are to report to Brister Hall 112 at least 15 minutes prior to your testing
                              time. Please be prompt - Late arrivals will not be admitted.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Exams are given Monday – Friday by Appointment Only.  To schedule an appointment,
                              please contact the UofM Testing Center by calling 901.678.2428 or emailing Kimberly
                              Avant ( kavant1@memphis.edu ) .
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           For detailed information regarding the ParaPro Assessment and to purchase preparation
                              materials, please visit the ParaPro Assessment website: http://www.ets.org/parapro
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     What to Bring
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           A non-expired Government issued photo ID is required and must be presented prior to
                              testing. Your photo ID must contain a signature and match the name you used when registering
                              exactly - NO EXCEPTIONS.  Acceptable forms of photo identification are: State-issued driver's license, State-issued ID card, Passport, Military ID card
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Acceptable Credit/Debit card so that your $55.00 exam fee can be processed through
                              the ETS ParaPro Assessment system; acceptable forms of payment are a valid Credit/Debit
                              card that displays the MasterCard, Visa or American Express logo. Please Note:  To
                              avoid the possibility of your payment being denied - you must know the address that
                              is linked to your credit/debit card.
