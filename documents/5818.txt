                        About Greg Morris
                        
                        Greg Morris, Suzuki Violin Instructor and Suzuki String Coordinator for the Community
                           Music School. Mr. Morris has been a faculty member of the Suzuki String Program since
                           1980. He holds the B.M. from the University at Albany and the M.M. in violin pedagogy
                           from the University of Memphis. He is a member of the first violin section of Memphis
                           Symphony Orchestra and Summer Symphony of Lakeside, OH. Mr. Morris performs on both
                           violin and viola in chamber music ensembles. He studied Suzuki pedagogy in Matsumoto,
                           Japan and with John Kendall and Yuko Honda
