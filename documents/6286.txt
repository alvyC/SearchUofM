                     PAST EVENTS
                     
                     APRIL 27, 2016 – RETIREMENT PARTY FOR DR. DONALD FRANCESCHETTI  
                     
                     Please join us to celebrate Dr. Donald Franceschetti’s 35 years of service at University
                        of Memphis, 4/27, 4 pm, Manning Hall.  Please RSVP by April 1st https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/ZMLT9QP
                     
                     If you would like to send cards or messages for Dr. Franceschetti, they will be delivered
                        to him then. 
                     
                     
                     MARCH 23, 2016 - SPS TRIVIA NIGHT - The 3rd Annual Trivia Night sponsored by the Society of Physics Students will be
                        on Wednesday, March 23, 2016, 6-8 pm, in the River Room, UC 300. This fun even includes
                        questions on science, pop culture, and general knowledge. Come see student research
                        poster sessions! Enjoy free pizza and cookies! More Information
                     
                     APRIL1, 2016 - FIRST LOIS MCGLOTHLIN DONALDSON ENDOWED LECTURE IN PHYSICS - The Department has received its first endowment to support an annual lecture in
                        physics. At least every four years the topic will be in astrophysics. Dr. Eric Chaisson,
                        the inaugural speaker from Harvard University, will deliver his lecture, "Cosmic Evolution
                        and the Rise of Complexity in Nature," on April 1, 2016 in the UC Theater. Reception
                        at 6:00 pm, lecture at 6:30 pm. Learn about Lois McGlothlin Donaldson and this memorial
                        endowed lecture at http://www.memphis.edu/physics/news/lois-mcglothlin-donaldson.php
