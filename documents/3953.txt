      Posting Details
      
      
           

              Posting Information
              
              
                    
                      Posting Number
                        HMAE1109
                    
                    
                      Advertised Title
                        Office Associate
                    
                    
                      Campus Location
                        Main Campus (Memphis, TN)
                    
                    
                      Position Number
                        001110
                    
                    
                      Category
                        Staff (Hourly/Monthly)
                    
                    
                      Department
                        University Libraries
                    
                    
                      Minimum Position Qualifications
                        Requires an Associate’s Degree or two years of college, and at least two years of administrative support experience.
Candidates must have the ability to type 45 wpm; possess computer experience with proficiency in various office production software such as word processing, spreadsheet, and database management.
                    
                    
                      Special Conditions
                        The department is especially interested in candidates with excellent customer service skills.
                    
                    
                      Work Schedule
                        Monday Friday
8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
                    
                    
                      Posting Date
                        10/26/2016
                    
                    
                      Closing Date
                        11/09/2016
                    
                    
                      Open Until Screening Begins
                        No
                    
                    
                      Hiring Range
                        $10.10 - $14.29 per hour
                    
                    
                      Full-Time/Part-Time
                        Full-Time: Benefits Eligible
                    
                    
                      Working Conditions
                        While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to sit; use hands to handle, or feel; and talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to stand, walk, and reach with hands and arms. The employee is occasionally required to stoop, kneel, or crouch. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 10 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision.
                    
                    
                      Additional Working Conditions
                        This position functions in an office environment within a technologically oriented academic research library where the Administrative Office provides service and support for the entire organization.  Position has an extensive public service component requiring regular interaction with the general public as well as with the faculty and staff of the University Libraries. Requires ease with face-to-face, telephone, and online communications. Position requires some standing, walking, bending, and reaching. Incumbent must be able to bend, stoop, and lift up to 20 pounds. Responsibilities may require that the incumbent push and/or pull wheeled book trucks weighing up to 300 lbs., when full.  Might be required to climb on step ladders and reach overhead. Position requires vision that will support close detailed work and hearing that will support telephone and online communications.
                    
                    
                      Special Instructions to Applicants
                        All applications must be submitted online at workforum.memphis.edu.
Applicants must complete all applicable sections of the online application in order to be considered for a position. Please upload a cover letter, resume, and a list with five (5) work related references after completing your application.
Candidates who are called for an interview must notify the Department of Human Resources in writing of any reasonable accommodation needed prior to the date of the interview.
                    
                    
                      Is this posting for UofM employees only?
                        No
                    
                    
                      Positions Supervised
                        May supervise student assistants, as needed. May assist with training of other staff. May be designated team leader for certain projects.
                    
                
           
           

              Job Duties
              The duties and responsibilities listed are intended to describe the general nature and level of work to be performed in this position and are not to be construed as an exhaustive list of the requirements of this job.
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Provide Administrative and clerical support to the seven (7) department heads in the University Libraries. Includes tasks such as: preparing correspondences, reports, bulletins, and other documents; scheduling meetings; preparing materials for meetings; processing travel requests and reimbursements; preparing presentations; .
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Provides administrative and clerical support for the Associate Dean of University Libraries. Includes tasks such as:  calendar management; and compiling statistics; assisting with preparation of reports; assist with file management; assist with assessment projects and activities.
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Provides administrative and clerical support to the Office Manager  for the Office of the Dean of University Libraries.  Includes tasks such as: Preparing correspondence and reports; gathering and compiling statistics; assists with managing the document retention plan; assists with maintaining the document filing and retrieval system of the office.
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Participates in providing general administrative and clerical support to address library-wide needs. Tasks include: helps with staffing the office front desk; sends, receives, and distributes faxes; assists with large mailings or other distributions; assists with meeting logistics and preparation; replenishes paper and toner in copy machines or printers; and process requests to use  library meeting rooms
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Maintains a variety of records, electronic or manual file systems, or database files requiring classification and compilation of varied information.
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Plans and prioritizes workload to meet set deadlines.
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Recommends changes to procedures affecting assigned function.
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Participates in library-wide projects and activities such serving on committees and task forces, participating in available  training.
                    
                
                
                    
                      Duties & Responsibilities
                      Performs other duties as assigned
