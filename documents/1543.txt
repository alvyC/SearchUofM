                           	About
                           	
                        
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     Keeping up with what today's students look for in college.
                     
                     The Associate Vice President for Student Affairs/Dean of Students is responsible for
                        all aspects of Student Life. Many of the programs and services within Student Life
                        create and nurture the kind of environment that helps our students fit in and find
                        where they belong. The departments under Student Life include: Adult and Commuter
                        Student Services, Conference and Event Services (University Center/Michael D. Rose
                        Theatre/Scheduling Office), Office of Multicultural Affairs, Office of Student Conduct,
                        Student Government Association, Student Leadership and Involvement (Fraternity & Sorority
                        Affairs, Frosh Camp, Leadership Programs including Emerging Leaders, Student Activities,
                        Student Organizations) and Veterans Resource Center. The Associate Vice President
                        for Student Affairs and Dean of Students also directly oversees liaisons with the
                        Religious Life organizations and is the administrative connection with the Student
                        Government Association.
