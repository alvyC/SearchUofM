                     Travel and Lodging
                     
                     Directions to Institute:
                     
                     Physical Address: University of Memphis FedEx Institute of Technology 365 Innovation Drive, Suite 303 Memphis, TN 38152
                     
                     
                     The FedEx Institute of Technology (FIT) is located on the University of Memphis campus
                        on 365 Innovation Drive. Innovation Drive is located off Central Avenue. We are right
                        across from the Fogelman Executive Center Parking garage.
                     
                     
                     When you turn onto Innovation Drive, you will see the parking garage on the left,
                        and the FedEx Institute of Technology will be on the right. The FedEx Drone Expo will
                        be held on the first floor of the Institute. 
                     
                     
                     Hotel Accommodations:
                     
                     The Fogelman Executive Center is located across the street from the FedEx Institute and is the closest hotel. Free
                        shuttle service to and from Memphis International Airport is included.  Book by March 18th to receive the conference rate!
                     
                     
                     Conference Rate: $90.00 + tax
                     
                     To reserve room: Call (901) 678-5410
                     
                     Physical Address: Fogelman Executive Center and Hotel 360 Innovation Drive, Suite 303 Memphis, TN 38152
                     
                     
                     Alternative Hotel: Holiday Inn University of Memphis Campus
