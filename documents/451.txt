                     Welcome
                     
                     Welcome to the Department of Management in the Fogelman College of Business and Economics
                        at the University of Memphis. The mission of our department is to enhance the business
                        and academic careers of our students by providing rigorous and relevant management
                        curricula.
                     
                     
                     We offer courses that are strengthened by our faculty's research programs and consulting
                        experience.  Through our BBA, professional MBA, executive MBA, international MBA,
                        customer-driven MBA, and online MBA programs, we prepare students for careers in a
                        globally competitive and technology-driven marketplace. Through our PhD program, we
                        prepare students for faculty positions at research-oriented academic institutions.
                     
                     
                     We contribute to the intellectual development of the management discipline through
                        rigorous and relevant scientific research. Our faculty members are nationally and
                        internationally recognized for their research that they publish in premier scholarly
                        journals.  We contribute to the business community’s development through consulting,
                        employee training and development, and other professional outreach activities.
                     
                     
                     Our faculty members have diverse areas of expertise including human resource management,
                        organizational behavior, leadership, organization theory, strategic management, international
                        management, entrepreneurship and innovation, business communication, and organizational
                        research methods. Our faculty hold doctoral degrees from universities such as Case
                        Western Reserve, Florida, George Mason, Georgia State, Illinois, Kentucky, LSU, Ohio
                        State, Penn State, SUNY-Albany, Texas, Texas A&M, Tulane, and Vanderbilt.
                     
                     
                     Please browse our web pages for more information about our faculty, PhD students,
                        degree programs, and research programs.  If you have any questions, feel free to contact
                        me.  Thank you for visiting the Department of Management in the Fogelman College of
                        Business and Economics at the University of Memphis.
