                     Accommodations
                     
                     The FedEx Institute of Technology is a $23 million, state-of-the-art facility like
                        no other. The 187-seat, tiered amphitheater boasts the second largest implementation
                        of digital congress units outside the United Nations, and is complimented by an 85-seat
                        high definition presentation theater and 17 meeting rooms. Large projection screens,
                        web cams, touch panel screens, laptop computers, totally wireless network and video
                        conferencing are just some of the cutting-edge features of this facility.
                     
                     
                     Conference housing accommodations are available from late May through early August
                        for both on- and off-campus groups seeking affordable housing, excellent dining services,
                        and a variety of meeting areas located both indoors and outdoors on campus. Centennial
                        Place, our newest residence hall, is the PRIMARY location for conference housing on
                        the University of Memphis Campus. Click Here for additional information and a conference
                        housing application.
                     
                     
                     The Fogelman Executive Conference Center & Hotel sets the benchmark for meeting planner
                        expectation. This International Association of Conference Centers approved full-service
                        meeting facility offers 51 queen guest rooms, three amphitheaters: 2,580 sq ft. amphitheater,
                        3,572 sq ft theater, 1,425 theater, 11 conference rooms, and two dining rooms. Over
                        17,000 sq ft. of meeting space is available. Our award winning hotel, with its four
                        story atrium of rich woods and leather seating, offers 82 two-rooms suites, a restaurant,
                        private dining room, lounge, five meeting rooms and a 12,180 sq. ft. grand ballroom.
                        Over 14,000 sq. ft. of meeting space is available.
