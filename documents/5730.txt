                     Audition Information - Undergraduate
                     
                     Audition days are held to determine admission into School of Music programs and to
                        award talent-based/performance scholarships. All undergraduate students who want to
                        major or minor in music must audition. Auditions are scheduled by applying to the
                        School of Music through Acceptd . Students who do not intend to major or minor in music but want to apply for scholarship
                        must apply to the School of Music for Lessons Only and Scholarship and audition; a
                        very limited number of non-music majors or minors are awarded School of Music scholarships.
                     
                     
                     Live auditions are highly preferred.
                     
                     The School of Music will accept non-edited video recorded auditions from international
                        students who reside outside the United States. The preferred deadline for recorded
                        audition submission is December 1 and no later than January 1. Recorded auditions
                        must include appropriate repertoire listed in Audition Requirements. The Rudi E. Scheidt
                        School of Music reserves the right to require a live audition on campus if necessary.
                     
                     
                     Students who want to be a member of the marching band, the Mighty Sound of the South,
                        and do not plan to major or minor in music, may call 901-678-2263 to schedule a Marching
                        Band Audition.
                     
                     
                     Keep in mind that those seeking School of Music talent-based performance scholarships
                        are encouraged to apply to the School of Music by December 1, audition by the end
                        of February, and be accepted by the University of Memphis before their audition day.
                        Also remember that Incoming Freshmen who seek University of Memphis academic scholarships
                        must be accepted by the University by December 1.
                     
                     
                     AUDITION DAYS FOR 2017-2018
                     
                     Auditions are held on the following dates:
                     
                     December 3, 2016 February 4, 2017 February 18, 2017 February 25, 2017
                     
                     
                     Students who wish to audition for the marching band and its scholarships should pre-register
                        by calling the band office at 901.678.2263.  For information email  mightysound@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     First round auditions for the student ensemble Sound Fuzion will be held in April
                        2017 with recalls the following day. For information, interested students should email  soundfuzion@memphis.edu .
                     
                     
                     AUDITION DAY SCHEDULE
                     
                     Auditions are held at the University of Memphis Music Building. All auditionees should
                        plan to be available from 8:15 am until mid-afternoon. Auditionees are notified of
                        audition results by mail within two weeks after their audition, and are notified of
                        scholarship awards by mail in mid/late-March.
                     
                     
                     SAMPLE AUDITION DAY SCHEDULE
                     
                     8:30 am:  Check-in. Music Building, Harris Concert Hall Lobby 9:00 am:  General meeting for undergraduate auditionees, parents, and friends 9:15 am:  Music Theory and piano placements (if applicable) 9:15 am:  Meeting for parents and friends with faculty members 10:00 am:  Auditions begin. 10:30 am:  Music Industry Orientation for Music Business and Recording Technology
                        applicants, their family and friends 12:00 pm:  Meeting for all Music Education auditionees 12:00 pm:  Campus Tour 1:00 pm:  Aural Theory placements (transfer students only)
