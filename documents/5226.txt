                           Shelia Gaines, Department Head
                     
                     sgaines1@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-8201 Office: McWherter Library: RM 102K
                        
                        Responsibilities:  Manages operations related to personnel, borrowers, billing, Reserve Room, student
                           workers, equipment, and circulating collections. Shelia responds to inquiries, complaints,
                           and suggestions. She also maintains files, reports and statistics for the department.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Frankie M. Perry, Billing Associate fperry@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-4483 Office: McWherter Library: RM 102J
                        
                        Responsibilities: Frankie handles fines, maintains files, reconciles accounts, and acts as a liaison
                           to the Bursar's office. She handles accounts for Interlibrary Loan, Government Publications,
                           Reserve Room, branch libraries, and area colleges as well.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Sharon Tucker, Library Assistant III stucker@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-8248 Office: McWherter Library: RM 102F
                        
                        Responsibilities: As the Evening Supervisor, Sharon monitors evening activity, keeps stats, assigns
                           study carrels, and assists with Reserve Room processing.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Delaine Pegues Wilson, Library Assistant III
                     
                     dpegues@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-3311 Office: McWherter Library: RM 102E
                        
                        Responsibilities: In addition to public service assistance, Delaine compiles statistics for our Special
                           Privilege users, records student hours and completes their payroll. She also maintains
                           New Additions collection, orders supplies, maintains supply cabinet and assists with shelving.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Casey Parkman, Library Assistant III ckprkman@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2955 Office: McWherter Library: RM 102C
                        
                        Responsibilities: Casey is the reserves room manager in charge of course reserves, permanent reserves,
                           and circulating technology. He also maintains the 3D printers, leads 3D workshops,
                           handles scheduling for 3D lab and workshops, and maintains circulation website.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Greg Woods, Library Assistant II gwoods@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-5124 Office: McWherter Library: RM 102A
                        
                        Responsibilities: In addition to public service assistance, Greg checks book drops, monitors laptop
                           usage, compiles book log stats, does book repair, and assists with shelf maintenance
                           projects.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     ReKeycha Harris, Library Assistant I rharris1@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2205 Office: McWherter Library: Room 102
                        
                        Responsibilities: In addition to general Circulation duties (sorting and shelving books, assisting
                           at the public service desk, and executing projects essential to the library), ReKeycha
                           serves as a liaison to the Cataloging Department. ReKeycha also maintains the order
                           of the collection in Government Documents and the video collection on the second floor.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Sheneva Graham, Library Assistant I sgraham3@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2205 Office: McWherter Library; Room 102
                        
                        Responsibilities: In addition to general Circulation duties (sorting and shelving books, assisting
                           at the public service desk, and executing projects essential to the library), Sheneva
                           serves as a liaison to the Cataloging Department. She also works specifically to maintain
                           the order of the 3rd floor collection.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Javin Peete, Library Assistant I jmpeete@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2205 Office: McWherter Library; Room 102
                        
                        Responsibilities: In addition to general Circulation duties (sorting and shelving books, assisting
                           at the public service desk, and executing projects essential to the library), Javin
                           maintains the Brister storage extension and runs book drops.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Roderick Burns, Library Assistant I rsburns@memphis.edu Phone: (901) 678-2205 Office: McWherter Library; Room 102
