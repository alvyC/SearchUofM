                     Earthquake Resources
                     
                     
                     
                     CERI seeks to understand the puzzling absence of all but the most minor topographic
                        manifestations of the New Madrid seismic zone earthquakes, by refining and blending
                        techniques from geology, archaeology, civil engineering and soil science. This evolving
                        multidisciplinary process, now joined by investigators from several institutions,
                        is beginning to unravel the earthquake history of the region, putting the present
                        earthquake hazard in clearer perspective.
