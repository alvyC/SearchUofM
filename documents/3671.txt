                     When to Apply
                     
                     To receive priority consideration Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) as early as possible beginning October 1 st   (beginning with 2017-18 year).
                     
                     
                     DON'T DELAY!
                     
                     Entering in Fall?  Submit by February 1 st NOTE: The Tennessee Student Assistance Award (TSAA) deadline is January 17.  If you
                           are a Tennessee resident, are Pell eligible, pursuing a first bachelor's degree, and
                           meet other state criteria, you will lose out on receiving this grant if you complete
                           the FAFSA after the January 17th deadline.
                     
                     Entering in Spring? Submit by October 1 st
                     
                     Entering in Summer?  Submit by March 1 st
                     
                     
                     CHECK myMemphis OFTEN FOR MISSING REQUIREMENTS!
                     
                     PLEASE NOTE THE FOLLOWING:
                     
                     
                        
                        The 2016-2017 school year includes Fall 2016, Spring 2017, and Summer 2017.
                        
                        The 2017-2018 school year includes Fall 2017, Spring 2018, and Summer 2018.
                        
                        Summer aid requires the additional Summer Update Form located on the Financial Aid Forms page (available in early April each year).
                        
                        
                        June 30, 2017 is the deadline for the central processing agency to receive your FAFSA
                           for the 2016-2017 school year.
                        
                        
                        Failure to meet FAFSA and document submission deadlines will result in late payment
                              fees.
                        
                     
                     
                     Financial Aid Processing Timeline 2017-2018
                     
                     Your attention to this timeline will help to ensure your aid is processed prior to
                        your first day of class.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        October - Continuing and new students need to complete the FAFSA online for the 2017-2018 academic year (includes Fall 2017, Spring 2018 and Summer
                           2018).
                        
                        
                        November and December - Watch for your 2017-2018 Student Aid Report (results of your FAFSA filing). Pay
                           attention to any document request e-mail notices received from the Financial Aid Office.
                           SUBMIT REQUIRED DOCUMENTS IN A TIMELY FASHION.
                        
                        
                        New Freshmen/New Transfers
                        
                        
                           
                           January - View your Financial Aid Award for Fall 2017 on myMemphis under the Account$ tab. If necessary, change award years by choosing the Award Information by Year option. The Student Requirements section will indicate if you are missing any financial aid documents for a particular
                              award year. NOTE: The deadline to submit ALL required documents for the Fall term is APRIL 01
                           
                           March/April - Review your award information available on myMemphis . You can elect to 'Accept,' 'Accept Partial,' or 'Decline' any awards offered to
                              you
                           
                           
                           June - Check to be sure you have done everything you are supposed to do for the Fall 2017
                              semester. Have you submitted all missing documents?
                           
                           
                           July - It's TOO LATE NOW! You will NOT BE AWARDED UNTIL AFTER FALL 2017 registration in
                              mid-September. You will need to cover your own fees at this time and then be reimbursed
                              by your financial aid later assuming you meet the eligibility criteria to receive
                              aid.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Continuing Students
                           
                           
                              
                              May - View your Financial Aid Award for Fall 2017 on myMemphis under the Account$ tab. If necessary, change award years by choosing the Award Information by Year option. The Student Requirements section will indicate if you are missing any financial aid documents for a particular
                                 award year. NOTE: The deadline to submit ALL required documents for the Fall term is APRIL 01
                              
                              June - Check to be sure you have done everything you are supposed to do for the Fall 2017
                                 semester. Have you submitted all missing documents?
                              
                              
                              July - It's TOO LATE NOW! You will NOT BE AWARDED UNTIL AFTER FALL 2017 registration in
                                 mid-September. You will need to cover your own fees at this time and then be reimbursed
                                 by your financial aid later assuming you meet the eligibility criteria to receive
                                 aid.
