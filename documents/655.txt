                     Welcome to Parking & Transportation Services!
                     
                     Whether you are a visitor, alumnus, new student or new faculty/staff, we are glad
                        to see you on our campus. Parking & Transportation Services (PTS), an auxiliary unit
                        within the Department of Business Services, manages all University owned parking lots
                        and is responsible for administering the Parking and Traffic Regulations of the University
                        of Memphis and the Tennessee Board of Regents.
                     
                     The Parking and Transportation Services Office will be closed from 10:45 a.m. - 1:30 p.m. on Friday, November 11, 2016. We apologize for any inconvenience.
THE CENTRAL GENERAL LOT (LOT 44) HAS BEEN FILLING UP, BUT THERE IS NO SHORTAGE OF PARKING IN THE SOUTHERN GENERAL LOT (LOT 15). THERE HAVE BEEN AT LEAST 200 FREE SPACES EVERY DAY, AS WELL AS ADDITIONAL PARKING IN LOT 13.
Spring 2017 online permit sales begin Tuesday, December 6, 2016.
Please ensure your mailing address is current. You may do so in MyMemphis under your Student tab in the Personal Information portlet. More details on how to update your personal information can be found online .
