                     Magnetic Nanoparticles/Nanocomposites
                     
                     Dr. Sanjay R. Mishra
                     
                     Magnetic Materials
                     
                     
                        
                        Magnetic nanoparticles: spinel ferrites, hexaferrites
                        
                        Exchange Coupled Metallic Nanocomposites
                        
                        Exchange Coupled Oxide Nanocomposites
                        
                        Core-shell magnetic Nanocomposites
                        
                     
                     
                     Solar Energy Related Materials
                     
                     
                        
                        Core-shell oxide for photocatalysis
                        
                        Reusable photocatalysts
                        
                        Cu oxide based solar materials
                        
                        Surface Plasmon based photocatalysts
                        
                     
                     
                     Biomaterials
                     
                     
                        
                        Stimuli responsive Chitosan-ferrite composites
                        
                        CaP nanoshells for bone cancer treatment
                        
                     
                     
                     Resources
                     
                     
                        
                        Synthesis Laboratory: Furnaces, ball milling, ultrasonicator, arc melter, spin coater,
                           chemical hood and storage
                        
                        
                        Characterization: Bruker XRD, RT 1.5 T VSM, RT Mossbauer spectrometer, UV-vis spectrometer,
                           Raman Spectrometer, ellipsometer, 14 GHz Dielectric Analyzer, 100 MHz LCR Meter, viscometer,
                           zeta potential analyzer, Quantachrome BET surface area analyzer, 10 -300 K Resistivity
                           Meter, RT Magnetoresistance, DSC/TGA, High Resistivity meter, high temperature capacitance
                           analyzer.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     For more information, please contact Dr. Sanjay R. Mishra at srmishra@memphis.edu or 901.678.3115.
