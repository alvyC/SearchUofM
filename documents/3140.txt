  Welcome to THEPI!



    Welcome to the Web site of the Tennessee Higher Education Purchasing Initiative.The Tennessee
    Higher Education Purchasing Group was organized to affect improvements in purchasing processes
    that would provide expedited, reasonably priced sources for common goods/services required by
    member institutions.
    The THEPI group is made up of representatives from various institutions within the Tennessee
    Higher Education Institutions and the Boards represented. 
    This site is a central repository of contracts that permits use by members. Contracts are
    listed on this site by the issuing institution. Any questions regarding an agreement should
    be directed to the issuing institution.
