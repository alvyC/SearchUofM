	   Tiger Tailgates
Join the Alumni Association, 901 Club and M Club at the Pipkin Building on Tiger Lane before each Memphis Tigers Home Football Game! 
Cost: $8 for members. $10 for non-members
Includes BBQ and all the fixins
Members: Show your mobile membership card to save! 
Life Members: Receive complimentary entry to EVERY tailgate! (Membership dues must be current. Contact us to check your payment status.)
Saturday, November 12: Memphis Tigers vs. USF Knights, Time TBA
Friday, November 25: Memphis Tigers vs. Houston Cougars, Time TBA
The UofM Alumni Association is continuously planning pre-game parties, tailgates and
pep rallies for football, basketball, bowl games, tournaments and other UofM athletic
events. Visit this site often for updates on game day activities. Join us on your
way to see the Tigers play!
