                     Diploma Mailing Schedule
                     
                     If you have not received your diploma, please call 901-678-5547. Please call us prior
                        to coming to ensure your diploma is in the office. Diplomas are typically held for
                        2-3 years maximum, depending upon the nature of the reason the diploma was held. Please
                        call prior to coming. 
                     
                     
                     The Commencement Office's goal is to provide all graduates with diplomas in a timely
                        manner. Listed below are the diploma mailing schedules for each graduation term. 
                     
                     
                     Recent Ceremony Diplomas
                     
                     Recent ceremony diplomas are provided 2-3 months after each Commencement. Doctoral
                        diplomas will be given to participating students on Commencement day. 
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Diplomas for May graduates will be mailed by the Commencement Office by August 1 of
                           the same year.
                        
                        
                        Diplomas for August graduates will be mailed by the Commencement Office by November
                           1 of the same year.
                        
                        
                        Diplomas for December graduates will be mailed by the Commencement Office by March
                           1 of the next year.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     No diploma will be released to any student that has a financial hold or has not completed
                        requirements for certification. 
                     
                     
                     Individual Diploma Orders and Duplicate Reorders 
                     
                     
                        
                        Individual Diplomas
                           
                           
                              
                              I ndividual diplomas reordered for any reason will be available one week to two weeks
                                 from the day of inquiry. 
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Duplicate Reorders
                           
                           
                              
                              Once we receive your reorder request, it will take approximately two weeks for our
                                 vendor to process your diploma.
