                     Hall Staff
                     
                     Aretha Milligan Associate Director Residence Life Phone: 901.678.5913 Fax: 901.678.5219 armillgn@memphis.edu
                     
                     Aretha is directly responsible for the daily operations of the Residence Life program
                        and supervises Area Coordinators, Assistant Area Coordinators, and Graduate Assistants.
                        She plans and implements professional and paraprofessional staff training sessions
                        for customer service, staff selection, payroll, emergency procedures, counseling,
                        and maintenance procedures. Aretha attends contractual discipline hearings, and coordinates
                        departmental programming systems. She acts in absentia of the Associate Dean of Students
                        for Residence Life and Dining Services to maintain department operations. Aretha earned
                        her Bachelor of Science in Biology from Maryville University in St. Louis, Missouri.
                        She also earned a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from the Indiana University of Pennsylvania
                        in Indiana, Pennsylvania and has a Master of Arts in Student of Affairs in Higher
                        Education. Aretha is the advisor for the Residence Hall Association and impacts students
                        by engaging, challenging, and supporting them. She was the 2008 Pyramid Award winner
                        and enjoys reading and listening to music.
                     
                     
                     Amanda Virag Area Coordinator Phone: 901.678.6250 avirag@memphis.edu
                     
                     Amanda is originally from West Alexandria, Ohio and graduated from the University
                        of Dayton with a Bachelor of Science in Adolescent to Young Adult Education with a
                        concentration in English. She received her Master of Science degree in College Student
                        Personnel from the same institution. Amanda is the Area Coordinator for Rawls Hall,
                        Smith Hall, Mynders Hall, South Hall, and the Living Learning Complex. Her primary
                        responsibility is to support and manage the Assistant Area Coordinators as they ensure
                        residents have a safe, comfortable living and study space. The most fulfilling part
                        of Amanda's job is seeing students engage their community and become leaders. 
                     
                     
                     Michael Courson Area Coordinator Phone: 901.678.5929 mjcurson@memphis.edu
                     
                     Michael earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Interdisciplinary Studies from Arkansas
                        State University and a Master of Arts in Higher Education from Louisiana State University.
                        Michael supervises the professional staff for Richardson Tower, Carpenter Complex
                        and the Graduate Student and Family Housing. He is also responsible for designing
                        the training program for all the student staff. His primary goal is to prepare staff
                        members to meet residents' needs and concerns. Michael was the 2012 Outstanding Graduate
                        Student at the Louisiana State University. He was also the 2013 Graduate Student of
                        the Year for the Louisiana Association of Housing Officers."
                     
                     
                     Telecia Taylor Area Coordinator of Student Services and Residence Hall Phone: 731.425.7349 Fax: 731.425.7358 ttylor21@memphis.edu
                     
                     Telecia is recent graduate of Salisbury University in Salisbury Maryland with a Bachelor
                        of Science in Information Systems and Psychology and a Master of Post-Secondary Education.
                        She is responsible for the daily operations of Carney-Johnston Residence Hall at the
                        Lambuth Campus. Telecia manages the hall staff, advises student organizations, and
                        oversees events. She takes pride in knowing residents by name and building a rapport
                        with new residents. Telecia believes it is important to be honest and helpful to create
                        a sense of ease for those in a new environment. Telecia enjoys Zumba, traveling and
                        jamming to the smooth sounds of Bruno Mars.
                     
                     
                     Administration
                     
                     Glenda Barton Residence Life Supervisor gbarton@memphis.edu
                     
                     Glenda supervises the office coordinator, office assistants, and student workers.
                        She authors departmental forms, assigns rooms, oversees the computerized assignment/room
                        change processes, and manages student files. Glenda is a housing expert who routinely
                        counsels students on room assignments, adjusting to campus life, and meal plan options.
                        Her duties also include writing and overseeing the distribution of Residence Life
                        correspondence to applicants, residents, other departments, and the wider community.
                        Glenda is a recipient of the University of Memphis Student Services Pyramid Award
                        and the University of Memphis Outstanding Employee Award.
                     
                     
                     Jazmine Phillips Marketing & Outreach Coordinator                                                  
                                                                                             Phone: 901.678.2294 Jpphllp2@memphis.edu https://www.flickr.com/photos/uofm_2/26407232150/
                     
                     Jazmine graduated from the University of Memphis where she double majored in Marketing
                        and Management. She earned a Bachelor of Business Administration in 2012. She is responsible
                        for developing and implementing the marketing activities for Residence Life, Dining
                        Services and the Child Development Center. Jazmine also manages special events, tours,
                        campus visits and attends all on campus and regional recruitment events and receptions.
                        The most fulfilling part of Jazmine's job is seeing new students successfully transition
                        into college and become engaged on campus and in the Memphis community
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Nicholas T. Scull Financial Services Associate nscull@memphis.edu
                     
                     Nicholas graduated from the University of Memphis with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics
                        in 2009. He is responsible for student accounts and performs various financial duties
                        in Residence Life and Dining Services. Nicholas also coordinates accounts receivable
                        and accounts payable for housing and child care charges. Nicholas is a member of the
                        University of Memphis Alumni Association, enjoys anything sports related, preferably
                        Tigers, and was born on Christmas Day.
                     
                     
                     Facilities
                     
                     [Position open] Assistant Director of Residence Life Facilities Phone: 901.678.4029
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     Tony Johnson Coordinator, Residence Hall Facilities Phone: 901.678. Fax: 901.678. tjohnsn5@memphis.edu
                     
                     Tony graduated from the University of Arkansas at Monticello in Monticello, AR with
                        Bachelor of Science and has a Master of Arts from Murray State University in Kentucky.
                        Tony is responsible for the planning renovations and preventive maintenance for Residence
                        Life and Dining Services. His duties include assigning work orders, purchasing, and
                        inventory control for general maintenance and housekeeping. Tony coordinates with
                        Physical Plant for major renovation projects, trains and supervises the general maintenance
                        and student staff, and recommends training priorities. Tony also acts in absentia
                        for the Assistant Director of Residence Life Facilities.
                     
                     
                     Tech Support
                     
                     Ann Marie Hall Local Technical Support Provider III Phone: 901.678.5940 annhall@memphis.ed u
                     
                     
                     Ann Marie graduated from the University of Memphis with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in
                        Performance. She directs the operations of the technology for Residence Life and Dining
                        Services, her duties include interacting with Campus IT and outside vendors to keep
                        housing residents connected to the internet on a safe, dependable network. Since coming
                        to Residence Life she has implemented the ResNet Service Center which makes tech support
                        available to all residents. She also installed and maintained the StarRez housing
                        management system that enables online housing applications and maintenance reporting.
                        Ann Marie attends and presents at numerous housing professional and technology seminars
                        to remain knowledgeable in the fast changing field. In her spare time, Ann Marie is
                        involved in local theatre and is Theatre Works board member.
