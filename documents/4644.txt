                     Information for New Staff
                     
                     The information contained here is to help you, as a new employee, get a brief overview
                        of what to expect as a new employee at the University of Memphis.
                     
                     
                     On your first day of employment, you will attend a new employee orientation or Onboarding
                        session. Details are included in the Offer and Orientation Letter you received from
                        the Workforce Management office. During the day you will complete employment forms
                        and hear information about policies and procedures vital to your continued employment
                        at the University of Memphis.
                     
                     
                     Please be prepared to make choices regarding insurance coverage during your orientation
                        by reading the New Employee Benefits Packet before your scheduled session. Below is a list containing links to information you
                        may find helpful in preparing yourself for orientation:
