                     Student Resources
                     
                     Whether you are just starting your studies in Journalism and Strategic Media or preparing
                        to transition to your professional career, we want to help you reach your academic
                        potential and professional goals. The links below offer more information regarding
                        specific needs for current undergraduate and graduate students.
                     
                     
                     • Undergraduate Student Handbook   Your go-to guide for undergraduate studies in Journalism and Strategic Media
                     
                     
                     • Undergraduate Catalog   Degree requirements for all undergraduate programs in Journalism and Strategic Media
                     
                     
                     • Graduate Student Handbook Your go-to guide for graduate studies in Journalism and Strategic Media
                     
                     
                     • Graduate Catalog   Degree requirements for all graduate programs in Journalism and Strategic Media
                     
                     
                     • Course syllabi   Syllabi for all courses taught in Journalism and Strategic Media
                     
                     
                     • Undergraduate course descriptions   Find a class that interests you
                     
                     
                     • Graduate course descriptions   Plan your program of study
                     
                     
                     • Honors Societies Students with high GPAs can earn entry into one of our two honor societies
                     
                     
                     • Student Media Get involved with one of our student media outlets
                     
                     
                     • Scholarships The Department of Journalism and Strategic Media annually awards more than $30,000
                        in scholarships
                     
                     
                     • Study Abroad Do you want to travel the world and learn about other cultures? We offer several opportunities
                        for our students
                     
                     
                     • Internships Do you want to gain real world work experience? Learn about available internship opportunities
                        and how to get credit in the Department of Journalism and Strategic Media
                     
                     
                     • Student Media Practicum Earn credit for work in student media
                     
                     
                     • Career Services Do you need help planning your career? Contact our Career Services center for help
                     
                     
                     • Student Organizations Do you want to get involved on campus? Join one of our many student associations
                     
                     
                     • Student Services   Do you require extra assistance? Contact Student Services for help with tutoring,
                        advising, disability services, and more
