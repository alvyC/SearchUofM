                     Ensemble Video
                     
                     Ensemble is one of the University's video content management systems available to
                        faculty and staff as a tool to help engage the learning community. This platform enables
                        users to organize and manage their own video library, upload video files, and easily
                        share them through multiple sources. The system allows users to easily publish videos
                        to eCourseware, HTML pages, blogs, and other web technologies.
                     
                     
                     Enhance your course content with engaging videos to promote student success in your
                        classroom with Ensemble. To begin using this service, login with your UUID and Password
                        by visiting the University of Memphis - Ensemble Video portal. In addition, the following videos and documentation will help you get started
                        using the service.
                     
                     
                     Getting Started
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           University of Memphis - Ensemble Video Portal
