                     Guidelines for Admission
                     
                     Students currently admitted to a graduate program at the University of Memphis or
                        another university and individuals already holding a graduate degree (MA, MFA, MS,
                        PhD) may apply for admission to the Interdisciplinary Graduate Certificate Program
                        in Museum Studies.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Students already enrolled in a graduate program are required to have a minimum GPA
                           of 3.0 for admission to the Museum Studies certificate program.
                        
                        
                        Students may not be concurrently enrolled in more than one certificate program at a time.
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Students attending an institution of higher education other than the University of
                        Memphis or who already hold a graduate degree must apply to the Graduate School for
                        admission to the university and, in addition, must submit an application directly
                        to the Program in Museum Studies (see address below). The student's Museum Studies
                        application will include copies of undergraduate and graduate transcripts (copies
                        of official transcripts submitted to the University of Memphis Graduate School are
                        acceptable). In rare instances, students who have completed an undergraduate degree program, but
                        who have not been admitted to a graduate program, may apply and will be considered
                        for admission to the program on an individual basis. For more information on this
                        option, contact Dr. Luebbers. All applications to the museum studies certificate program will be reviewed by the
                        Admissions and Advisory Committee. The Committee may also interview prospective students in order to ensure a good fit
                        between the program and the individual student's needs. To apply, students must -
                     
                     
                     1.  be admitted to the Graduate School of The University of Memphis        http://www.memphis.edu/gradschool/
                     
                     or another institution of higher education. If the student is     in a graduate program outside of the University of     Memphis, or is already in possession of a graduate degree, the student     must apply to the Graduate School and must submit the following to the     Program in Museum Studies at the address given below -
                     
                     
                     2.  send the completed Application Form  to the Interdisciplinary      Graduate Certificate Program in Museum Studies
                     
                     
                     3.  submit copies of undergraduate and graduate transcripts (copies of official transcripts      submitted to the University of Memphis Graduate School are acceptable)
                     
                     
                     4.  submit a letter from the applicant describing the student's reasons for      wishing to acquire a graduate certificate in the area of museum studies      and how the program corresponds with prior experience and anticipated      career plans; and
                     
                     
                     5.  submit three sealed letters of recommendation along with the approved        Recommendation Request Form from each recommender.
                     
                     
                     DEADLINES:
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           The deadline for applications to the Museum Studies Certificate Program for Fall semester 2017  is  April 3, 2017 .
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Deadlines for applications for graduate study in specific programs at the University of Memphis for FALL 2017  are set by the individual departments. Please check the appropriate links from The Graduate School - Academic Degree Programs web page. Note: Deadlines for admission to the Graduate School are usually later than Departmental program deadlines. If you submit your application
                              using the Graduate School's deadlines, you will have missed most Departmental cut
                              off dates and your application will be delayed at least one semester.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Deadlines for Financial Aid applications at The University of Memphis for FALL 2017  vary. Some deadlines may have passed. Please check the appropriate links from The Graduate School - Fellowships, Awards, and Federal Financial Aid web page.
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     MAIL THE MUSEUM STUDIES APPLICATION PACKET TO: Program in Museum Studies Art Museum The University of Memphis 142 Communication & Fine Arts Bldg. Memphis, TN 38152-3200 Inquiries may be directed to Dr. Leslie Luebbers, Director of the Art Museum (lluebbrs@memphis.edu)
