                     Transportation Resources
                     
                     In addition to the University’s Blue Line Buses, there are other transportation resources
                        students can take advantage of if they need it.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        The Blue Line
                        
                        
                           
                           U of M Campus’s own bus shuttle that even has a weekly shuttle to local stores during
                              the school year. The Blue Line has several routes around campus and a GPS app that
                              you can download to see when the next bus will arrive at a bus stop.
                           
                           
                           http://www.memphis.edu/parking/blueline/
                           
                        
                        
                        U of M Carpool Portal
                        
                        
                           
                           Take advantage of carpools and other transportation alternatives found on student’s
                              My Memphis Portal home page
                           
                           
                           https://sso.memphis.edu/idp/profile/cas/login?execution=e3s1
                           
                        
                        
                        MATA Bus Transportation
                        
                        
                           
                           Public transportation provider for the Memphis area with many different route options
                              and drop off at the U of M main campus. Find your best schedule on their website
                           
                           
                           MATA Administrative Offices 1370 Levee Road Memphis, TN 38108
                           
                           (901) 722-7100
                           
                           http://www.matatransit.com/
                           
                           Central Route drops off at the University of Memphis http://www.matatransit.com/uploadedFiles/Main_Site/Content/Maps_and_Schedules/Route_Maps_and_Schedules/Fixed_Bus_Routes/Route_4(1)/Rt%205%20map%20only%205_16.pdf
                           
                        
                        
                        UBER
                        
                        
                           
                           Car service with reasonably priced fares that has an app that can be downloaded on
                              your smart phone
                           
                           
                           https://www.uber.com/cities/memphis/
                           
                        
                        
                        LYFT
                        
                        
                           
                           Car service with reasonably priced fares that has an app that can be downloaded on
                              your smart phone
                           
                           
                           https://www.lyft.com/cities/memphis
