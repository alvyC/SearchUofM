                     Class Descriptions
                     
                     Agility Training
                     
                     This high-intensity, total-body fitness class combines fast-paced intervals to build
                        strength and speed.
                     
                     
                     Blue Theory
                     
                     Blue Theory is a HIIT workout that rotates between a comfortable pace, a push pace,
                        and an all-out pace. The workout changes every day so that it won't become repetitive.
                        This class incorporates a full-body workout with body weight exercises and cardio
                        for an invigorating fitness experience!
                     
                     
                     Body Sculpt
                     
                     This class utilizes barbells, dumbbells, medicine balls, resistance bands, and other
                        equipment to strengthen muscles and improve flexibility.
                     
                     
                     Boot Camp
                     
                     Boot Camp is a high-intensity workout that consists of a strength and cardiovascular
                        workout.
                     
                     
                     Cycle
                     
                     Cycle is a high-energy stationary bike workout that enhances cardiovascular endurance
                        and muscular strength.
                     
                     
                     Cycle HIIT
                     
                     Traditional cycle class gets a twist with interval-style strength training and sculpting
                        moves for a full-body workout. This new approach to your normal cycling routine incorporates
                        HIIT intervals both on and off the bike. Short spurts of sprints and hill climbs on
                        the bike are mixed with high intensity intervals of body weight exercises off the
                        bike, providing the recipe for burning fat while sculpting your muscles in a quick,
                        efficient fashion.
                     
                     
                     HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training)
                     
                     This class features short, intense intervals of cardio exercises to elevate the heart
                        rate and provide a full-body workout.
                     
                     
                     Killer Kore
                     
                     By incorporating a variety of exercises, including partner exercises, group activities,
                        and stability balls, this class takes a fun, fresh approach to strengthening and toning
                        your core. Don't let the name scare you--this class is suitable for all ability levels!
                     
                     
                     Morning Pump
                     
                     No need for a mid-week slump when you can get your Wednesday morning started right
                        with a gentle flow of toning exercises using the stability ball. This class focuses
                        on elongating and strengthening a different muscle group each class, with the help
                        of the stability ball to tone your muscles in a low-impact, yet effective way. This
                        class is a perfect addition to your regular fitness routine, because it will tighten
                        you up while also helping you to stretch your muscles and work on your form and breathing.
                     
                     
                     Outdoor Running Group
                     
                     This class will help participants increase their endurance through engaging in group-based
                        running activities. This class will meet rain or shine--on inclement weather days,
                        we will meet indoors. 
                     
                     
                     PiYo Strength
                     
                     This Beach Body Class features stretches and exercises that combine yoga and Power
                        Pilates movements, stretches, and strength training exercises. Participants will get
                        a good core workout while also enjoying a great stretch.
                     
                     
                     Turbo Kick
                     
                     This Beach Body cardio class uses a combination of kickboxing, boxing, dance, and
                        drill movements. A fun, innovative way to gain cardio, health, and strength!
                     
                     
                     Yoga
                     
                     Yoga integrates slow movements, stretching, and breathing, plus relaxation strategies,
                        to increase flexibility, balance, and strength and reduce stress.
                     
                     
                     Zumba
                     
                     Zumba is an exhilarating, easy-to-follow and high calorie-burning dance fitness party
                        that is Latin inspired.
