                     Emerging Innovation Series
                     
                     Every month the FedEx Institute of Technology invites a leading innovator in emerging
                        technologies to speak about what they are most excited about. Featuring researchers,
                        local leaders, nationally-recognized innovators, and business luminaries, this series
                        is designed to encourage conversation and engage the Memphis community. All talks are free and open to the public.
                     
                     Plan on Attending? Click on the title of the talk(s) you want to hear and we will be sure to keep you
                        up to date.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Emerging Innovations in Intelligent Systems
                        
                        
                        
                        Intelligence Systems are changing the way we engage with the world. Dr. Vasile Rus , William Dunavent Professor of Computer Science, talks about where the future in
                           AI is headed.
                        
                        
                        November 18th @11:30
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Promise and Potential of Mobile Sensor Data-To-Knowledge
                        
                         
                        
                        In 2015 the NIH funded a research initiative in mobile health data. This initiative,
                           based at the University of Memphis, has pioneered the implementation of sensor technology
                           in healthcare. Join Dr. Santosh Kumar for a discussion of where mobile healthcare is and where it is going.
                        
                        
                        January 19th @11:30
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Technology Innovations in Orthopedics
                        
                        
                        
                        Emerging technologies in material sciences and additive processes are changing the
                           way orthopedics are manufactured and implemented. A leading figure in medical device research (announcement pending)    joins us for a talk on the future of the industry. 
                        
                        
                        February 16th @ TBD
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Emerging Innovations in Logistics Information Technology
                           
                             
                           
                           Logistics is at the heart of Memphis industry and requires constant innovation to
                              get the world where it needs to go. Join FedEx Services SVP Russ Wheaton as we discuss the emerigng technologies that help the world relax. 
                           
                           
                           March 16th @ 5:00
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Memphis Grizzlies: Technology Innovation in Professional Sports
                           
                            
                           
                           Professional sports are a hotbed of technology innovation in sports medicine, multimedia
                              technology, and many other fields. President of Business Operations for the Memphis
                              Grizzlies, Jason Wexler , speaks about the innovations happening at the FedEx Forum.
                           
                           
                           April 20th @ 5:00
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           The Future of FinTech
                           
                            
                           
                           Fintech is changing at a rapid pace and banks are constantly finding new ways to inovate
                              their industry. Rob Hinds , SVP and Deputy CIO of First Tennessee, joins us for a conversation about the emerging
                              trends in this field
                           
                           
                           May TBD @ 5:00
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     
                        
                          Technological Advancements Enabling Larger Arrays for Submillimeter Spectroscopic
                              Astronomy
                        
                          
                        
                        he submillimeter (submm) is one of the last wildernesses in the electromagnetic spectrum. Join
                           Dr. Caleb Wheeler of Vanderbilt University as he highlights the current state of heterodyne submillimeter
                           spectroscopy from giant molecular cloud to published plot. 
                        
                        
                        June 15th @ 5:00
                        
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     
                        
                        Previous Events
                        
                        
                           
                           Blockchain and Market Competition
                           
                            
                           
                           Blockchain is changing the way we do business. Join SimplyAgree CEO Will Norton  and U of M Law Professor John Newman  for a discussion of how markets will respond to this emerging technology 
                           
                           
                           October 20th @12:00
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Smart Cities: The Future of Technology in Memphis
                           
                             
                           
                           City of Memphis CIO Brent Nair discusses the future of emerging technology integration in Memphis
                           
                           
                           September 22nd @ 11:30
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Augmented Reality and the Success of Pokemon Go
                           
                           
                           
                           The most successful augmented reality app to date is discussed by a panel of experts from the University of Memphis and Memphis Technology Foundation
                           
                           
                           August 11th @ 6:00
