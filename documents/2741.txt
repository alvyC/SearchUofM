                     Undergraduate Programs
                     
                     Undergraduate students admitted to University College may choose from 21 distinct
                        concentrations among our Bachelor of Professional Studies and Bachelor of Liberal
                        Studies degrees, or create an individualized program of study through the Baccalaureate
                        Contract Program.
                     
                     
                     Minor concentrations are also available in Alcohol and Drug Abuse Services, Child
                        Development, American Studies, and Religion in Society.
                     
                     
                     Courses can be completed on campus or online or a combination of both. Our college
                        also strives to assist non-traditional students by offering courses during the day,
                        at night and on the weekends.  No matter what family commitments, erratic work schedules,
                        or limited mobility you may encounter, we want to give you the opportunity to succeed
                        at earning a degree.
                     
                     
                     Students who wish to accelerate the completion of their degree may be eligible to
                        earn credit through Prior Learning Assessment (PLA) which includes Experiential Learning , credit earned by CLEP or DSST standardized exams, or departmental credit by examination. 
                     
                     To be admitted to the University College, please complete and submit our Declaration of Major form.
