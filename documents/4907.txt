Center for Innovative 
Technology Management





 

The Center for Innovative Technology Management (CITM) is home to research and
collaboration projects that explore new developments in technology and
related managerial concerns. The Center seeks to provide and facilitate
multidisciplinary opportunities for faculty, students, and  businesses. Our activities include research-based consulting,
academic/industrial collaboration, and seminars/training programs
developed by academic professionals knowledgeable about current
information technology issues.
