                     MBA Programs
                     
                     Interested in better career prospects or want to advance in your career? The University
                        of Memphis offers five different MBA tracks. Choose the one that is appropriate for
                        you.
                     
                     
                     Professional MBA  
                     
                     Want to get an MBA degree at your own pace? Want to specialize in a particular functional
                        area? The Professional MBA is right for you.
                     
                     
                     Online MBA  
                     
                     Have a traveling job and want an MBA degree? Like the flexibility of taking courses
                        from home? The Online MBA is right for you.
                     
                     
                     International MBA  
                     
                     Ready for a career in a Global organization? Like studying abroad and understanding
                        other cultures? The International MBA is right for you.
                     
                     
                     Executive MBA  
                     
                     Want to go from the class room to the board room? Prefer to attend classes with the
                        top executives in Memphis? The Executive MBA is right for you.
                     
                     
                     Customer Driven MBA  
                     
                     Enjoy interning while you study? Like an MBA being sponsored by a Fortune 500 Corporation?
                        The CD MBA is right for you.
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Which University of Memphis MBA?
                        
                        Regardless of where you are in your career or life, and whether you live across the
                           street or across the world we have an MBA right for you. Check which MBA is best for you .
