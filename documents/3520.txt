                     Discover the uniqueness of a University College degree.
                     
                     Welcome to University College. Our mission is to provide interdisciplinary educational
                        opportunities in a flexible, relevant, and responsive format. Since its creation in
                        1975, the college has been an entry point for students, an innovator of new programs,
                        a catalyst for interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary efforts throughout the campus,
                        and a facilitator for the community to interact with the University.
                     
                     
                     We seek to provide access to quality educational programs and services for a diverse
                        community of traditional and non-traditional adult learners who seek change through
                        increased knowledge and skill.
                     
                     
                     University College offers individualized and interdisciplinary degrees not traditionally
                        offered by other colleges on the University of Memphis campus. Courses can be completed
                        on campus, online, or a combination of both in order to meet the flexible needs of
                        your schedule. Areas of study include 21 concentrations among our Bachelor of Professional
                        Studies and Bachelor of Liberal Studies degrees. Our Master of Arts in Liberal Studies
                        degree allows students the option of developing personalized areas of emphasis, and
                        our online Master of Professional Studies program offers areas of study in strategic
                        leadership, human resources leadership, or training and development. Degrees can also
                        be customized to fit specific learning interests, and credit hours may be earned through
                        work experience and professional training.
                     
                     
                     Come learn more about University College and create a degree as unique as you.
