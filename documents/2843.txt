                     Senior Class Gift
                     
                     Hey Seniors!
                     
                     You're almost there -- a University of Memphis graduate and a lifelong Tiger! You
                        have much to be proud of, and your UofM degree will open new doors of opportunity
                        as you build your future.
                     
                     
                     As a member of the 2016 Senior Class, your support for the Senior Class Gift campaign
                        will show your Tiger Spirit true and blue! Unite with your classmates to support scholarships
                        and advancements for freshmen following in your footsteps.
                     
                     
                     Click here to be a part of something great!
                     
                     
                     Consider making a gift in the amount of your class year, $20.16.
                     
                     100% participation is our goal.
                     
                     All donors will be recognized in the official Commencement Program as a TRUE BLUE STUDENT DONOR .
                     
                     
                     This year you have a tremendous opportunity to beat previous senior class gift campaigns. 
                        A donor has stepped forward to match every gift made by this year's senior class! 
                        That means your gift of $20 will actually be $40!  Together with your classmates,
                        you can make the 2016 Senior Class Gift one of the best in UofM history.
                     
                     
                     Give your gift today .
                     
                     
                     Click here to view Frequently Asked Questions .
