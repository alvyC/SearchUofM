                     Interpreter Course Requirements
                     
                     Applicants to the Level I Interpreter Course must meet the requirements for language
                        proficiency, education, and age as described below.  Completion of the Level I course,
                        taken at the University of Memphis or an equivalent course at another institution,
                        is a pre-requisite for the Level II course.  All persons who are interested in taking either
                        course should contact Espi Ralston, Course Instructor, prior to registration and payment
                        to ensure that they meet all the requirements.
                     
                     
                     Espi Ralston, MA, MAT, MA, CMI-Spanish, CHI 901.218.4691 espiralston@gmail.com
                     
                     LAnguage Proficiency Requirements
                     
                     Oral proficiency in English and at least one other language is required.
                     
                     Applicants must provide documentation of proficiency in English for one of the following:
                     
                     
                        
                        Five years or more of professional experience in the US, or in another country where
                           English is the official language
                        
                        
                        A high school diploma (or equivalent),or an undergraduate or graduate degree from
                           an accredited institution of higher education in the US, or in another country where
                           English is the official language
                        
                        
                        Acceptable score for one of the following language exams:
                        
                        
                           
                           TOEFL: 570+ paper, 230+ computer version, 90+ on iBT
                           
                           ELPT: 950+
                           
                           MELAB: 80+
                           
                           ECPE: Pass
                           
                           FCE Level 3: A
                           
                           CAE Level 4: B
                           
                           CPE Level 5
                           
                           IELTS: 7.0+
                           
                           OPI* in English: Advanced Mid-Level
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Applicants must also provide documentation of proficiency in a second language:
                     
                     
                        
                        OPI* in a foreign language: a score of Advanced-Mid Level or higher is required
                        
                     
                     
                     *OPI  The Oral Proficiency Interview is offered at the University of Memphis Testing
                        Center and, if needed, should be completed at least two (2) weeks prior to the start
                        of the HCI Course.  The cost of the OPI is $90.
                     
                     
                     Education Requirements
                     
                     Applicants must have at least a high school diploma or the equivalent.  A bachelor's
                        degree or higher is recommended.
                     
                     
                     Age requirements
                     
                     Applicants must be at least 18 years of age, but it is recommended that they be 21
                        years or older.
