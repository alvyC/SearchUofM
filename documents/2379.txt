                     Alumni & Friends
                     
                     You might not have known it then, but the day you were accepted to the Herff College
                        of Engineering you began a lifelong relationship with the college. Herff was committed
                        to your success as a student and we are equally committed to your success today. To
                        do this, we encourage you to stay connected with Herff : participate in alumni events, connect with faculty members, volunteer on campus,
                        mentor a student, make a financial commitment, or work with our career services office
                        to give back as a guest during a career development workshop.
                     
                     
                     Visit our faculty and staff page to find the person most suited to help you.  
                     
                     
                     Please stay in touch, we look forward to seeing you soon!
                     
                     Nominate an Outstanding Alumnus
                     
                     The true measure of an engineering college's success can be found in the achievements
                        of its alumni. The Herff College of Engineering is known to develop leaders in engineering
                        and other related professions. Since 1989, we have been recognizing those who have
                        unique distinction through success in their professions, service to society and commitment
                        to the Herff College.
                     
                     
                     On April 9 we will welcome the 2016 Outstanding Alumnus into this prestigious group during our
                        Celebration Event and Awards Dinner.
                     
                     
                     Nominate an outstanding alumnus here!
