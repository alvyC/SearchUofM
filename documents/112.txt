	   Together, We Make Memphis Stronger
Your connection to this campus does not have to end when you
graduate. When you join the UofM Alumni Association, you’re not just
giving back to a school – you’re giving back to a community and a
vibrant, growing network. Your gift is more than a membership. It helps
provide student scholarships, alumni programs and events, while also
increasing national rankings of the University. 

Membership Levels
GRAY                              
Complimentary for Annual University Donors* & New Graduates

Whether you give a small monthly gift or have endowed a scholarship, we know that donors are the lifeblood of our University. We want to keep you connected by providing you with a complimentary Alumni Association membership.**
Upgrade to Blue>>
BLUE                               
$50 Annually

Show your support for your alma mater and reap the rewards! Blue members receive additional benefits and help support new programming for alumni and friends. First time annual members receive a complimentary membership package.

JOIN NOW>>

LIFE                               
$750 (or $100 monthly installments for 8 months)
Life members play a critical role in shaping the future of our University. Life
membership dues support the UofM Alumni Scholarship Fund, ensuring that promising students have the resources they need to complete their education. Life Members receive additional membership benefits and a welcome package.

JOIN NOW>>


*All donors that have made a donation of any amount to the University in the past fiscal year is considered a Gray member.
* *Gray members receive limited member benefits. Donors are automatically enrolled as a gray member (no enrollment form required). Upgrade to Blue to receive additional membership benefits!
