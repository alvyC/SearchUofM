                     Accuracy is important! Proof your name.
                     
                     Graduation is a HUGE moment. We want to make your day as special as possible. Spelling your name accurately
                        in the Graduation Program is important to us and we know it is important to you and
                        your family and friends too. To ensure everything is correct, you are required to
                        proofread your name in the Program before it goes to print.
                     
                     
                     The DEADLINE to make changes to the December 2016 Commencement Program is November 9, 2016 @ 12:00 pm CST . 
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     How to Proof your name:
                     
                     Each semester, we will update the graduation program 3-4 times. It is the your responsibility
                        to ensure your name and information is accurate by The Attendance/Non-Attendance Form
                        deadline. To update your name, correct your name, and more, see further information:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Find your name by using Ctrl F 
                        
                        Confirm the spelling, degree, honors designations, prior degree information
                        
                        Reviewing your name is required by all names
                        
                        The program proof and diploma name must match and is how your name will be announced
                           at Commencement
                        
                        
                        If you need to change your name in the University system, please visit the Name Change
                           link to the left. 
                        
                        
                     
                     
                      
                     
                     You'll need the Adobe Reader to view the program proof below. If you don't have Adobe
                           Reader installed on your computer, please click the "Get Adobe Reader" icon below
                           to download the latest FREE version. Each College is linked to it's section of the
                           program proof. These are first-run Galley proofs, so please ignore the layout and
                           check your name, degree and college.
                     
                     Confidentiality Disclaimer 
                     
                     If you would like to have your name removed for confidentiality or any other reasons,
                        please contact our office by the Attendance Form deadline. Your name will be included
                        in the Commencement Program unless you contact us to remove it, whether or not you
                        have prior confidential restrictions on your student account.
