                     Global Cultures, Foreign Languages and International Perspectives
                     
                     The University of Memphis is proud to serve as the host of the Governor's School for
                        International Studies which takes place on the U of M Campus each year during the
                        month of June. The month-long program typically begins on the last Saturday of May
                        and ends on the last Saturday of June.
                     
                     
                     The University of Memphis is located in the heart of Tennessee's growing international
                        corporate center on the banks of the Mississippi River - home to Federal Express,
                        Holiday Inns and Schering-Plough.
