                     Brian Cayolle
                     
                     Local Saxophonist Plays Live Jazz In Memphis
                     
                     For Release: November 8, 2016 For more information, contact: Lafayette's (901) 207-5097
                     
                     
                     Musician Brian "Breeze" Cayolle is set to perform in the Memphis area this month.
                        His performance will be held atÂ Lafayette's Music Room, located on 2119 Madison Avenue
                        in Midtown Memphis. The events take place on every Wednesday night in November (2nd,
                        9th, 16th, 23rd, and 30th ) starting at 5:30 p.m. and ends by 8 p.m.
                     
                     
                     Drawing inspiration and influences from jazz, R&B and Rock traditions of New Orleans,
                        Memphis is his new home after the devastation from Hurricane Katrina.
                     
                     
                     Cayolle's music has guided around the globe, prompting applause from many "Jazz Lovers"
                        worldwide. He has performed at the Montreux Jazz Festival & New Orleans Jazz & Heritage
                        Festival. In addition to his concerts, Cayolle has released three albums and worked
                        alongside Allen Toussaint, Elvis Costello and Stevie Ray Vaughn.
