                     Research Teams
                     
                     In order to enhance student's educational experience CEPR faculty encourage students
                        in the department to participate collaboratively in their ongoing research programs.
                        Students and faculty work as a team to conceptualize, develop, implement, present
                        and write-up for publication research on their chosen topics. Students interested
                        in the teams should contact the team faculty member.
