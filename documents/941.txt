                     Academic Unit Reports
                     
                     These reports contain information about various academic units, comprising university-level,
                        college-level, department-level, and course-level reports.
                     
                     
                      College/Department/Major Reporting
                     
                     
                        
                         Program Review (Excel)
                           
                           View University,College or Department data for a term. Includes 1) Program Summary:
                              enrollment by gender and ethnicity, average class size, mean GPA, mean test scores.
                              2) Enrollment Profiles: By age, registration type, attendance schedule, and geographic
                              origin. 3) One Term Retention. 4) Applicants. 5) Concentrations: 4-year trends of
                              concentration enrollments by Lambuth status. 6) Time to Degree: Average time to degree
                              since enrollment in major.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Enrollment Dashboard (Visual Analytics - Login) 
                           
                           Interactive dashboard allowing you to view historic enrollment and credit hour trends
                              and details by semester. Also includes one year retention rates and enrollment projections.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Upcoming Term Enrollment - Details by Program (Visual Analytics - Login)
                           
                           Enrollments at the program level for a specific day in an upcoming term.
                           
                        
                        
                          Graduation & Retention Rates Dashboard (Visual Analytics - login)
                           
                           4-6 year graduation rates and one-year retention rates for first time freshmen cohorts
                              and first time transfer cohorts. Filter results by student characteristics as of their
                              first semester: gender, ethnicity, nontraditional age, full/part status, Pell status,
                              STEM status, and college of major.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                          Trend Report on Graduates, Degrees, Majors and Concentrations (PDF)
                           
                           5 years trends of degrees awarded at the University (by demographic), Degree, College,
                              Major, and Concentration levels
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     College/Department/Course Reporting
                     
                     
                        
                          Grade Distributions (Excel - login) 
                           
                           Summarizes the faculty, course section, enrollment, enrollment capacity, withdrawal
                              rate, success rate, average course GPA, effective teacher rating (SETE), and grade
                              distribution. Based on term, level, college, dept, and course.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                          Course Success tabs and Trends tab, Teaching Activity Dashboard (Visual Analytics
                              - login) 
                           
                           Success rates and number of courses offered, filterable by faculty type, rank, college,
                              course type,and more.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Program Review (Excel)
                           
                           View University,College or Department data for a term. Includes 1) Course Demand:
                              14th day number of sections, credit hours, number enrolled, capacity. 2)Course Service:
                              courses taught to majors vs non majors 3) Credit Hour Production: total credit hours
                              taught by department 4) Instructional Load: hours taught by faculty.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Daily Enrollment Tracker (Upcoming Term) Details by Course (Excel- Login) 
                           
                           Enrollment at the college, dept, subject and course level in an upcoming term for
                              today's date compared to the same day last year.
