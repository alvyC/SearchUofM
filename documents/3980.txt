                     Look Up Classes: Popular Lists
                     
                     Below are instructions for retrieving some of the most commonly requested class listings.
                     
                     Off-Campus Courses
                     
                     To Select All Off-Campus Offerings :
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        In the Subject field, select all Subjects: click on the first Subject, hold the [Shift]
                           key down, and scroll to and click on the last Subject.
                        
                        In the Campus field, select all options except "All" and "Univ of Memphis, Main."
                           Here's how: Click the first location after "All", hold the [Shift] key down, and scroll
                           to and click the last location. Then scroll up to "Univ of Memphis, Main"; hold the
                           [Ctrl] key down and click on this location. All Campus options should be highlighted
                           except for "All" and "Univ of Memphis, Main."
                        
                        Click [Class Search].
                        
                     
                     
                     To Select All Offerings at a Particular Satellite Campus :
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        In the Subject field, select all Subjects (see above).
                        
                        In the Campus field, select the Campus you are interested in. NOTE : You can select more than one Campus by clicking the first location you want and then
                           holding down the [Ctrl] key when clicking other individual locations.
                        
                        Click [Class Search].
                        
                     
                     
                     Offerings for a Particular Part of Term
                     
                     To Select All Offerings for a Particular Part of Term:
                     
                     
                        
                        In the Subject field, select all Subjects: click on the first Subject, hold the [Shift]
                           key down, and scroll to and click on the last Subject.
                        
                        In the Part of Term field, select the desired term.
                        
                        Click [Class Search].
                        
                     
                     
                     Web Courses Only
                     
                     Regents Online Degree Program (RODP) Courses Only
                     
                     
                        
                        In the Subject field, select all Subjects: click on the first Subject, hold the [Shift]
                           key down, and scroll to and click on the last Subject.
                        
                        In the Part of Term field, select "Regents."
                        
                        Click [Class Search].
                        
                     
                     
                     All Non-RODP Online Courses Only
                     
                     
                        
                        In the Subject field, select all Subjects: click on the first Subject, hold the [Shift]
                           key down, and scroll to and click on the last Subject.
                        
                        In the Instructional Method field, select "WEB-Asynchronous" option.
                        
                        Click [Class Search].
                        
                     
                     
                     All Online Courses
                     
                     
                        
                        In the Subject field, select all Subjects: click on the first Subject, hold the [Shift]
                           key down, and scroll to and click on the last Subject.
                        
                        In the Instructional Method field, select any of the three "RODP-WEB Asynchronous"
                           options you are interested in plus the "WEB-Asynchronous" option. (Click the first option desired, hold the [Ctrl] key
                           down, and select all the desired options.) NOTE : With regard to the RODP courses, DSP=Developmental Studies, UG=Undergraduate, and
                           GR=Graduate. If you are an undergraduate, select the "UG" and/or "DSP" RODP options
                           plus "WEB-Asynchronous"; if you are a graduate student, select only the "GR" option
                           plus "WEB-Asynchronous."
                        
                        Click [Class Search].
