                        Bridget VanLandeghem 
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     
                     Bridget VanLandeghem
                     
                      
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        B.S. in Psychology, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
                        
                        M.S. in Kinesiology – Applied Sport Science, Indiana University-Bloomington
                        
                     
                     
                     Bridget VanLandeghem joined the University of Memphis staff in August of 2002 to serve
                        as Athletic Academic Counselor for various teams. She is the counselor for men's baseball,
                        men's golf, women's golf, women's tennis, women's volleyball, and co-ed rifle. As
                        of October 2014, Bridget serves as the Assistant Director of CAAS and oversees the
                        student workers, facility supplies, campus reservations, as well as training staff
                        on academic advising and campus resources. Prior to joining the University of Memphis
                        staff, Bridget was employed at St. Bonaventure University from 1999 through 2002 where
                        she served as an Athletic Academic Coordinator and began her career in athletic academic
                        services.
                     
                     
                     In addition to serving as Assistant Director, Bridget is a member of the National
                        Association of Academic Advisors for Athletics (N4A), coordinator and instructor for
                        ACAD 1100: Academic Strategies, Orientation chair, and Campus Communication Liaison.
                     
                     
                     Originally from Paris, Illinois, Bridget is a die-hard Chicago Cubs fan, and an avid
                        follower of the 2010, 2013 and 2015 Stanley Cup Champion Chicago Blackhawks. She also
                        enjoys devouring Memphis BBQ, visiting Graceland, and singing, especially "Take Me
                        Out To The Ballgame" at FedExPark.
                     
                     
                     Bridget's favorite quote is "A life is not important except in the impact it has on
                        other lives." Jackie Robinson
