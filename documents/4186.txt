                     Academic Highlights
                      
                     
                     
                        
                        
                           
                           Baseball (third year), Men's Basketball (fifth year), Women's Golf (fourth year), and Women's Tennis (third year) received NCAA Public Recognition Awards from the 2014 Division I Academic Performance Program (APP) for their APR's.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Women's Tennis was recognized as ITA All-Academic Team , with a team GPA of 3.792 for the academic year.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Volleyball won the AVCA Team Academic Award for its 3.458 team GPA.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           The UM women's teams have now posted a 3.0 combined GPA in 24 of the last 25 semesters.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Women's Cross Country,Women's Indoor Track, Women's Outdoor Track, Women's Soccer,
                              Women's Tennis & Men's Golf were honored with the 2013-14 American Athletic Conference Team Academic Excellence Award for having the highest college GPAs in each conference sport.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Memphis had more teams win this award than any other institution in the AAC.
                           
                        
                        
                        
                           
                           Memphis has graduated 159 student-athletes since August 2013.
