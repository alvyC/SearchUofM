                               Learn More About Complete Professional Program!
                        
                     
                     
                     The Professional Development Center (PDC) was created to provide business majors a
                        distinctive edge through a variety of opportunities designed to complement their academic
                        coursework. Our unique holistic approach prepares students to meet the demands of
                        today's competitive business world while providing our business partners a first-hand
                        look at the top talent coming out of Fogelman College. Thanks to the support of entrepreneur
                        and business leader Avron B. Fogelman, the PDC offers an impressive list of programs , as well as career coaching services , including resume workshops, mock interviews, and internship placement assistance.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                       Click here to download the PDC Calendar of Events   Click here to access our CPP Online Registration System
                     
                     INTERNSHIPS
                     
                     Our mid-south community partners (corporations, small businesses, non-profit organizations,
                        government agencies) have been highly supportive of hiring Fogelman business students
                        as interns. The goal is to prepare and place students, while keeping our top talent
                        right here in Memphis!
                     
                     
                     If you'd like to explore how to develop an internship program with the Fogelman College
                        of Business and Economics, email Carolyn Cates, Assistant Director , for more information. Click here to post an internship or early entry job opportunity on our Fogelman Internship Network.
                     
                     
                     COMPLETE PROFESSIONAL PROGRAM
                     
                     Through a variety of seminars and training modules that focus on different areas of
                        professional growth, the Complete Professional Program strives to produce graduates
                        who are educated, ethical, polished, healthy, and community-oriented. Click here to learn more!
                     
                     
                        
                        "Our cutting edge approach teaches students to productively chart a successful career
                              path in our community. These programs, we believe, are the future cornerstone for
                              student and employer engagement." - Dr. Kathy Tuberville, Director
