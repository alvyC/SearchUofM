                           About Dr. Yongmei Wang
                           
                           Education

                           Ph.D. University of Notre Dame
                           
                           Publications

                           PI Millionaire, University of Memphis, 2012
                           
                           Research and Scholarly Activities
                           
                           Computational studies at the interface of macromolecular systems and nanomedicine,
                              in particularly interested in the study of separation and characterization of macromolecules
                              and nanoparticles; rational design of polymeric systems for gene and drug delivery;
                              and plasmonic optical properties of metallic nanoparticles; and the use of nanoparticles
                              in nanomedicines.
