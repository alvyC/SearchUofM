                        About Dr. Jeremy J. Wessel
                        
                        Education
                        
                        A.B. Ripon College
                        
                        Ph.D. Yale University
                        
                         
                        
                        Professional Experience
                        
                        Fall 2012 - present Instructor of Chemistry, University of Memphis Spring 2012 Adjunct Instructor of Chemistry, University of New Orleans, New Orleans,
                           LA Fall 2011 Adjunct Instructor of Chemistry, River Parishes Community College, Sorrento,
                           LA 2003 - 2010 Assistant Professor of Chemistry, Nicholls State University, Thibodaux,
                           LA 2001 - 2003 Visiting Assistant Professor of Chemistry, Tulane University, New Orleans,
                           LA 2000 - 2001 Visiting Assistant Professor of Chemistry, Xavier University of Louisiana,
                           New Orleans, LA 1996-1998 Assistant Professor of Chemistry, Jamestown College, Jamestown, ND
