                     Student Reports
                     
                      
                     
                      Enrollment and Credit Hours
                     
                     General Enrollment
                     
                     
                        
                         Enrollment Report Generator (Interactive Tool)
                           
                           Create customizable reports based on any student characteristics across time
                           
                        
                        
                         Program Review (Excel)
                           
                           View University,College or Department enrollment data for a term. Includes 1) Program
                              Summary: enrollment by gender and ethnicity, average class size, mean GPA, mean test
                              scores. 2) Enrollment Profiles: By age, registration type, attendance schedule, and
                              geographic origin. 3) Concentrations: 4-year trends of concentration enrollments by
                              Lambuth status.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Enrollment Monitor (PDF)
                           
                           Basic University-level statistics as of the 14th day census. Reports by student class,
                              first time freshmen, registration type and class, ethnicity, and gender.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Undergraduate Profiles (PDF)
                           
                           20 University-level tables: age by gender and race, enrollment status by avg attempted
                              credit hours, majors by class, residency & campus residence, enrollment in online
                              courses, enrollment in off-campus/evening/weekend classes, enrollment as an honors
                              student or athletics, enrollment as first generation status, preadmissions criteria,
                              aspirations, college choice, desire for educational help, living arrangements, work
                              expectations, adjusted income & financial aid.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Daily Enrollment
                     
                     
                        
                        Daily Enrollment Tracker (Web - Login) 
                           
                           Compare enrollment for an upcoming term with the same day last year.
                           
                        
                        
                        Details by Course (Excel- Login) 
                           
                           Enrollment at the college, dept, subject and course level for a specific day in a
                              term compared to the same day last year.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Details by Program (Visual Analytics - Login)
                           
                           Enrollments at the program level for a specific day in an upcoming term.
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Enrollment Projections
                     
                     
                        
                        Markov Chain (Excel) 
                           
                           University-level projections with the ability to modify future retention and new student
                              parameters in order to evaluate the impact on future enrollment growth and retention
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Enrollment Dashboard - Enrollment Projections tab (Visual Analytics- Login) 
                           
                           Markov enrollment projections that can be drilled down to University, College, Department,
                              and Lambuth levels with the ability to modify future retention and new student growth
                              estimates
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     Retention and Graduation
                     
                     Retention
                     
                     
                        
                         Retention & Graduation Trends (PDF)
                           
                           University-level graduation rate trends for specific populations of interest
                           
                        
                        
                          STEM Retention & Graduation Trends (PDF)
                           
                           Trend report for graduation rates for first-time full-time STEM students
                           
                        
                        
                          Graduation & Retention Rates Dashboard (Visual Analytics - login)
                           
                           4-6 year graduation rates and one-year retention rates for first time freshmen cohorts
                              and first time transfer cohorts. Filter results by student characteristics as of their
                              first semester: gender, ethnicity, nontraditional age, full/part status, Pell status,
                              STEM status, and college of major.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Graduation
                     
                     
                        
                          Trend Report on Graduates, Degrees, Majors and Concentrations (PDF)
                           
                           5 years trends of degrees awarded at the University (by demographic), Degree, College,
                              Major, and Concentration levels
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Graduation Report Generator (Interactive Tool)
                           
                           Create tables of graduation data on their own. You choose the variables for the rows
                              and columns of the report. Reports can be filtered by a number of different variables.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     Student Success
                     
                     
                        
                          Course Success tabs, Teaching Activity Dashboard (Visual Analytics - login) 
                           
                           Success rates and number of courses offered, filterable by faculty type, rank, college,
                              course type,and more.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                          Grade Distributions (Excel - login) 
                           
                           Summarizes the faculty, course section, enrollment, enrollment capacity, withdrawal
                              rate, success rate, average course GPA, effective teacher rating (SETE), and grade
                              distribution. Based on term, level, college, dept, and course.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                          Post-Graduation Student Followup 
                           
                           Reports on the earnings, debt, time-to-degree, and continuing education of studentes
                              who have graduated.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     Applications & Admissions
                     
                     
                        
                          Admissions Activity Dashboard (Visual Analytics - login) 
                           
                           Compare application activity for the upcoming Fall term as of today with the same
                              day last year. See total students who applied, admitted, and enrolled. Drill down
                              to the Program Level.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                          Program Review, Applicants tab (Excel - login) 
                           
                           Mean High School GPA, ACT scores, and GRE scores. Available for University, College,
                              and Department levels.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     First Time Freshmen
                     
                     
                        
                          Freshmen Profiles (PDF) 
                           
                           21 First Time Freshmen tables: Age by gender and race, majors by gender, residency
                              & campus residence, enrollment in online courses, enrollment in off-campus/evening/weekend
                              classes, enrollment as an honors student or athletics, enrollment as first generation
                              status, preadmissions criteria, aspirations, college choice, desire for educational
                              help, living arrangements, work expectations, adjusted income & financial aid.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     Online and Off-Campus
                     
                     
                        
                          Online Course Profiles (PDF) 
                           
                           Online Credit Hour Enrollment by student age, gender, full-time status, ethnicity,
                              geographic origin, residency, and college.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                          Lambuth Profile (PDF) 
                           
                           Online Credit Hour Enrollment by student age, gender, full-time status, ethnicity,
                              geographic origin, residency, and college.
                           
                           
                        
                        
                         Enrollment Report Generator (Interactive Tool)
                           
                           Use Attendance-Lambuth Campus, Attendance-All Online, Attendance-Off-Campus, Attendance-UM
                                 Online , etc. as a Row Variable, or as a Separate Tables By variable. Create customizable
                              reports based on any student characteristics across time
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Daily Enrollment Tracker-Details by Course (Excel- Login) 
                           
                           Filter the pivot table by camp_code to see off-campus courses, and by instructional_method to see online/RODP courses.
