	   Visit The Campus
Relive your college days and see all of the exciting changes that are happening at
the UofM by visiting campus! Stop by to cheer on your Tigers at FedExPark , catch a show in the "Big Blue" theatre , grab a bite to eat in the Tiger Den , and stock up on your Tiger gear . Join hundreds of Tigers for Homecoming each fall.

Schedule a student-led tour ( learn more here ) or download a campus map . Stop by Parking Services first to get a visitor parking pass. Not in the area? Take a virtual campus tour !


University Overview
Learn everything you need to know about the University in the UofM Fact Book including our leadership, what we offer, what we have accomplished and where we are
going.


Athletics
It's a great time to be a Tiger! The Tigers have a promising season ahead as a part
of the American Athletic Conference . View the Athletics Calendar, purchase your tickets, and keep up with all the stats at gotigersgo.com . Do you have a future Tiger? Sign them up for Pouncer's Pals ! Find out about an upcoming Alumni Association True Blue Tailgate or pre-game event
in your area by visiting the Go to the Game page.


Admissions
Be a True Blue Tiger by recruiting prospective students! Share your favorite UofM
memories and encourage a student to sign up to learn more and help the Office of Admissions recruit the best of the best.


Career Services
The UofM doesn't stop preparing you for your future just because you've graduated.
As alumni, you may continue to enjoy Career Services resources including career advising, resume and cover letter critiques, mock interviews
and job searching through Tiger Link. Log to Tiger Link or schedule an appointment by calling 901.678.2239.


Student Life
The UofM campus is bustling with activities for students year round. There is an organization or activity for every student to take part in and develop leadership skills. The Future Alumni of Memphis (FAM) is teaching students how to be good alumni. And of course students are logging plenty
of hours in the library . It's a great time to be a Tiger!


Campus Development
The University of Memphis is ever-expanding and transforming with the additions of
the Community Health Building ; University Center ; FedEx Institute of Technology ; Lambuth campus ; Cecil C. Humphreys School of Law ; Kemmons Wilson School and Holiday Inn ; Anfernee "Penny" Hardaway Hall of Fame; and Living Learning Complex . 

View renderings of the proposed master plan . Learn more about how you can help reach this vision.

News
Re-connect with YOUR University by subscribing to the University of Memphis Magazine , read college publications , and reading the latest news releases . Follow the UofM on your favorite social media site to watch videos and get important updates.


Alumni News
Stay connected! Update your calendar with upcoming Alumni Association events . Read our monthly e-newsletter, Memphis Connect , for news, alumni spotlights, and upcoming events. Like us on Facebook , follow up on Twitter , and watch videos on YouTube .
