                     Lauren Hillman
                     
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        B.S. in Sports Administration, The University of Southern Mississippi
                        
                        M.S. in Sports Administration, The University of Southern Mississippi
                        
                     
                     
                     Lauren Hillman joined the University of Memphis staff in July of 2013 as an Athletic
                        Academic Counselor for Football. Prior to Memphis, Lauren was an Academic Counselor
                        and the Life Skills Coordinator at The University of Southern Mississippi. During
                        her tenure, Lauren advised several sports, including men's and women's basketball,
                        soccer, softball, men's and women's golf and volleyball. From 2008 until 2012, as
                        part of Student-Athlete Development, Lauren oversaw events such as the First Annual
                        SAAC Date Auction and Mardi Gras Ball, mentoring and literacy awareness at local elementary
                        schools, annual department-wide canned food drive, Operation Christmas Child shoe
                        box project, and hosting special needs students for athletic facility tours and activities.
                     
                     
                     In addition to serving as an Academic Counselor, Lauren is also an ACAD 1100 instructor,
                        assists with the Mentoring Program, PAWS Committee, CAAS Website Management and Professor
                        Grade Cards.
                     
                     
                     Lauren is originally from Brandon, MS and enjoys traveling (to New York City especially),
                        shopping, running, watching all sporting events, and spending time with her nieces,
                        Micah and Maggie, and nephew, Matthew.
                     
                     
                     Favorite Quote: "You can't live a perfect day without doing something for someone
                        who will never be able to repay you." ~John Wooden
