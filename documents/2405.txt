                     How To Apply
                     
                     Admission Process to the Executive MBA Program is highly selective. Participants must
                        make a serious commitment to the program and have the support of their company and
                        family as they embark on the rigorous program requirements.
                     
                     
                     Candidates for the University of Memphis Executive MBA Program must complete the following in
                        order to be considered for admission:
                     
                     
                     Required :
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        Bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university
                        
                        Official transcripts from all academic institutions must be submitted
                        
                        A minimum of five years experience in a managerial or professional position
                        
                        Letter of support from your company. Entrepreneurs and independent professionals should
                           submit letters explaining their individual situation. Click here to download a sample support letter.
                        
                        
                        Two letters of Recommendation
                        
                        * Satisfactory competitive score on the Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) or Graduate Record Examination (GRE) To find out more, visit the Information Sessions page today! 
                        
                        
                        Complete the University of Memphis Application for Graduate Admission and submit the $35 application fee.
                        
                        
                        Complete the Executive MBA Application for Admission. Click here to download the application .  Essays are listed on page 3 of the application.
                        
                        
                        Possess strong oral and written communication skills
                        
                        Current resume
                        
                     
                     
                     All qualified applicants must participate in a personal interview, which will be scheduled
                        after all other application requirements are completed.
                     
                     
                     We are in the process of seating the next incoming class - Executive MBA Class of
                        2018. The regular deadline for admission to the Executive MBA Program is July 15,
                        2017, priority deadline is December 30, 2016. Applicants are highly encouraged to
                        apply early to secure a place in the next incoming class. Classes will begin August 2017
                        during the annual Executive MBA Residential Week.
                     
                     
                     * GMAT Requirement may be waived on a case-by-case basis for individuals who possess
                        all of the following:
                     
                     
                     
                        
                        10+ years of documented professional experience
                        
                        Executive level positions - Sr. Director or above
                        
                        Undergraduate or Graduate GPA of 3.25 or a graduate degree from an accredited institution
