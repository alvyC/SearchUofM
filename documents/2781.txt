                     There's no Shortage of STEM Opportunities at the UofM!
                     
                     Activities and Opportunities for uofM Students
                     
                     On this page (see tab to the left) students are provided information on clubs, scholarships,
                        grant opportunities, workshops and webinars to assist in their STEM course of studies.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Activities and Opportunities for UoFM Faculty
                     
                     This page (see tab to the left) provides information for university faculty including
                        grant opportunities, workshops and webinars to assist in higher education.
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     Activities and Opportunities for K-12 Students
                     
                     This page (see tab to the left) lists a variety of camps, competitions and Campus
                        Day events, as well as contacts for students at the K-12 level. Click on the blue
                        name link above for more information. Most of these activity links are maintained
                        and updated by the professors in charge of the program. CRISTAL posts their links
                        but we are not responsible for their content. Summer opportunities usually updated
                        in March and April of each year. Please check this page often!
                     
                     
                     
                     
                     K-12 Teacher Resources
                     
                     The K-12 Teacher Resources page (see tab to the left) offers a variety of resources
                        for K-12 teachers as well as professional development opportunities.
