                     Services
                     
                     CAESER helps in "building smarter, stronger communities." Â Some of the services we
                        offer that accomplish this goal:
                     
                     
                     Water
                     
                     
                        
                        Water mass balance (atmospheric, surface and subsurface)
                        
                        Environmental assessments
                           
                           
                              
                              Conduct stream condition surveys to identify impacts to ecosystem; includes mapping
                                 illegal dumping/discharge, destructive farming practices, and remediation recommendations
                              
                              
                              Identify potential contamination sources to drinking water supplies
                              
                              Testing of soils for contamination such as from mining
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Groundwater assessment and modeling
                           
                           
                              
                              Conduct water level survey to map gradients
                              
                              Collect water samples for geochemical analysis and age-dating
                              
                              Perform numerical modeling to simulate complex flow conditions and predict long-term
                                 stressor impacts
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Determine contamination potential and conduct contaminant plume modeling
                           
                           
                              
                              Identify contamination sources and concentrations
                              
                              Perform numerical modeling to delineate contaminant plumes and their migration
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Groundwater/surface water interaction
                        
                        Subsurface mapping of geologic features and strata
                           
                           
                              
                              Use geophysical techniques (P-wave and S-wave) to map substrata
                              
                              Use drilling techniques to collect soil samples and determine geologic properties
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Flood modeling
                           
                           
                              
                              Flood routing and determining inundation extents
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Public awareness
                           
                           
                              
                              Teach about the importance of water conservation and the source of drinking waterÂ 
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Disaster Resiliency
                     
                     
                        
                        Develop spatial data for disaster planning and recovery
                           
                           
                              
                              Collect and map critical infrastructure information
                              
                              Provide a decision support visualization tool for non-technical users
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Flood inundation modeling
                           
                           
                              
                              Use simple to complex techniques to predict flood inundation from different storm
                                 frequencies
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Damage assessment
                           
                           
                              
                              Determine impact to structures and people
                              
                              Develop evacuation lists
                              
                              Determine post-disaster access to damaged properties
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Public education
                           
                           
                              
                              Provide maps and information to public on how to respond to impending disaster
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                     
                     
                     Mapping/GIS/Data Processing
                     
                     
                        
                        Legacy data migration
                           
                           
                              
                              Moving data in older formats like paper into GIS or another digital format, often
                                 with a spatial connection
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Custom application development
                           
                           
                              
                              Develop a complete mapping application, typically for remote field data collection
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Utility and municipal infrastructure
                           
                           
                              
                              Map and attribute stormwater and sanitary sewer system
                              
                              Map and attribute utility electric and natural gas lines
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Neighborhood/Community planning and analysis
                           
                           
                              
                              Determine factors that contribute to the decline in the quality of life of a community
                                 or neighborhood
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Develop decision support analytics
                           
                           
                              
                              Develop intuitive decision support visualization tool to assist elected officials,
                                 city planners and citizens to strategically enact positive change in their surroundings
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Remote sensing
                        
                        Quality of life, health and safety
                           
                           
                              
                              Determine walking routes for children to and from school that avoid harmful conditions
                                 such as railroads, large transportation corridors, industries, high crime areas, etc.
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Asset management
                           
                           
                              
                              Map municipal assets (infrastructure, vehicles, and facilities) to manage condition,
                                 number, changes, etc.
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Web development: interactive mapping and decision support
                        
                        Geodatabase design
                        
                        Spatial data repository and dissemination
                           
                           
                              
                              Collect and store spatial data within a RMDB (relational database management system)
                              
                              Develop geo-portal (spatial library of data) for easy dissemination to public or others
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Mobile data collection
                           
                           
                              
                              Conduct mapping in the field using mobile data collection application
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Mobile application development
                           
                           
                              
                              Develop custom built mobile data collection tool
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Data visualization/manipulation
