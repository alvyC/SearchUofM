                     Parking
                     
                     Faculty parking is available in the FedExForum garage. For more information about
                        specific parking options, see below: 
                     
                     
                     location and parking
                     
                     FedExForum Parking Options for University of Memphis Commencements:
                     
                     Location: FedExForum 191 Beale Street Memphis, TN 38103
                     
                     
                     Directions : Printable directions Route Map
                     
                     Parking : Parking is available in the FedEx Forum garage for a charge of $5.00 per vehicle for
                        the event. Please allow ample time to find the arena and to enter the parking garage.
                     
                     
                     For more information on different parking options, please click HERE .
                     
                     
                     Faculty Marshals
                     
                     Marshals for commencement are to be appointed by the college dean
                     
                     
                        
                        Parking and Cap and Gown
                           
                           
                              
                              Parking passes will be provided to Faculty Marshals to us on Commencement day. Cap
                                 and Gown orders must be rented. Commencement does not pay for regalia rentals for
                                 faculty marshals. 
                              
                              
                           
                           
                        
                        
                        Arrival Time and Duties
                           
                           
                              
                              Information regarding time of arrival and duties for Faculty Marshals will be emailed
                                 by the Coordinator of Commencement 2 months before Commencement day. For more information,
                                 call 901.678.5547.
