                     In order to vote, you must:
                     
                     
                        
                        Be a U.S. citizen
                        
                        Be at least 18 years old on or before Election Day
                        
                        
                        Have not been convicted of a felony or, if convicted, had voting rights restored by
                           court order or pardon
                        
                        
                        Be a registered voter
                        
                     
                     
                     VOTER REGISTRATION INFORMATION
                     
                     TENNESSEE RESIDENTS: Application deadline to register to vote in TENNESSEE for any
                        election is thirty (30) days prior to the election date. Tennessee will accept voter
                        registration applications through February 1, 2016. Shelby County residents may scan
                        the completed and SIGNED application and email it to VoteInfo@ShelbyCountyTN.gov .
                     
                     To register to vote in Tennessee you must complete a Voter Registration Application and mail or deliver
                        it to your County Election Commission Office.
                     
                     
                     Voter Registration Application is available online at http://www.shelbyvote.com/DocumentCenter/View/72 . They are also available for pick-up on campus at the University Center Information
                        Desk.
                     
                     
                     Shelby County residents mail or deliver your Voter Registration Form to:
                     
                     Shelby County Election Commission Voter Mail Registration 980 Nixon Drive Memphis, TN 38134
                     
                     To email your Voter Registration Form, please print, sign, scan, and email the form
                        to VoteInfo@ShelbyCountyTN.gov .
                     
                     For residents of other Tennessee counties click http://tnsos.org/elections/election_commissions.php  to find your County Election Commission address.
                     
                     
                     To check your registration status in Shelby County you may go to Voter Registration
                        Lookup -  http://www.shelbyvote.com/index.aspx?NID=87 .
                     
                     NOTE:  If you registered to vote by email or mail you must vote in person in the first election
                        after your registration becomes effective. This includes Early Voting. You CANNOT
                        vote by absentee ballot.
                     
                     
                     Voters who are already registered can make address changes at an early voting site.
                        For further information, call 901.222.1200.
                     
                     
                     For Absentee Voting information click  http://www.shelbyvote.com/index.aspx?NID=80 .
                     
                     For questions or additional information please go to the Shelby County Election Commission
                        website, http://www.shelbyvote.com or call them at 901.222.1200.
                     
                     
                     NON-TENNESSEE Residents : For information on registering to vote in other states please CLICK HERE to find your state's information for voter registration.
                     
                     
                     EARLY VOTING LOCATIONS* for Shelby County and Tennessee residents
                     
                     Early voting for State and Federal General Election – October 19 through November
                           3, 2016 at ALL Satellite Sites
                     
                     Locations are subject to change. To check early voting dates, times and locations
                        click http://www.shelbyvote.com/DocumentCenter/View/1005 .
                     
                     *Tennessee residents who are registered to vote in any Tennessee county other than
                        Shelby County should contact their resident county election commission office for
                        early voter locations. To locate your county election commission office click  http://tnsos.org/elections/election_commissions.php .
                     
                     NOTE: There are NO voting locations on The University of Memphis campus .
