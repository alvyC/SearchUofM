Join Pouncer's Pals! 

Pouncer's Pals is an exclusive club designed for little Tiger fans. Each member must
be 12 years old or younger. The membership fee for the 2016-2017 season is $30.

$30 Annual Membership Includes:

    Pouncer's Pals Club T-shirt*
    Official Membership Card
    Quarterly Membership Packets with update and activities
    Exclusive invitations to special events and activities
    One walk around the field at a home football game

JOIN ONLINE!**
*T-shirt size and design based on availability. T-shirts can be picked up at the Alumni Center (635 Normal Street, Monday-Friday, 8am-4:30pm) or at your first event.

**You can register up to 3 children on the form. If you need to add additional children,
please call us at 901.678.2586.

** Download an application and return by mail or fax.

Pouncer's Incredible Pizza Birthday Bash
Pouncer's Pals had a blast at Incredible Pizza in celebration of Pouncer's birthday!

Pouncer's Pals Superhero Slam
Pals joined Pouncer, Batman and his sidekicks to cheer on our Women's Tiger basketball team against Tulsa in January. View Photos
Pouncer's Pals Walk Around the Field
Pouncer was so excited to have his Pals join him on the field at the Homecoming Game for the annual "Walk Around the Field." View Photos

Blue Lantern Tour
Pouncer and his Pals wore their best Halloween costumes and trick-or-treated on the UofM campus while visiting "ghosts" of days past. Our Student Ambassador Board provided informative tours to teach us about our campus history. View Photos
