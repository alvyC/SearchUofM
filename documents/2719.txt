                     News and Events
                     
                     New course to be offered starting in Spring 2017: AUSP 4300 - Autism: Communication and Socialization . More information will be posted as soon as it becomes available. (posted October
                        18, 2016) Read Syllabus
                     
                     Dr. Gavin Bidelman was a recipient of the College of Arts and Sciences Early Career
                        Research Award. (posted August 19, 2016) Read More
                     
                     Dr. Kim Oller and Dr. Eugene Buder have been awarded a 5-year, $3.2 million grant
                        from the National Institutes of Health for a study on early vocal development and
                        predictions of autism. (posted November 19, 2015) Read More
                     
                     Dr. Shaum Bhagat received a grant from the Turtle Beach Corporation for a study of
                        HyperSound® Hearing Solutions technology. (posted October 21, 2015) Read More
                     
                     Darlene Winters was featured on LocalMemphis' "Local Hero" segment on July 30! Darlene
                        currently runs the Language Learning Lab, a program for young adults with Down Syndrome,
                        at the Memphis Speech and Hearing Center on Wednesday mornings. (posted August 16,
                        2015) Read More Watch the interview: Local Memphis (starts at 7:40)
                     
                     
                     Ph.D. student Chris Constantino will be hosting for StutterTalk from the International
                        Fluency Association's World Congress in Portugal, July 6-8 this summer. Here is a
                        link to a podcast Chris did recently that talks about disability rights and stuttering
                        treatment and his research on passing as fluent. (Posted June 5, 2015) Listen to Podcast
                     
                     Drs. Linda Jarmulowicz and Lisa Lucks Mendel appeared on TV - Local Memphis (ABC 24)
                        on Monday, May 25, 2015  and WREG News Channel 3 on Tuesday, May 26, 2015 during their
                        respective "Live at 9" morning shows. They talked about "Better Hearing and Speech
                        Month" (May) and educated viewers about early detection and intervention of speech/hearing
                        issues. Watch the interviews: Local Memphis Live | WREG Live at 9
                     
                     New study led by Dr. Gavin Bidelman shows that musical training late prevents loss
                        of listening skills later in life. Read more: Washington Post and National Post
                     
                     CSD researchers led by Dr. Michael Cannito will study Parkinson's Disease with Michael
                        J. Fox Foundation grant. Read More
                     
                     U.S. News & World Report has again nationally ranked our School as having top graduate
                        programs in both Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology. Read More
                     
                     Drs. Gavin Bidelman and Shaum Bhagat receive grant funding from the GRAMMY Foundation®
                        to study the effects of musical training on hearing health. Read More
