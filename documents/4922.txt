                        Profile
                        
                        Dr. Mark A. Sunderman joined the faculty at the University of Memphis in 2008. He
                           is a tenured professor and holder of the Morris Fogelman Real Estate Chair of Excellence
                           in the Fogelman College of Business and Economics. He joined the University of Memphis
                           after spending 22 years at the University of Wyoming. His B.S., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees,
                           all in finance, are from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. His research
                           is primarily in the field of real estate, with a focus on property tax administration,
                           valuation, real estate finance, and the valuation of government grazing leases. Professor
                           Sunderman has published scholarly papers in journals such as the Journal of Real Estate Research , Journal of the American Real Estate and Urban Economics Association , Land Economics , Journal of Real Estate Finance and Economics , Appraisal Journal , and Journal of Risk and Insurance . He also has over 150 presentations at professional meetings, as well as numerous
                           presentations at in house and regional workshops. Dr. Sunderman has been a guest speaker
                           at Indiana State University, DePaul University and West Virginia University.
                        
                        
                        Dr. Sunderman is renowned for his development and teaching of online instructional
                           programs in real estate, including a Professional Real Estate Certificate program
                           he has developed at the University of Memphis and an online BBA in Finance with a
                           concentration in Real Estate degree. He has also received several awards for online
                           teaching, including the eOT (Excellence in Online Teaching) award from eCollege, the
                           Hollon Family Award for Teaching Excellence in Off-Campus Programs awarded by the University of Wyoming and the Master Educator for Distance Education at the University of Wyoming awarded by WyDEC (Wyoming Distance
                           Education Consortium).
                        
                        
                        In addition to his teaching and research, he has consulted for the National Park Service
                           and the Ad Valorem Tax Division of the Wyoming Department of Revenue and Taxation,
                           and he has been an expert witness for the Resolution Trust Corporation. Dr. Sunderman
                           is a member of the American Real Estate and Urban Economics Association , Financial Management Association , American Real Estate Society , and the Southern and Southwest Finance Association . He is on the Board of Directors and the Webmaster for the American Real Estate Society . Dr. Sunderman also has professional membership in Lambda Alpha International , Urban Land Institute , International Association of Assessing Officers , Tennessee Chapter of IAAO , and CCIM Institute .
