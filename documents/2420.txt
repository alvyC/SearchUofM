                     Scholarships
                     
                     Thanks to an extremely generous gift from a donor, the Department of Foreign Languages
                        and Literatures is able to offer substantial scholarships through the Mary Pettus
                        Thomas Memorial Scholarship Fund to students pursuing a University of Memphis study
                        abroad program. The exact amount awarded to each student will depend on the individual
                        student's financial need.
                     
                     
                     In order to be eligible for the scholarship, students must have a declared major in
                        Foreign Languages, have achieved Junior standing (60 hours), have at least a 3.00
                        GPA, demonstrate financial need, and have a letter of support from a faculty member
                        in the department.
                     
                     
                     The funding is contingent on enrollment and participation in a study abroad program.
                        Please note that students applying for this scholarship should also apply for the
                        Study Abroad scholarship awarded by the Study Abroad office, which is different from
                        this scholarship.
                     
                     
                     The department is not  currently accepting applications.  In early Fall 2016, we will be soliciting applications
                           from students intending to pursue a study abroad program through the university in
                           Spring 2017.
                     
                     MARY PETTUS APPLICATION FORM
                     
                     For more information, contact:
                     
                     Dr. Will Thompson Chair, Department of Foreign Languages and Literatures The University of Memphis wjthmpsn@memphis.edu
