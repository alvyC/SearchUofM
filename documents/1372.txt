                     Student Ticket Policy for Men's Basketball/Football Games
                     
                     Below is important information about student tickets for home men's basketball games
                        at FedEx Forum and home football games at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium.
                     
                     
                     Full-Time Students
                     
                     Full-time students (six hours or more for this purpose) with a valid University of
                        Memphis ID will be admitted free of charge at each game. You must have your ID with
                        you, or you will not be admitted.
                     
                     
                     Part-Time Students
                     
                     Part-time students (five hours or less for this purpose) may purchase a $7 ticket
                        for themselves at the Athletic Ticket Office located in the Penny Hardaway Hall of
                        Fame at the Athletic Office Building (570 Normal Street) during regular office hours,
                        Monday-Friday 8:00-4:30, while supplies last. Part-time student tickets will not be
                        sold at FedExForum or at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium.
                     
                     
                     Student Guest Passes
                     
                     
                     Students may purchase one guest ticket for men's basketball and up to two for football
                        for each game (limit one vs. Ole Miss) at the Athletic Ticket Office for $10 ($25
                        for the Ole Miss football game), while supplies last. Only a limited number of guest
                        tickets will be available for each game and will be on sale the week of the game.
                     
                     
                     Student Entrance
                     
                     Students and persons holding student guest tickets must enter through the student
                        gate.
                     
                     
                     FedExForum – Student entrance with ID is located on the left side of the main entrance into
                        FedExForum off of Third Street.
                     
                     
                     Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium – Student entrance with ID is located at Gate 6 (Guests of Students will need a ticket
                        to enter and can use East side of Gate 6). Gate 6 is located near South Hollywood
                        Street entrance.
                     
                     
                     Please note : There will be an assigned seat system for all games at FedExForum. After IDs are
                        scanned (and guest tickets taken) at FedExForum, each student (and guest) will be
                        given an assigned seat for the game, which will then be scanned gate entrance personnel.
                        Students (and guests) wishing to sit together must enter together. As a general rule,
                        seats will be assigned on a first come - first served basis, starting with the lower
                        level of the student section and, after those seats are filled, moving to the upper
                        level of the student section. The Student Section at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium
                        will be ticketed as a General Admission (first-come, first-serve) seating area, the
                        designated student section is Section 123 and 124.
                     
                     
                     Parking
                     
                     FedExForum - The Student Government Association has leased a parking lot adjacent to the FedExForum
                        for the exclusive use of UofM students. The lot is free and will be available on a
                        first-come, first-served basis for all home basketball games. Students must present
                        a valid University of Memphis ID to be admitted to the lot. The lot opens three hours
                        prior to each game and, for the safety of the vehicles that park there, the lot will
                        be attended throughout each game. For directions to the lot, click here . 
                     
                     
                     Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium – A student tailgating lot has been established adjacent to the Liberty Bowl. A limited
                        number of free parking passes for the student tailgating lot will be distributed from
                        the UC Information Desk. Passes will be given out on a first come- first served basis.
                        You will need your Student ID in order to pick up a parking pass. There is a limit
                        of one pass per student. The student football tailgate section is in the Tan Lot near
                        the Southern Avenue entrance and next to the Coliseum.
                     
                     
                     Take the Blue Line to Football Games
                     
                     There is no reason for students to hassle with parking and traffic on game day when
                        the Tigers play football at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium.
                     
                     
                     The Blue Line Shuttle runs from the Central Avenue parking lot on the University of
                        Memphis campus two hours prior to game time and runs continuously until kickoff. Shuttles
                        return to the Central Lot at halftime and immediately following the game. Shuttles
                        returning riders to the Central Lot are available up until an hour after the conclusion
                        of each home game.
                     
                     
                     Shuttles are free and open to anyone with a Memphis ID or someone traveling with a
                        person with a valid UofM ID.
                     
                     
                     Until a half hour prior to kickoff, drop off is at Tiger Lane, then moves to Gate
                        6 (student gate off Hollywood). When headed back to campus, students can catch The
                        Blue Line on Hollywood outside of Gate 6.
                     
                     
                     When are Fall and Spring Only Semester IDs Valid?
                     
                     Fall students' IDs will be valid for entry to games through January 15. For students
                        enrolled for the spring semester, IDs will be valid beginning January 9.
                     
                     
                     Damaged Card
                     
                     If your ID card is damaged, you may receive a free replacement ID card at the Campus
                        Card Office on the 5th floor of Wilder Tower upon presentation of your damaged card.
                     
                     
                     What If Someone Uses Your ID?
                     
                     Please note that if your ID card is used by someone else, your ID will be confiscated
                        and turned over to the Office of Student Conduct.
                     
                     
                     What If the Student Section Fills Up?
                     
                     There are a limited number of seats in the student section at FedExForum and Liberty
                        Bowl Memorial Stadium. If all seats in the student section are filled, no more students
                        (or guests) can be admitted, although every effort will be made to find additional
                        seating.
                     
                     
                     Disability Assistance
                     
                     Students who are registered with Disability Resources for Students, who may need assistance
                        with special seating, should contact the Memphis Ticket Office at tigertickets@memphis.edu at least 24 hours prior to each game if they wish to attend.
                     
                     
                     Admission to Additional Athletic Events
                     
                     Students are also admitted free to Women's Basketball and Baseball home games. Home
                        events for Men's Soccer, Women's Soccer, Softball, Volleyball, Men's Tennis, Women's
                        Tennis and Track and Field are free to the public.
