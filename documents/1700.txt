                        MFA Application Process
                     
                  
                  
                     
                     
                     
                     		
                     
                     To the prospective student from Sarah Brown, Director of Graduate Studies: Thank you
                     for your interest in our graduate programs in the Department of Theatre & Dance!
                     
                     At this time, we are accepting applications for the graduate program beginning study in the Fall
                           Semester, 2017. Applying to our program is a two-part process.
                     
                     
                     Applicants must apply to the University's Graduate School. Apply Here
                     
                     Applicants must also apply directly to the Department of Theatre & Dance. Apply Here
                     
                     Applicants are encouraged to contact Kristin Shupe, Administrative Secretary. (901-678-2523)
                        Kristin will coordinate contact with an appropriate faculty member once we receive
                        your completed departmental application.
                     
                     
                     Those invited to campus will have a one-hour interview with appropriate faculty in
                        your area of interest, a tour of our facilities, and tickets to an evening performance
                        of our current production.
                     
                     
                     If you are not able to come to our campus, other opportunities for interviews with
                        members of our faculty are available at the following conferences: SETC in Lexington,
                        KY. Wednesday, March 1 – Saturday March 4, 2017 and USITT in St. Louis, Missouri,
                        Thursday, March 9 – Saturday, March 11, 2017.
                     
                     
                     If you are unable to travel, a formal telephone or skype interview will be scheduled
                        for you with the appropriate faculty members.
                     
                     
                     Typically, all decisions regarding graduate admissions and assignment of graduate
                        assistantships for the upcoming academic year are made by mid-March. We encourage
                        you to file your applications early with both Graduate Admissions and the Department
                        of Theatre & Dance.
