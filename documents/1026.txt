                     Career Services
                     
                     Educating, engaging and empowering the UofM community, Career Services works with
                        students during all phases of the career development process and offers numerous resources
                        during your career search. For the most up-to-date information and to make an appointment
                        to be first in line, go to TigerLink today! For more information call or email  Michelle Reddick at 731.425.7398 or visit Career Services at the UM Lambuth campus in the Wilder College
                        Union, 4H. 
                     
                     Prepped for Success! - A Professionalism Certificate Program for Lambuth campus students.
                     
                     Counseling Services  
                     
                     
                     Disability Resources
                     
                     At UofM Lambuth, please contact Tammy McCoy , Varnell-Jones Hall, Room 126 A, at 731.425.1906. 
                     
                     
                     Student Health Services  
                     
                     
                     Testing Center
                     
                     The Testing Center at UofM Lambuth is proud to announce our reopening.  Click  to see a list of our offered services. 
                     
                     
                     TigerLink
                     
                     Career Services' new software provides students, alumni, and employers with enhanced
                        tools and services, as well as information about upcoming Career Services events and
                        workshops. 
                     
                     
                     Tutoring @ ESP
                     
                     FREE academic assistance for students who are currently taking classes at UofM Lambuth.
                        The Lambuth Learning Center is located in Wilder College Union, Room 4A. Email Michelle Reddick or call 731.425.7398 for more information.
