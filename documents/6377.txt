                     UofM, UT, or other TBR schools Employee Spouse/Dependent Fee Discount Rules & Procedures
                     
                     Dependents and spouses of Tennessee Board of Regents/University of Tennessee employees
                        may be eligible for a discount of undergraduate tuition, program service fees and
                        RODP tuition at The University of Memphis. Students eligible for the discount must
                        submit a new certified discount form each semester to the *Bursar's Office prior to the appropriate fee payment deadline to avoid deletion of classes . The employment certification on the form should not be any earlier that July 1 for
                        Fall, December 1 for Spring and April 15 for Summer. Discount forms may not be processed
                        retroactively. For detail eligibility guidelines, click on Fee Discount Rules below.
                     
                     
                     University of Memphis employees should refer to the UofM Human Resources department for specific information. A new process is effective beginning Summer 2016.
                     
                     
                     * Non U of M Discount forms may be submitted in one of the following ways:
                     
                     Fax to: 901.678.0288 Scan and email to: bursar_ar@memphis.edu
                     
                     
                     Mail to: The University of Memphis-Bursar's Office 115 Wilder Tower Memphis, TN 38152-3520
                     
                     
                     * NOTE: Forms must be received by the Bursar's Office by the appropriate fee payment deadline.
