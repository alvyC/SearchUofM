                     Cayuse IRB
                     
                     Cayuse IRB ( memphis.cayuse424.com ) provides visibility into the entire Institutional Review Board review and submission
                        process while it simplifies the protocol creation process. Using your University of
                        Memphis log in credentials, you will have 24/7 access to submission creation, IRB
                        protocols, paperless electronic approvals – no waiting to receive approval docs -
                        and management of IRB protocols from initial submission to close.
                     
                     
                     Timeline
                     
                     Cayuse IRB will be ready for use on August 1, 2016.   Investigators are requested
                        to create new/initial protocols using Cayuse IRB starting August 1st.
                     
                     
                     Thursday, August 25th – Wednesday, August 31st the IRB will not be accepting protocols for review so the Cayuse IRB software implementation can be
                        finalized.
                     
                     
                     On September 1, 2016 , it will be REQUIRED for all UofM researchers to use Cayuse IRB for all protocol submissions. If you have
                        tried opening Cayuse IRB and are unable to access it or have any questions, please
                        contact Jessica McMorris at jmcmrris@memphis.edu or (901) 678-2705.
                     
                     
                     Access
                     
                     To access CayuseIRB go to memphis.cayuse424.com , enter your UofM login and password, and select Cayuse IRB (Human Studies Compliance).
                     
                     If you are a current student, faculty or staff member and have tried logging in to
                        Cayuse and do not have access, please contact cayusesupport@memphis.edu .
                     
                     General Cayuse Questions?  Contact  cayusesupport@memphis.edu or (901) 678-2705 .
