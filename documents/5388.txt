    MISSION STATEMENT
    The Commuter Student Association represents the interests and needs of the commuting students attending The University of Memphis. Through programming events and activities, representing commuters in the Student Government Association (SGA), and in their dealings with the University, it is the hope of CSA to help the commuting student become an integral part of the campus community.
