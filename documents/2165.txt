                     Department of Architecture Gallery
                     
                     The Department of Architecture Gallery is located off the lobby of Jones Hall.  Exhibits
                        change throughout the academic year and feature work by students in the department. 
                        If the Gallery is closed when you visit, please come to the Department office, 404
                        Jones Hall, for admission.
