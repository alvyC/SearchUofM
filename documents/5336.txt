                     Health Services
                     
                     Students following guidelines for Health Services are interested in the helping professions.
                        This curriculum will prepare students for a wide range of health care positions in
                        hospitals, health departments, health agencies, research facilities, schools and industry.
                        This program is also for experienced health care professionals who have completed
                        non-baccalaureate programs in health care specialties such as medical laboratory technology,
                        respiratory therapy, radiological technology, and nursing who will build upon their
                        training and experience by taking courses in such areas as business practice, interpersonal
                        skills, policy, risk management, social context of health care, and financial management.

                        the History requirement.
